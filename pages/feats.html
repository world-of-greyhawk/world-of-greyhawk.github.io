Feat Changes

All Classes

Mounted Combat

Rider gets a dodge AC bonus of +1 per 5 ranks in the ride skill while mounted. This does not stack with tumble (the tumble AC bonus is retained while mounted).

Casters

Brew Potion

Scripted so that the level of the potion will better approximate the level of the maker.

Craft Wand

Scripted so that the level of the wand will better approximate the level of the maker. Crafted wands are always made with 50 charges. Also a crafted wand can only be used by the class that crafted it.

Epic Spell: Dragon Knight

Dragon has been substantially beefed up.

Epic Spell: Greater Ruin

Damage is increased to 35d6 + 2d6/caster level over 20.

Epic Spell Hellball

Damage increased to 10d6 + d6/2 caster levels over 20 for each of the 4 categories of damage.

Epic Spell: Mummy Dust

Mummy has been substantially beefed up.

Expertise/Improved Expertise

Is not supposed to be available to casters while casting. Apparently a bug allows expertise mode to be used until after the first spell is cast.

Scribe Scroll

Scripted so that the level of the scroll will better approximate the level of the caster. A scribed scroll can only be used by the class that scribed it.

Summon Familiar

The death of a familiar will cost the caster 100 gold and 50 xp.

Barbarians

Terrifying Rage:

Panic effect lasts 4d4 rounds, not 1d3. Gives +d20 to intimidate, not d10. Uses turned effect so creatures run away instead of standing there. Fear immunity prevents effect, not mindspell or paralyzation immunity.

Blackguards

Epic Fiendish Servant

The Epic Vrock is more powerful than the default one.

Turn Undead

(See below)

Clerics, Blackguards, and Paladins

Turn Undead

Evil clerics rebuke undead and even dominate if sufficient level. Certain domains and feats add to this ability. Having your holy symbol out in hand will also improve this ability.

Harpers

Harper Crafting

A 5th level Harper, in addition to Bull's and Cat's Grace, can also create potions of Invisibility, Ultravision, Haste and Clairaudience/Clairvoyance.

Purple Dragon Knights

Heroic Shield

Now lasts for PDK level + charisma modifier in rounds.

Rallying Cry

Now lasts for PDK level + charisma modifier in rounds.

Shadowdancers

Epic Shadowlord

Summoned shadow has a changed appearance, the ability to cast improved invisibility three times and has attacks that can drain strength.

Hide in Plain Sight

Has a cool down period scripted so that you cannot HiPS twice within 6 seconds.

Shadow Evade

Duration changed from 5 rounds to 1 round + 1 round/level. As you get shadow evade at level 4, this will never shorten this ability for the player and promotes leveling in shadowdancer.
