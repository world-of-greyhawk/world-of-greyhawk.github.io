Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

Kama Fury version 2....in which our hero rebuilds himself in a more advantageous fashion...specifically: More Ranger, more Favored Enemies, earlier Bane of Enemies, more Epic Feats, more Monk (and more Monk speed!), less redundant Shadow Dancer, same Epic Dodge and more BA.

Special thanks to Grizzled Dwarflord for taking time to explain slowly (and with small words) where I went awry in my first attempt. Thanks too for Avendesora84 for his advice.

Note that this particular build version is geared for a slow (normal) leveling PW, and not for a "Get to level 40 in 1 hour!" PW. For a fast-leveling mod, the more 'uber-l33t' build would be Ranger 26/Monk 9/SD 5.

I looked through the list of builds to see if an Epic Dodge Monk/Ranger was out there, but didn't see one that quite fit what I was thinking of. My goal was a build that included these attributes:

  • Dexterity-based scrapper
  • Dual wielding of Kamas
  • Bane of Enemies
  • Epic Dodge
  • Flurry of Blows
