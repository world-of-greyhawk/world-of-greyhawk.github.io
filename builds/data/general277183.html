To answer your previous question, Fighter = d10, Monk = d8, and Weapon Master = d10.

Since I do not know how many levels you will be taking for them... I will provide an example of maximum HP you could get for the combo you mentioned. And the CON at 16.

18 Fighter/6 Monk/16 Weapon Master

W/O Toughness
-F = 234 HP
-M = 66 HP
-WM = 208 HP
Total = 508 HP

With Toughness feat
-F = 252 HP
-M = 72 HP
-WM = 224
Total = 548

Of course, you will have to factor in every time you add an CON modifier at level up. That changes the number you will get for that specific level.

Let me remember how that works... I believe it adds the character level to the max HP of the class hitdice...

For example... if you have increased the CON from 16 to 18, increasing the CON twice. At level 20, that is if you have adjusted the CON to 18 as you level up on 20th level, you will get 34 HP(W/O Toughness) or 35HP(W/Toughness) if you level up as fighter.

I believe the math is accurate... although it has been while since I have done the math... if anyone spots mistake... please correct me.

Oh, and here's how to do the math...

(CHD+CM)*CL + (CHD+CM)*CL + (CHD+CM)*CL, that is if you have three different classes... (CHD = Class Hitdice + Con ability modifier) * Class level.

As for other part of math... I explained the details of how to deal with CON modifiers. The math shown just now only gives you maximum HP if you don't increase the CON during the character's life.
