I'd recommend FTR 20/WM 7/Rogue 3. It nets you an extra 1d6 sneak attack, a few more skill points, and Uncanny Dodge (you'll probably only get +1 DEX bonus to your AC, but why not keep it if you're caught flat-footed?), while that 21st FTR level doesn't really do much for you. Start with Rogue at level 1 for the extra skill points (and you can also get your Initimidate requirement filled right then).

You'll lose 1 AB this way, so you'll have to decide if it's worth it.
