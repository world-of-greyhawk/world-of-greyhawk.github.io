Basically this build takes advantage of the favored power attack of the Ultimate Ranger and focused strike of the Man at Arms to do lots of damage, mainly against its favored enemies. With power attack active and dual wielding a heavy flail and a scimitar, this build has an AB of 41 and can do about an average of 256.5 damage per round, 466.5 against a favored enemy, if all hits connect and no criticals. With improved power attack active and dual wielding a heavy flail and a scimitar, this build has an AB of 36 and can do about an average of 291.5 damage per round, 571.5 against a favored enemy, if all hits connect and no criticals.

This build could be altered by reducing Man at Arms levels to 4 and increasing Ultimate Ranger levels to get more favored enemies. It could also be changed to 28 Ultimate Ranger/4 Man at Arms/8 Tempest, you'd lose weapon specialization but you'll get one more attack per round with perfect two weapon fighting.
