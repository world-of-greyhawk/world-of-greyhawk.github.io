I chose halfling, but one could easily go with elf instead. Also, the last FE slot can be substituted for Epic Prowess if one needs.
The field scout has a 13d6 sneak attack and crippling strike to deal with his opponents and can count on a high naked AC, epic dodge and SCV to avoid being hitten. The animal companion is a good sneak generator and IKD makes up for his small size.
Stakable cat's grace and invisibility given by his harper affiliation are a useful added value.
Direct damage against sneak immunes is not surprisingly very low, but UMD and set traps are resources that the field scout knows how to use to their full advantage.
STR or damage enhancing items are something the field scout should strive to get, of course.

Let me know what you think about it.
