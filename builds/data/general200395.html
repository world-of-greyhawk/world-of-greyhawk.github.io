A build that concentrates on being one class to the exclusion of all others where possible. So for example either 40 levels of a base class or 30 levels of a prestige class(the maximum you can get in each cast).

e.g.

40th level rogue would be an iconic rogue
10 rogue/ 30 Shadowdancer would be an iconic shadowdancer
Even 7 fighter /3 rogue/ 30 shadowdancer would be an iconic build IMO (some may disagree).

As long as it maximises the level of the class you are trying to "iconise".
