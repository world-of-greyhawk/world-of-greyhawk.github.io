To increase your AB I would recommend to take more Paladin, and only Paladin pre-epic, sort of Paladin 21 / Sorcerer 3 / Pale Master 16, you'll lose Epic Spell Mummy Dust (which isn't a big deal, summons are weak by that level), and 2 AC for +3 AB, besides, you should probably also leave CHA at 16 and go all STR, , that will net you +4 STR for +2 AB and -2 AC buffed, you could end with 28 STR now. I consider your AC is quite good, and you should worry more on increasing AB, so that's why I tell you to change CHA for STR.
