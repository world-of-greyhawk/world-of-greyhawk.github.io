ty Epic Warding isn't available to Druids (nor Epic Mage Armor - just arcane casters) and the one monk level was for pure powergaming purposes - not to dilute caster level too much. Druid 22 was taken for her to get +11 WIS from the spell Owl's Insight and considering that shifters are most powerful in ultra low magic worlds (unless a high magic world has a script so all items shift, shifters don't kick so much ***) I wouldn't want to drop it. This is a power-shifter build - maybe some of the other more playable shifter builds would suit you better like that Druid 17/ Shifter 17/ Monk 6 one.

Her leveling order isn't ideal; you might want to mess with it a bit to get the shapes earlier. I don't know how effective the epic spells Druids can get are - they don't seem to good IMO and with so few Druid levels, their worth is questionable though I suppose it's cool to clear a room in a shot.
