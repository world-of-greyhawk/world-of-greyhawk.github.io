I've been messing around with a few shifter ideas, mainly Risen Lord shape. Although in, for example, the Risen Lord form, all stats are overwritten, if I have a high constitution, (and hence high HP) does this mean I have more HP when in the Risen form than if I had a low constitution?

You keep the hit points that you have gotten from the dice rolls on level up, as well as from the toughness feat, but the new constitution determines how many "add on" hp you will have. In other words, your natural Con does not effect your total hp in Risen Lord form.
