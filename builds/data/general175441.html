Well, I don't know about that, but regardless of whether it's Dex or Str that determines if you hit or not, to have the Feat of Devastating Critical: <bow> you must have a strength of 25.

There are some archer builds posted that have Strength 25 (for Dev Crit) but have higher Dexterity for superior AB. A good example would be a Bard/RDD/Arcane Archer build.

Of course if you're going for Dev Crit: Longbow, hopefully you have a Mighty bow that permits your strength bonus to affect damage.
