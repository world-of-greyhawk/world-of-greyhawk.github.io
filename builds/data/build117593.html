Pros: High AC, High AB, decent damage, tons of buffs to choose from, tons of spells including bigby, premonition, isaac's greater missile storm, haste, improved invis, spell mantle, aura of vitality, divine power, and harm.
Cons: Very low Hitpoints, fragile pre epic, susceptible to spells, weak when dispelled, 3 attacks instead of 4, low skill points, saves aren't great, and long long long buffing times.
