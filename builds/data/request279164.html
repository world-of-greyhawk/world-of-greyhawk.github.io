what kinda of skills and feats do you recommend? It's difficult to say without knowing specifics. Don't even know if it's pvp or not, and you might have more luck with the following general ideas than going on the advice of some outsider with little diversity like me on the finer points of builds.

Take improved KD if there are no immunity items and you can be reasonably succesful with it.

If it's a low ac and high ab setting, then intiative feats, blooded and thug will help you hit before you get hit. If mages and other DC related nastiness could be a problem then I suggest you replace those rogue levels with bard and take spellcraft to help saves vs spells. Might still need save feats to help though.

Otherwise 3 rogue is good for a bit more damage, evasion and UMD, but if the flame arrow spells will be a problem then epic fire resistance will absorb a lot of damage, something like 90 or more fire damage from a level 36 flame arrow maximised
