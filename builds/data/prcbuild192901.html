I have been interested in the Sacred Fist class for a while, and noticed that there weren't any builds for it here, so I figured I'd give one a shot. The key to this build is making your wisdom work some major overtime. As a monk, your wisdom boosts your AC. As a cleric, your wisdom boosts your spell DCs and gives you more spell slots. From the Intuitive Attack feat, your AB is based on your wisdom rather than your strength. And the Sacred Fist ability Sacred Flame adds your wisdom modifier (as well as your Sacred Fist class levels) to your damage for 1 minute (this build can use it 6 times a day). Here is the build:

When dispelled, you will only lose about 6 AB, and since I don't believe Sacred Flame can be dispelled (although I could be wrong) and divine damage is almost never resisted, she'll still pack a wallop. She can cast powerful damaging spells on a group while rapidly pummeling the most powerful enemy to death. She can also sneak in very rapidly, through her own stealth abilities and Improved Invisibility, coupled with Monk Speed. and beat an enemy to death before being seen. And she has extremely high AC, making her very hard to hit. I've only played around with it a bit, but it's fun to watch her dismantle dragons in a single round before they even know what hit them. Anyway, enjoy .
