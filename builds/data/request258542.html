Is it possible to create a build with DevCrit + Epic Dodge + EDRIII ? Try a dwarven rogue/bard/RDD.
Starting with str16, dex16, con18, wis8, int8, cha6.
RDD will get you to str24, con20 (and int10, cha8). You will need +1 to str and con and +9 to dex.
I think there should be enough feats.
Rogue bonus feats will qualify for ED.
Be prepared to suffer an xp penalty.
