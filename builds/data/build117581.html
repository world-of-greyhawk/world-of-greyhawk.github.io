This character build which I would call 'Freak of Nature' is a Human Bard/Pale Master/Red Dragon Disciple. He will have immunities to critical hits, fire, paralysis, hole, stun, and sleep. You can choose to have a martial weapon proficiency and/or select another weapon. With about 40 ranks in tumble his final naked AC will be 36. Put on some armor, shields, and other items and he will be extremely hard to hit. His greater undead summon will be a skeletal blackguard
