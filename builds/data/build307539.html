17 Rogue, 26 Dex, and you didn't manage to slip in epic dodge? Hey, congrats on your first build. I'll give you a few suggestions that will help this guy out.

First, Epic Dodge: unless you haven't imbedded somewhere where I can't see it, you'll need to grab it. Otherwise, there's really no point in going Dex with a build like this.

Two, stat spread: 14 Wisdom? No. 10 Int? No. Maybe you have those backwards, but your skill points are far more valauble than a paltry +2 to Will saves. Instead I would go something like this:

Str: 14
Int: 13
Wis: 8
Dex: 17
Con: 16
Chr6:

Then, you'll want to take Great Dex feats at Level 21 or 24. This will allow you to pick up Epic Dodge as early as possible-- Level 27. You'll need to make sure you have Defensive Roll and Improved Evasion already selected by then, which is easily doable.

Drop Improved Crit: Rapier. You won't need it. I'd drop another one of those combat activated feats to get dualwielding in pre-Epic. There's really no reason to take those in Epic. With EWF and Epic Prowess available as bonus feats, you only really need to spend general feats on EWS and Armor Skin. This gives you the ability to take at least 4 Great Dexes, which would give you a final Dex of 31-32. This raises your AB by 3 and your AC by 3. Drop Epic Will. You have UMD.

Not crazy about dualling rapiers. I would go for a small weapon or, if you had to go with a medium weapon, one with dual-damage types (morning star). I would go with Hand axes, maces, shortswords, kukris, light war hammers, etc.

Good night, and good luck.

For a dexer, it isnt much. Remember, you are a dexer first! You will not notice the +2 damage in the grand scheme of things. Wear armor? YOU ARE A DEXER!!

I guess i have become a huge min/maxer... how did that happen!? If i were doing a dexer in a dwarf, i would try to start with 18 dex or 20 if i was elf/halfer. Sure, 30 isnt bad, but 30 isnt the best. By my math, 19+10+4gr dex is 32, and thats without really trying. With alittle more thought you could get it to 34 (of course without seein the build, who really knows).

Oh, and self concealment? dude, what is goin on! Self concealment is one of those things that is ALL or nothing. what is the point in 10% concealment when an improved invis gives 50%?

I know this is a first build. Im just trying to give you ideas on how to think like an epic builder. Something that was never told to me, and i posted quite a few builds before i figured it out. But, like i said earlier, i tend to be a min/max type of guy. After all, its a game, and why not try to dominate the best you can, right? Anything less than SCV is actually a waste and even SCV is debatable in usefulness if Imp Invis is available... 5 feats for 25% concealment against what it counts is just too much. SCI is just a joke, pure and simple ( SCI = 1% concealment against BF, which is all meleers).

Regarding starting DEX, I see nothing wrong with starting at 16 in a non elf/halfling build. There is merit in balance if one has to play the build from ground up. And dexers do wear armor, until they can do without it (which happens in epic), not to mention that tower shields have now a crazy weight and hence a decent STR score is necessary.
