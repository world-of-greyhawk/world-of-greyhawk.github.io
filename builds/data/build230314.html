-DaMouse First of all, it would me much easier to read this if you post using the free build template

It will also help you to remember to include all of the stats, like unbuffed AC, AB, saves, unbuffed HP, etc. (none of which were included).

That said, this build has a lot of room for improvement, at least from what I can read of it. With such a low charisma, everyone will be making your saves except on a natural 1 autofail, and so the spell focus feats become useless. Just stick to the typical no save spells. Also, I assume this is a straight level 40 build, but it is going to be awfully difficult to level this between level 4 and 14. You do not learn any new spells when taking levels in pale master, and I am not sure whether or not you can make these up if you return to sorceror later (although you can use the spell slots if you have the approprite metamagic feat, because you do still gain spells per day). Wizards can get around this by learning from scrolls, but sorcerors can only learn on level up, so they get the shaft.

You mentioned "that reflex save," which we don't know what it is, but it probably isn't high enough to justify evasion. I doubt you'll be making any saves from anything but yourself.

This build wouldn't be too difficult to fix, though, I don't think. As the previous poster mentioned, take EDR III, drop the spell focus feats, move PM to epic, and focus on throwing up damage shields when in combat (such a tactic benefits from a low AC). You can throw some missile storms at your opponents too, if you want to speed things up a bit.
