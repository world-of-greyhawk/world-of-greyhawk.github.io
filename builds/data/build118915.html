Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

Originally intended as a mage-slayer, this character ended up being pretty good all around. He was my chosen ahead of Anvil and the Enforcer as the character that can with a few magic items, would have a good chance of victory against any opponent. He was originally posted on March 12th,
Click Here
Give this character an immunity of critical hits, 20 points of magic damage resistance, and a selection of scrolls and he can pretty much handle anything.

Advantages

-Has high saves and evasion. Virtually 95% immune to any spell with a save.
-Has an AB 43. Pretty good for a fighter.
-Does a lot of damage per hit. He can bypass most damage reduction.
-Has Use Magic Device. He can use any scroll.
-Devastating Critical.

Disadvantages

-Low hit points.

Variant Comparison

-Variant 1 does 2 points more damage per hit.
-Variant 2 has an additional +8 bonus to initiative.
