A note on possibel changes to accomodate personal taste:

1) Spellcraft could be swapped for Taunt if desired, but you'll lose a substantial boost to saves vs spells, and this build needs help in that area.

2) If Still Spell is desired, Toughness is the most likely candidate to be dropped.

Should be a pretty decent melee build, despite the lowish HPs, as he has some good self-buffing options and can Curse quite effectively as well. Saves are a concern, but even then he has some spells and Bard Song to help out there as well.

Wow, Cin, another nice one. I really can't stand WMs, but the Bard in this takes away that loathing. Progresses quite nicely and seems thoroughly playable all the way.

My suggestions are trivial, and won't really affect the outcome of the build, just minor tweaks.

1. Start with 15 Str and 14Chr. Raise the Chr when you approach the need to cast those Extended 4th levels. This increases your early pre-pic AB and carrying capacity when you need it most.

2. Start with 14 Str and 14 Chr, and raise Dex and Int to 14. 43 more skill points, +1 reflex save. Stop with a 28 Str. Loss of 1AB and damage, but at 46AB he can afford it. This will give you one leftover bonus feat to raise one of your weak saves, or to take an ESF.
