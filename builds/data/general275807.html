I am going to try build a melee fighter that at least has an attempt to avoid getting pinned by those ugly green hands . For Bigby's Grasping Hand, you must first hit the target, with a check of 1d20 + caster level + (casting ability modifier + 10) minus 1 vs. target's armor class.

Then, to grapple the target, you must succeed with a check of 1d20 + caster level + (casting ability modifier + 10) + 4 vs. 1d20 + target's BAB + target's strength modifier + target's grapple size modifier.

Bigby's Forceful Hand is simply an STR check, with a mod of 14.
