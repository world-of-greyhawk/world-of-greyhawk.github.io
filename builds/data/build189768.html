Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

Well, I was looking at some of the massive sneak damage I have made recently and quickly realised that there was only one way to go any further with the concept. This was to increase the number of attacks/round, the 6/round dual wielder managed 180d6 sneak damage per round,wheras this monk variant reaches a not unimpressive 216d6 sneak damage/round (potentially), thats a theoretical maximum of 1296 sneak damage/round.

So we finish with 16 d6 sneak damage from the rogue levels, 1d6 blackguard sneak attack and another 10d6 sneak damage from improved sneak attack feats, dual wielding 2 kamas gives an attack schedule of 34/31/28/25/22/19 (main hand) and 34/31 (offhand), unbuffed.

Saving throws at lv 40 unbuffed are: fort 24, ref 33, will 20

Final stats are:

Str 10, Dex 28, Con 12, Wis 10, Int 14, Cha 12

The last rogue level at 40 is necessary for having epic dodge, however if this dosen't appeal to your tastes, then epic dodge can be swapped for blinding speed, which would give another attack/round from haste. Alternatively, level 40 can be replaced with a monk level to max out discipline and concentration or a blackguard level to max out discipline, concentration, taunt and get a succubus summon 1/day, which might be good as a diversion so you can get the first few sneak attacks in.
