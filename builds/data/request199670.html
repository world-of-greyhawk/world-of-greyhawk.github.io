Peace And one thing to keep in mind about the Pale Master class: it gives you extra castings per day and access to higher levels spells (if you don't already get 9th levels spells from your wizard levels), but it does not add to your caster level. So, durations, damage, spell penetration checks, etc. will all be based on your wizard level only. Hence avado's suggestions for a wizard heavy build (or at least, one of the reasons for them).
