Savage Corsair

Keeping in tradition with the Pirate motif....

Wily, cagey, and highly dangerous, the Savage Corsair swings into combat with Greater Rage that creates a Terrifying aspect to all who behold him-- make a Will Save opposed to his Intimidate which is a solid 56. Those who are panicked and flee must now deal with not just his powerful arm (30 Str+6 for Rage), but also suffer 8d6 sneak dice per hit. And if they're a high dodging dexer, he can Taunt them with his Taunt at a 55 DC check to bring that AC down by -6. He is a pirate for the ages, with an Epic Reputation that makes him known throughout the Realms. The scimitar is chosen because it is the closest thing that resembles a cutlass.
