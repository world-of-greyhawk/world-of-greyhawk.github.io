Playable from level 1 40 ..PvM Build

First of all, sorry for my bad English. I am no native speaker and dont have the english version. So hopefully I spell everything right.
I build this character a while ago in a slightly different setup. It was made for a persistent world where you cant just bash chest, but have to pick the locks to get good items. So i need quite a lot Rogue levels to get all the skillpoints.

Race: Human to avoid exp penality. Halfling or Elf is possible if you drop one feat and 3 points in set traps.

The starting Stats are:
Str: 10, Dex: 18, Con: 14, Wis: 8, Int: 14, Cha: 8.
all points go to dex when you level up.

Final Skills:
Discipline (42), Tumble (40), UMD (41 to compensate the cha mali), Hide (30), Move Silent (30), Pickpocket (20),
Open Locks (43), Disarm Traps (43), Set Traps (5) Search
(43) + 40 Rest
Take the skillpoints at the levels when they are classskills. So Discipline at Fighter and Cot level and save the rest for rogueslevels.

Weapons: 2 Short Swords or Dagger if you are a Halfling.

Levelprogress:
1-6 Rog - Toghness, Dodge, Weaponfiness, Weapon Focus
7-11 Fgt - Knockdown, Ambidext, Imp. Knockdown, Weapon Spec.
12 Rog - Two Handed Fight
13-19 CoT - Imp. Two Handed Fight, Blind Fight, Expertise, Imp. Expertise, Imp. Crit.
20 Rog
21-22 CoT - Greater Dex I, Epic Weapon Focus
23 Fgt - Epic Weapon Spec
24-38 Rog - Greater Dex II, Def. Roll, Greater Dex III, Imp. Evasion
Greater Dex IV, Epic Dodge, Self Concealment I-III
39 CoT - SC IV, Armor Skin
40 Rog - SC V
Take the feats in that order they are listed. Some may need others first.

Final:
AC: 31 (10 + 8 from Thumble, 11 from Dex, 2 from Armor Skin)
AB 40/35/30/25 + 40/35 with 2 Short Swords
Saves: Fort 28/Reflex 39/Will 20.

Those numbers may not be that outstanding but you will profit a lot from buffs and items.
After all you are the lockpick of the group .
