TM If the magic level is very high, I wouldn't recommend a buffing Cleric. You're better off with a WM, or anything that has high unbuffed AB (because you'll be buffing it with items at that point, and a Cleric has low AB generally).

Say, a Cleric has 40 AB unbuffed. His max is thus 66, which he may reach with only his spells. Actually, he will Now, a WM might have about 50 AB unbuffed. He gets his +10 weapon and +12 STR. There's the 66 AB. However, he still has another +10 that can be boosted by his party members and whatnot. So, build a WM.
