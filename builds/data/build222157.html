You may be able to get thundering rage - you've missed 2 feats at level 9 (a general and CoT bonus) so I'd suggest you move Blind Fight to pre-epic so you end up being able to use it most of pre-epic (concealed enemies, help combat having to fight in Darkness) and drop Power Attack/Cleave which is less useful as you go up in levels. Also, how did you allocate skillpoints? Didn't look like you crossclassed into Tumble, nor took Armor Skin. Your ending AC is low so I'd suggest getting those, and using a shield.
