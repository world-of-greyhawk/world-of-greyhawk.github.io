Saosin777 Sounds like you're ready for a RP server, to get more depth to your playing experience.

I play a lot on 3kds. http://www.3kds.com/index.php
It's low/mid magic with max +3/+4 items in the shops. CNR crafting and mastercrafting. Items are customised to give save bonuses instead of immunities (there's no autofail on 1).
The server is reset several times daily, so you can do the quests quite often. There's much DM activity and LOTS of players, many of them really good roleplayers. There are many active guilds of different alignments.
There are rules that promote the RPing aspect of the game. Some of them put restrictions on what character builds are possible. As an experienced roleplayer I'll say the rules make sense.
The PW is on Game Spy's roleplaying page. No password, but have a look at the server's homepage before you start playing.

Edit: Oh yes, you can get all the way to lvl 40. There are many epic characters there. But if you roleplay your way up into epic levels you might realize that you had just as much fun at the lower levels.
