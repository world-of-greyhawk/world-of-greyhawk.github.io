Quite okay, it has some potential. You should end the build Ranger21/Monk6/Rogue 13. You need 21 Ranger for BoE, which is quite necessary in a dex ranger, and you need 13 Rogue for epic dodge. That limits your epic feats. You need 5 FE's to qualify for Boe, so no epic feats from ranger slots. Another general epic feat must be used for Epic dodge. 5 feats left, getting yourself in a tight spot here. 3 of those can be used for EWF, EP and AS. 2 left for great DEX, ending DEX at 30.
Tough build to get right, but that should do it.

If you decide Boe is not worth it, trade in 1 lvl of Ranger for Rog 16, which gives you another feat, perhaps Crippling strike or Slippery Mind. At lvl 30, use the FE for EWF, opening 1 general feat, which you can use on Greater DEX, thus ending you DEX on 32. You have so few FE's, one less won't matter. Pre-epic, switch the Lightning reflexes for Blind Fight. You REF save is good enough.

As for playability, I know this guy will have a really rough ride until he closes on the epic's. Once he get to around lvl 16 or so, and from there on he will get better and better. But for the first 15 or so, life will be hard.

A few tweaks and this guy should be quite nice, at least once you have managed to survive to late pre-epics.
