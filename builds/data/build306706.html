It sacrifices the strength bonus to Discipline, so it needs to strike hard and fast. It squeezed in KD for that, too. The aforementioned revisions to the Purple Dragon Knight class (Badlands 3201):

-Increased Oath of Wrath duration to 10 rounds per PDK level.
-Changed Oath of Wrath damage type to Divine
-Changed "Final Stand" to a 10d10 bonus hit points to all allies
-Changed the damage type from Inspiring to be slashing over bludgeoning for the sake of undead shapes. Duration increased to five minutes (50 rounds)
-Changed rallying cry to a 4 minute (40 round) duration, and increase the + to attack bonus to +2 over +1
-Left PDK shield ability as is, for balance purposes.
