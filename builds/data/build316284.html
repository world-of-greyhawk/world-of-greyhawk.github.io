Now lets compare the two builds with each other:

Fighter version:
- more hp (388 instead of 334).
- better saves.
- has WS and EWS.
- bonus feats.
- slightly better ab and ac.

Rogue version:
- has uncanny dodge and epic dodge for the -1 ac.
- 7d6 sneak attack to compensate the loss of WS and EWS.
- has more skillpoints (UMD).
- gains more spellslots and a 1 ab and damage bonus with the Divine Favor spell.

Benefits of both:
50% speed bonus.
25 SR (not great but better then noting).
33 SF DC which is 39 max.
good ac.
decent ab with both.
monk unarmed damage combined with WS and EWS or sneaks.
6 attacks around.
Empowered healing.

Trickery is also a good option for both.
