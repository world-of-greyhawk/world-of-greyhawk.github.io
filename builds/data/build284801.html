This is a shifter build with a little boost to damage on a successful knockdown. The humanoid forms don't go epic, but all the others that can do. Even Greater Wildshape IV is unlimited times per day. There is great variety and plenty of protection and damage. The AB in general is on the low side and this build would not do well on a medium to high magic world.

Note on levels one through ten it helps tremendously to have a henchman. why dwarven axe? other than that one question (and it is just a question) nicely done.
