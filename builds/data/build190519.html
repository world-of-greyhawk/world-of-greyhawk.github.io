You are starting with an 18 wis and ending with an odd wis score. I would either start with a 14 dex, 17 wis and 12 con, or a 17 wis and 16 dex, or I would drop one of the Great Wisdom feats. If you are getting +12 to all stats, the extra point of Wisdom will do you no good since you are not a caster.

I would consider dropping one point of int as well, sticking it in dex, since you will only have one lvl of spells, but I like having the extra skill point as well, so I wouldn't sweat it either way.
