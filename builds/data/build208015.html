The purpose of this build was to provide a party with a self-reliant, disappearing, sneak attacking defender. On our PW, discussions lamenting the rogue as a lost art lead me to find a build that would be survivable in a high AB, high damage world with more than a few true seeing critters. HIPS is great until you get into those situations where True Sight abounds. This build would compensate the lost ability in those situations by reverting to a tank and going toe to toe if need be.

The pre-epic SD level also prevents you from getting that 4th main hand attack too by lowering your AB to 15. Assuming you get haste, 7 attacks per round means 3 attacks the first flurry, and the first flurry is what matters in sneak attacking (solo). A successful KD will make your opponent prone for 1 round. All subsequent attacks while your opponent's prone made by your character will be sneak attacks and also at +4 to hit.

IKD won't make any difference to your sneak attacks, but it will allow you to knock down larger creatures and make your KD attempt slightly harder to resist.
