Well, I made this build as a caster Druid, and it doesn't really fights shifting except for the obvious playable reasons at low levels.

Besides saying that Monk is a good addition when being WIS based, there are several stuff to consider.

What do you want for the other classes? as far as spellcasting is concerned, you should go over 30 Druid levels and take Epic Spell Focus, so for spellpower you should take a low amount of levels on your other classes.

Champion of Torm gets Great Wisdom as bonus feat, so it can be useful to pump up WIS for your DCs.

If you want to fight melee you can very well take Fighter, it's a no-brainer for added damage.

You could also take Arcane Archer, a Druid 20 / Wizard 1 / Arcane Archer 19 with Zen Archery (or a variant in spread for more spellpower) will get a high AB, and a good damage bonus from AA, that one is a sure one to work good. At any rate, taking Zen Archery is a good choice for a WIS based character, it can also work out like a Druid/Fighter/Rogue, since not only AA can add damage to a bow, EWS or sneak attack are also viable options.
