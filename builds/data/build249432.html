I had two DwD builds in the works for posting. However, the other is quite close to Grizz's most recent offering, The Citadel, so I'm just posting this one. In the vein of my Master of Resistance build, which is designed to get good resistance to elemental damages, this one goes for that as well, while also getting a decent AB with decent HPs and DR.

Skill points are at a premium, and the saves could be better. However, with the Rogue levels and UMD, there are a lot of options available to bolster the saves. The Energy Resistance feats were chosen to give a decent reduction against the 3 most common elemental damage types. Coupled with the good helping of HPs, he should be able to survive a barrage of elemental damage spells long enough to shut down enemy mages, and the 20/- DR (along with the 21/- DR to physical damage) in those damage types will also make a big impact on weapon wielders that rely on elemental damages as a significant component of their damage potential.
