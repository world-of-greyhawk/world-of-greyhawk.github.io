He probably searched for Druid/Wizard/Palemaster and missed it. Anyways, this is the build I would suggest as well. Should be original on your server and a nice surprise for people to run into. Druid / Pale Master is not a common build, you can surprise people easily, but in fact it isn't all that powerful as it might sound, yeah Pale Master gives you critical immunity but that's about it, you were already immune to sneaks while shifted and unless you take more PM to take Arcane Epic Spells, the rest of the PM levels are just low hit dice and a bit of AC (but druids usually take Monk which gives even more AC) while it's a considerable amount of levels where you don't get penetration on your divine spells.

Depending on your environment, it could be worth it to spend a bit more Pale Master for Epic Mage Armor or Epic Warding, since they're very powerful spells Druid can't access, though you would lose even more penetration...
