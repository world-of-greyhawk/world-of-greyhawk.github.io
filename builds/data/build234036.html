-The succubus has been raised directly from Hell. Her skin is as tough as hell So tough that no normal weapon can hit her (or nearly). She has base armor class of 39, and she has spells (Epic mage armor, Cat’s grace) to help her get over 60 AC. With Epic warding, she becomes almost immune to any physical damages.
-She also has several immunities (Critical hits, Hold, Paralyse, Stun, Sleep and Fire)
-The succubus can concentrate her energies to send, from Hell, a HellBall to her opponents (40d6)
-She can call from Hell a Lich lord to help her bring death to the world
-She can send a tiny cone of fire to her enemies. (10d6)
-7 times per day, she can paralyse her enemy with her infernal arm
-3 times per day, she can kill her enemy with a single touch

Even though she has many strengths, the succubus has an important weakness : low saving throws, which make her vulnerable to many spells (except for the one she is immune to)
She has a low base attack, but her high strength helps balance her attacks. Giving her strength equipment and Bull’s strength spell, she’ll become a strong melee character

Suggested Bard spells:
Protection vs alignment
Cat’s grace
Bull’s strength
Invisibility


I wished to use the Whip, because i felt it fit well with the Succubus’ psychology. However, for better critical damages i would replace Weapon prof (exotic) by (martial) and would replace all feats associated to Whip by Rapier and use a Highroad rapier (keen). That would give a crit 12-20 instead of 19-20 with whip.
