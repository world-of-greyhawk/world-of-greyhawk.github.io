Star Wreck If he has a fort of 47 then implosion might not be the best option.

The formula for a spell DC is 10 + spell level + wis mod + Spell foci. Implosion gets an additional +3 for being Implosion.

The max possible ammount wis can be buffed to is:
18 (starting) + 10 (bonus ability points) + 10 (great wis) + 12 (Items/buffs). That's a total of 50, which gives a wis mod of 20.

So, with ESF evocation, the MAX DC for implosion is 10+9+3+20+6 = 48. With a fort save of 47, the sorc will need a 1 to fail vs your implode. So assuming you can weather the sorceorous storm of Mucho Destructo, you'll still need a LOT of slots (and some luck) to take him down with an implode. Spell mantles will protect the sorc from implosions... at most you've got 1 shot if you catch the guy unaware.

I'd go with a mage build or an archer. Keep them small, of course.
