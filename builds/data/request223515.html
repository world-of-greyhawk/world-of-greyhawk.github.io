Depends how you want to play your character. Are you looking for basically a sneaking melee type with spells to buff and help out offensively, or a caster that has some capability of sneak attacking at need?

Quote: epic warding i want (is epic mage armour of any use?)

Epic Warding is going to require at least 21 WIZ levels. As for Epic Mage Armor, it depends on the gear you have available. You get +5 AC over four different AC types (+5 Dodge, +5 Armor, +5 Deflection, and +5 Natural? - not sure of the last type) Since Dodge is the only one that stacks, if you have +5 items that give you the other types, EMA may not be worth the investment.

Quote: focusing in evocation/enchanment

This means 6 feats (4 pre-epic and 2 epic) is you want Epic Spell Focus in both. Again, if you going with the character as mainly a caster (i.e. focussing on INT as much as possible), it may be worth the investment. If not, itmay be a waste of feats.

Quote: going 12 rogue 1 shadowdancer and 7 wiz pre epic

This is not optimal for your BAB. This distribution will result in a BAB of +12. If you go with Rogue 12/SD 4/WIZ 4, you'll have a BAB of +14. Much better if you plan on being a sneaky melee type.

If you're going to the caster with a bit of sneak capability, then the R 12/SD 1/WIZ 7 should be fine.
