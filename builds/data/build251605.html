I think avado answered your question like no other could...
With only 10 caster levels, Evocation is probably the worst school to focus in, and with an Int of only 20, you shouldn't be focusing at all. Even if they do roll an autofail 1, you still won't be doing any damage.

From the high dex, I'm guessing you are trying to be a spellsword. At the very least, switch to Wiz 4/ PM 8/ SD 8 pre-epic, and take weapon finesse long before weapon focus (weapon focus gives +1 AB, weapon finess gives this build +6)
