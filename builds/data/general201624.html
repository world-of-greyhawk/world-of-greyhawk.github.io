1) If she enters Improved Expertise mode, any spell then she makes disables that mode, isn't it true?

No, you can cast in Expertise/Improved Expertise mode without a problem.

Quote: 2) If I wish to remove Improved Expertise and change it to something else, can I lower the INT to 10? Or 12?

12 you could do, and drop lore as a skill But if you drop to 10, you'll have to lose another skill, and you don't want to drop conc, spellcraft, tumble, or discipline. You could drop part of multiple skills, but you honestly want to max those 4 at the least, so dropping it to 10 would be bad.

Quote: 3) If we're talking about stats here, why: 12 DEX & STR?
Dex for AC and STR to help her carry stuff? Please explain to me

She's meant to wear full plate. 12 DEX gives her a +1 dex modifier, the best she can get with Full Plate. And 12 STR allows you to carry that full plate and shield more easily.
