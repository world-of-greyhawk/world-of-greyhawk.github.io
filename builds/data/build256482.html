This is an old build of mine that I had a second look at recently and decided I wanted to share. What I had in mind with this build was to reach AC 100+.
The build is geared towards that goal in every way, sacrificing AB and HPs, making it quite hard to play normally.
The build has an AC of 101 self buffed in mundane equipment,(counting in the effect of curse song on the opponents).

the highest possible AC obtainable from a build is from a Bard (sorc) 3-5 / Monk 6-9 / Pale Master 28, halfling, starting stats 20 dex, wis 16 with all attributes going to wisdom & 6 great wis

without any items at all you get:

10 base + 1 halfling +16 pm +5 dexterity +11 wisdom +8 tumble +1 monk +20 epic mage armor +10 improved expertise +1 mage armor +2 armor skin = 85

not anything special, but that is completely naked!

...now if you throw in some Haste, items to give +12 to dex & wis, you get 101 and this is on your Character sheet, WITHOUT curse song effectively increase AC. Also you still have 10 AC left in the dodge cap to push this up to 111...

furthermore as most of your AC is hard-coded and from Wisdom you only lose 11-21 (depending on dex buff & having haste!) when caught flat-footed.

However this is even more of an AB gimp...you would have to use Zen Archery to even make an attack (29 AB ftw) and you may as well take Dirty Fighting to Maximize the damage you'd deal! Thanks, that's both informative and interesting!
When we use items to reach the caps, it becomes a totally different game.
