Now, you have +20 to max cap, which LEGALLY puts your guy at 76. I used to play a server where all 40s walkd around with +10 universal saves, so 66/66/66. Which isnt too shabby (and if you can find it, +10 more to add). Add in LoH, and you have 67!

The problem with this guy is, very poor ab and damage, though may not be too bad. Definitly, not a power build! If your purpose is to get high saves, then 20 monk pre is essential. Definitely not a build i would want to play through.
