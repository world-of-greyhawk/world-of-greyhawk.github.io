First, this tread is for epic finished builds only, you should have posted in the general tread or in the build request forum. This said let me try answering your question.
DC for stunning fist is 10 + 1/2 char.lvl + WIS mod
Starting with WIS 18 and taking all the stats increase in wisdom plus Great Wis X will net you 38 Wis. Stats enhancements are capped at +12 so the maximum theoretical Wis is 50, which means a +20 bonus. Add Improved Stunning Fist feat: each time you take it nets you +2 DC up to a maximum of +20. So the math goes:
DC= 10 (base DC) + 40/2 (1/2 char.lvl) + 20 (WIS mod) + 20 (10 Imp.Stunn.Fist) = 70
Theoretically it's therefore possible. I have to check out whether you can level something like this anyhow. CoT seems to be necessary in epic lvls to pump up WIS with the bonus feats.
