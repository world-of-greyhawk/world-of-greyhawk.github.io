This is fun character build to play in some PWs. It gives you a decent AB and AC, along with good bardic spells and skills. Using the whirlwind attack has been really useful playing on PWs with NPCs having high HPs and gang up on you. I supose you could drop some feats for KD and IKD, but I really like the all around build this character has. You can do anything with it. You get the nice Bardic Songs, Cursed Song, and combine with Taunt and maybe Expertise in there, you're looking at getting +10AB and +10AC. I've used this build in PvP. Using scrolls to enhance some protection makes the difference here. I've come close to killing in PvP a strong monk and 2 others, I was only 29th lvl at time while the monk was 40th. Its amazing what UMD will do for a character using scrolls. I've added the Still Spell feat for that extra edge to cast your Greater Dispell and War Cry when ever. Im not quite sure as to choose Blind Fighting or Armor Skin. If someone could weigh out the advantages and disadvantages that would be nice.
