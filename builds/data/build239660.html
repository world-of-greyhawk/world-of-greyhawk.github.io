Before I say anything, let me first admit that I am biased against Weapon Masters. Have never liked them, and will probably never play one.

Okay, that said, I see that you are taking 4 pre-epic feats in epic levels. Such are the consequences of making a WM without Fighter or CoT levels, and it translates to 4 fewer Great Dex (or Str, depending on the build). What do you get from 7 WM? Only +1 AB, so you're already at a loss in that department.

At the very least, I would drop the IC (Kama) feat. Your AB is low enough that the extra threat range probably won't help you.

Speaking of low AB, I would also drop the TWF feats. As far as I'm concerned, 38 is kind of at the cusp of allowable TWF AB, and even then only when you have self buffing abilities (some of the others may disagree). When you are as feat starved as you are, it becomes a fairly easy decision to drop them.

Final tweaks, drop starting Str to 9, and bump starting Int to 14. You won't even notice the loss to damage, and that should get you enough skill points to put a rogue skill or two at a useable level (put it into Set Trap and you will more than make up for the lost damage ). You can also get 5 more ranks by lowering Tumble and UMD a touch. . .both only work on multiples of 5 (Tumble on the base ranks, UMD on the modified ranks).

Good luck

Quote: What do you get from 7 WM? Only +1 AB, so you're already at a loss in that department.

Well, the WM levels also give him increased critical damage and critical threat range, thus alleviating the "where does the damage come from in a stand-up fight" problem with sneak-attacking dex characters to a degree.

Whether or not that's worth the price of entry, however, is up for debate - he's already got sneak attacks and free IKD. But 7 WM levels do provide a much more significant benefit than a small bonus to AB.

Of course, kamas are lousy critical weapons and his AB is weak enough that, in many epic environments, he will end up being a "20 machine" even if he single-wields. I'm not really sure why people build these characters - attacks per round are meaningless if you don't hit with them.

In all... I'm not too impressed. There are better builds for this purpose, and this one isn't offering me anything to get excited over.
