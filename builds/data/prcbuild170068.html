Author's Notes:

With this character.... you can get 1400+ DAMAGE with smite evil!!! Whooo! That is just a normal smite damage! At the most, you can get 1499. I am not sure about the criticals though... If you can figure what is the actual max damage from critical for the smite evil.. please tell me.

This is a god-killer character

Of course, the character is not using any equipment other than just scythe and normal clothes. But if you don't want to do scythe since you have to take one extra feat, switch it for greatsword for greater damage or greataxe for greater critical. For greataxe, you should average 1100 damage per smites, not sure about the greatsword though.

This build is designed to rely exclusively on the smite evil ability, however... it can hold up its own against other character that is not evil for that it has many weapon enchantment spells(at least 7 of them).
