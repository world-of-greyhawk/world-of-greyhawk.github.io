It is possible. If you take elf with DEX 20 and take Great Dex II, you can get self concealment at your lvl 33, 36 and 39 regular feats.
If you take 16 lvls of SD, you get bonus feats at lvl 13 and 16. If you take these two bonus feats at clvl 33 or later, you can get all the 5 self concealment feats.

If you have Bard 8/PM 4/SD 8 at lvl 20 your BAB will be 14. If you take Bard 12/SD 8 at lvl 20 your BAB will be 15.
Anyway you should be able to end up with Bard 14/SD 16/PM 10 at lvl 40 and get all the feats you asked for.
With BAB 15 you get 3 attacks. With weapon finesse, bard song, taunt and buffing spells, you should be able to hit quite often if not every time.
If you want epic warding, you need 6 more PM lvls. I think you must take them from the Bard lvls. You get weaker spells and song this way, and you have to take PM lvls pre-epic ending with a lower BAB (max 14).
But IMO giving this character more defence with epic warding is overkill.
