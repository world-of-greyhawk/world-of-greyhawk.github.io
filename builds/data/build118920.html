Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build
....( Duel Kama )

This character was a response to a request for Ninja and Samurai builds. I figured that a ninja would likely be a monk/rogue combo, and I threw in the shadow dancer level for colour. I went with rogue over assassin for the extra skill points. This character was originally posted with an elven counterpart on March 11th,
Click Here

Advantages

-Hide in Plain Sight. Coupled with a +11D6 sneak attack, any enemies that are vulnerable to sneak attacks will be in trouble.
-Many attacks. He has a lowish AB but with that many attacks he's bound to score some hits.
-Improved evasion. He has an okay reflex save, but his fortitude save is low, so he should watch out for horrid wiltings.
-Epic Dodge.
-Skill Mastery. He can lay traps in the middle of combat if he needs to.
-Use Magic Devices.
-Empty body. If he doesn't have a scroll of displacement or improved invisibility he can still get 50% concealment.

Disadvantages

-Low hitpoints.
-Low fortitude and will saves.
-Low damage. Against a critical hit immune enemy with damage reduction all he can do is use scrolls and traps.
