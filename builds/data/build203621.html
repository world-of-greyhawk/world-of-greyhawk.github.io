PVP build, playable lvl 1-40 (I hope)

I made this for a request in the NWN General Forum. I know there are a few reaper builds here already, namely Grim Reaper /Bard 4/Fighter 8/PM 28 and The Grim Reaper /Wizard 15/PM 12/WM 13, and perhaps others that don't have the word "reaper" in the build name. The others (that I found) use PM which gives you a nice skeletal arm, but I just don't think the reaper should be raising undead, it would seem more fitting (to me) that he would be intent on sending them to a more final death. As he gains more skill as a sorcerer I would recommend using robes (black casual robe for maximum reaper look) and relying on damage reduction spells rather than ac to stay alive, which means low-magic worlds are a must.
I've tried mixing concept and powergaming, hopefully without losing too much of either.
