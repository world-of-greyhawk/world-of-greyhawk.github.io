I like this build because it has immunity to Critical Attacks. It has an attack of +51/+46/+41/+36. This is kind of low for most archers but it should be enough to do the job. You could increase this by 1 more if you replace the Epic Fortitude and Will, but your saves will also be really week. This character has a naked AC of 36, and when buffed with cats grace, mage armor, and haste it becomes a nice 42. This build also has a decent HP of 340.
