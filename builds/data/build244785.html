The stats in the saved character section are never right, cause they get calculated with the preepic progression also in epic levels. Look only at the stats in game and better yet at the logs (with combat debugging enabled possibly). Your BAB is 25, for example, not 29.

You'll have to forgive me, but I can't hold it back: it's rogue, damnit, R-O-G-U-E

PM me the right stats after checking them up in game, please, I'll edit them in.

Now, you are not taking any weapon foci and no EP and your AB is really low, when it could easily be an acceptable 41. AS would help too and so would Blind Fight and KD/IKD or Called Shot. Spring Attack is a waste, although you are probably taking it only as a prerequisite for WWA, which is no big deal, actually, apart at the lower levels. I'd drop both of them to get better feats preepic. Stealthy is probably droppable, your stealthy skills are good enough. Epic Shadowlord also is no big deal better get something else (and the choice is wide).

HPs are too low, drop WIS to 8 and raise CON to 12. STR 14 is too expensive for a dexer halfling , go with 12 and free up another 4 points.

Actually I'd get more Rogue in for the sneaks. What about some fighter levels for extra feats and possibly WS/EWS to give you some dmg output?

Skills: where's set trap? A dexer sneaker with low damage output is gonna need to set traps to prepare the battle field. Points in spellcraft will improve your saves vs spells too. Drop one of spot or listen and put those points in set trap and spellcraft. I'd get less open locks and search too, usually you don't need such high values in those skills.

I guess that's all for now, just a few suggestion after a quick glance.
