When in doubt punt Depends on your intent, and why you're going with those classes. The main problem I see, is you will have split attributes. So; your Monk feeds off wisdom but your sorcerer feeds off Cha.
As a caster, I'd avoid it. You won't have decent spell slots; or lvl9 spells; also you have a stronger melee build than caster

As a melee... well, it might work out but you may be better served with something like Sorc18/rdd10/monk12 - this will give one lvl9 spell and you can still get a few max'd IGMS at the end if you take maximize spell feat and IGMS spell (take all rdd and at least 4 monk levels before epic for best results, I think - this means starting as monk; taking sorcerer at char lvl3 + 4). To be honest, imho; sorcerer is not the best melee caster build due to the minimal feats (and even then, Pally seems better served due to dual CHA benefit) and locked spells BUT it can work, if you plan it tightly.
