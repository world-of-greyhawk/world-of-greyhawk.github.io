In this case, all of the situational damage will get applied. Save your smite evil attacks for end bosses. The rest of the time you are doing a minimum of 42 dmg. per hit (47 if you use power attack)

You still have lay on hands that will do over 400 damage and you have a huge amount of turn undead attempts with all the paladin levels you have.

The benefits here are on such a low magic world you can raise your stats, particularly charisma, with aura of glory (+4 charisma), eagle's splendor (1d4 +1), and the harper's eagle's splendor (1d4 +1) . All of those stack. And you can make potions to drink to raise your dexterity or your charisma.

Finally, Harper's get tumble as a class skill getting you extra AC and the bonuses like Tymora's Smile, Deiner's Eye, etc. make this build a very tough build.

You have all the immunities that a paladin has too and the bonuses to saves from CoT. You get a nice kick to your attack bonus if you activate Divine Wrath, a huge bonus to AC from Divine Shield, and a huge bonus to damage from Divine Might.
