A wizard(10)/PM(30) is honestly not going to be much of a wizard, because even though Pale Master gives you spell slots and spell levels, it does not add to your caster level. You'd still cast as a level 10 wizard, so your damage would be low and you'd have problems with spell resistance. I'd go with the pure wizard rather than the PM version.
