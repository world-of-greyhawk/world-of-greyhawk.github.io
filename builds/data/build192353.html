Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM + PvP Build

This build was made with the idea of creating an elven killer that can do mincemeat with anything not immune to sneak. When a silent and deadly response is required, this is the elf the council calls upon.

I wanted to get epic dodge as early as possible, get self concealment V, and avoid xp penalties. I have seen similar builds, but none that met my requirements. Hope you like it.

It's SHADOWDANCER, dammit, shadowdancer!!! Thanks for the comments. When I tested the build I entered the feats in a slightly different order so I did not catch the issue with the CoT feat list, the correction proposed makes sense.

I am using CoT instead of fighter because of the xp penalty. If that is not an issues, fighter makes a stronger build for sure. However, in the analysis of the benefits of using CoT as opposed to rogue only, you get a +1 advantage of BAB on all attacks also which is important. This type of build is either hit-and-run or a flanker. For the hit and run, you want to make sure you hit as often as you can, that's why I tried to optimize AB.

And yes, the build can be changed to bring the first concealment from level 33 to 29, but that's at the cost of delaying epic dodge from 21 to 24 and loosing one of the improved sneak or the epic weapon focus. I think it's an equally good choice and it depends on the server you play on. In my case, with slow leveling, delaying from 21 to 24 means weeks if not months and I rarely make it post-30 with a character anyhow, but for faster leveling servers, it sounds like a good alternative.

Actually, if you don't mind waiting a little for epic dodge and the SC I-V, using human and fighter is probably the best build once at level 40.
