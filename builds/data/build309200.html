Second build posted and first of its kind i think (couldnt find on the epic build searcher). I apologize if its totally not the only one. Noticed that Anius and Kail once were thinking about a barb/palladin combo to...well i added bard to it.

The idear behind this one is simple:
- using TR for terrifying the enemy.
- using curse song to drop opponents ac, skills, attack, damage, AND saves so he has less chance to resist TR!
- using Dispelling scrolls to get rid of buffed mind immunities (UMD) so TR has effect.
- True Strike scroll as a reserve for adding huge AB for a short period of time.
- Use of Bard song/spells, Palladin spells and buffing scrolls to fill in the weaknesses of this build and up ab/ac.

Lvl: TR dc:

Devastating Crit dc at 40: 38 (+12 str = 44).

Arcane Spell Failure is a fact. Robe/Light armor with a large shield for some more ac but to limit Failure chance would be best.

This char begins with 15 cha. That is to get the lvl 5 bard spells. You could say to take 14 cha and spent 1 point while lvling on it but that ends up with -1 ab/str in the end no matter how you look at it.

max ab: 41+2(bard song)+2(war cry)+6(+12 str)+1(bless)+1/2(divine favor?? not sure)+20 true strike(scroll UMD)
= 73/74 ab.
not sure about this though might be my mistake. Ofcourse you could buff it up more with more different spell scrolls but the effects probably wont last long enough.
