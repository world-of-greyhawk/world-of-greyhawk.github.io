STR 16
DEX 8
CON 16
INT 12
WIS 14
CHA 8

You'd need to look at either exchanging 2 STR raises for 2 CON raises to get the EDR feats or just not bothring with EDR. I'd also drop the Initiative feats, as they do not stack the way I originally assumed when I did the build.

I'd pick up KD as the Human bonus feat and GRT WIS as the CoT bonus feat to replace Superior Initiative. With 18 CLC levels, you'll be susceptible to being dispelled though, so if you want a CLC build that's only going to have to worry about Mord's and not Dispels, let me know and I'll try to work something up (perhaps FTR 4/CLC 26/CoT 10).
