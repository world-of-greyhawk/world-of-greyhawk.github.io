I play there from time to time. (OK the only server I play on.) I'd recommend the Wheel of Time - The Third Age server. It was around a while ago, died a death because (I think) the person hosting it had lots of RL stuff going on, and has recently been started up again with a new admin team. There's a growing player base, reasonably quick levelling, but plenty of rp and frequent DM organised events. Also, since this incarnation of it is fairly new, anyone starting to play now can get involved in the major storylines from the start. Although it's based on Robert Jordan's Wheel of Time books, a detailed knowledge of the WoT canon isn't necessary, especially with the recent start (although I'd recommend reading the books anyway, as they're pretty good). Everyone I've encountered playing or DMing there is really friendly and helpful.
