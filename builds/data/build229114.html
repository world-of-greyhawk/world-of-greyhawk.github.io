I'd drop Power Attack and Cleave for a couple more Great Strengths. Your AB isn't high enough for you to comfortably knock 5 off of it for some extra damage, and Cleave is of very limited use at high levels.

Nice build, though . Welcome aboard. I like it! Good use of the fighter levels for extra epic feats too. I would probably swap crippling strike with imp evasion since he's more defense orientated and a lowish AB. He has Improved Evasion

Anyway I'd drop Power Attack, Cleave and Called Shot in the epic levels for epic feats. You can get 4 points more STR by dropping the above mentioned feats for Great STR's and moving EDR3 to DD22, picking a Great STR at 27. Your AB would hence rise to a decent +39.
