Tuoni is the finnish God of Death ruling the Tuonela (finnish underworld) and in the AD&D 1st edition incarnation he has the club as his symbol. His priests were known to be eager to administer final rites to the seriously wounded (or not so seriously either) on the battlefields actually helping them reaching the realm beyond thanks to the persuasive power of a broken skull. The belongings tha those they "helped" carried with themselves became theirs, of course, as a reward for their help in reaching the realm of their master. Interesting cult, ain't it?

Tuoni is a CN god with evil tendencies in AD&D 1e, btw.

Tales related to the Tuonela can be found in the Kalevala, a (the?) finnish epic poem.

***********

We've got a highlevel Cleric here, in two variants: battlepriest (STR route) and imploder (WIS route). Bard's in to help with singing the hymns and also with tumble and UMD. Fighter's in purely for the club exploit which is at the heart of this build, so, please, do not suggest dropping it

Because of the restricted choice of the Cleric's bonus feats the STR variant has got a higher than usual WIS for a battlepriest. It means more spell slots and a higher will save, so no harm comes from it.

Domains, theme wise, should include Death. Powerwise I'd go for Trickery and War, but pick whatever you prefer. Anyhow, anything that helps the priest administer death should be fine with Tuoni

In the WIS build you could go with STR 10, DEX 8, WIS 18 starting for -2 AB/dmg, -1 ref save (AC will be covered by Cat's Grace/AoV) but +1 Implosion DC and extra spellslots. I wouldn't do it though.

Enough ranting, here we go with the build.
