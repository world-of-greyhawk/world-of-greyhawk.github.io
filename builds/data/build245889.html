Introduction:
Faust (or Finny's Favourite)

Playable 1-40 PvM or PvP
requires an alignment shift

Pros
  • It's a Bard
  • Good damage output
  • Great AC
  • Even though a lot of his AC is lost flat-footed, the risk of being flat footed isn't that high with high AC (can't hit a KD), discipline (if someone rolls to hit you, chances are you'll succeed on the check) and listen (no sneaking)
  • Good skillset
  • Decent saves, especially Fortitude
  • 22nd level Bard Song for party support (assuming you've cast Eagle's
  • Buffing spells
  • Can Taunt pretty effectively even without ESF
  • UMD
  • Other stuff I can't remember now

Cons
  • HP's quite low
  • Over a quarter of his AC is Dodge/Tumble
  • Not enough skill points
  • Not enough feats
  • Alignment shift
