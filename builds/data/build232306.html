Naked with a mundane composite longbow, with but buffed this guy can get his AB up in the 60's, and his AC up in the 40/50's. With nice (high level)magical items it can go as high as 90, even 100.
Also he does great damage from afar with divine might and divine wrath. He draws ac from both dex and wis and cha, hahahaha.
All of which he can he can self buff. He also draws damage from both cha and strenght(with a mighty bow), both of which he can self buff.

He also has great saves. (+11 from COT levels)

Some of his powers are limited to one use per day (Divine Wrath, Strenght Domain Power, War Domain power). Those should be left for the final boss.


I see him as someone in a party, raining divine death from behind the front line of barbarians and weapon masters, which he can heal by the way....

There's just one thing with divine power you should be aware of, it doesn't works all so greatly with high magical items, because AB does not stacks, but the divine damage is nice.

Should change those domains, Strength won't work a bit if you cap your status with items, I'm not the expert in the domains part, but Trickery is nice, gives Improved Invisibility.

Good that you have kama as backup, but you could gain that same AB point from WF if you get the monk at epic levels (by no means better since playability goes down, but would end stronger), and use that feat for something else (Toughness gets to mind).
