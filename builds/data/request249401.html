thanks! Every Self Concealment feat gives 10% natural Conealment, to a max of SCV where it's 50%

Concealment is like a dodge, whenever a melee or a ranged attacks a target with Concealment, the percentage of Concealment will count, if you get the "roll" you get concealed, which means the attack automatically misses, no matter even if it's a critical hit, it will miss. on clear terms: A 50% Concealment means half the attacks made on you miss automatically, which is awesome, it just doesn't works on spells.

Blind-Fight taken by the attacker reduces the Concealment effect by 50%, so a Concealment of 50% will really work only in 25% against anyone who has Blind-Fight (hence why it's a very important feat).

It's rogue, dammit, ROGUE!!! Blind-Fight doesn't halve concealment, it lets you re-roll your miss chance. Hence the effective concealment is squared.
I.e:
Concealment w/o B-F Concealment w B-F
10% -> 1%
20% -> 4%
30% -> 9%
40% -> 16%
50% -> 25%
The concealment is only halved for 50%. This also illustrates why 30% concealment would be useless against someone with B-F.
