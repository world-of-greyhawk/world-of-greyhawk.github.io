Good enough for me. I didnt know that you could choose curse song on a H.S. level. Really nice build. I bet it is fun to play this class combo.

It costs a 2 feats and 22 skillpoints investment to be able to pick the HS class. These feats and skills may not be the hottest in a power build, but can have great RP value.
Anyhow, I think you get well paid for your investment here with two important bonus feats and all the "minor" HS class feats. Taking Bard 30 instead of Bard 25/HS 5 would also have given 2 epic bonus feats and no initial feat investment in alertness and iron will, but that wouldn't be a HS, right?

You have 321 skillpoints. After the HS prereqs you can (almost)max 7 skills.
Perform, hide, move silently, tumble, spellcraft, UMD and spot or listen are my favorites in this kind of build.
In a bard build you get the amplify spell that boosts listen. That can be a reason to pick listen if you want the character to be a really good scout. But it can also be a good reason to put skillpoints somewhere else.
As long as you have HIPS and good stealth you can often get out of harms way before you cast spells. That makes concentration less important.
