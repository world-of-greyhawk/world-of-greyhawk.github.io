Due to his classes this character has a max HP total of 618. Due to his dreadnaught levels he gets damage reduction 1 & 2 and takes 3 as a regular feat giving him 9/- total. Vigilant class gives great fortitude, iron will and lightning relfexes, dreadnaught gives epic fortitude, and with the epic will feat this character has respectable saves of 32 fort/19 ref/21 will. With a normal bastard sword this characters attacks are +43/+38/+33/+28 with a damage range of 16-25 per hit. For those really tough AC critters twice per day this character can activate his unstoppable ability giving him a +20 to hit on his next attack. If he is fighitng something that gives him trouble with knockdowns or disarms, he can activate his unmovable ability giving him +20 discipline, but this reduces his speed. Fortunately he can sprint once a day to make up for that lost speed. Finally he has a natural regen of 1 and natural armor of +2. In normal full plate with a tower shield his AC is a very respectable 30. Some basic magic can easily push that into the low 50s.
All in all this character plays like a very solid tank, able to take damage as well as dish it out.
