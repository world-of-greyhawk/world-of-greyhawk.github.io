Well, i know i might be considered not answering the question, but one more point in acritical ability (charisma, strengh, constitution) might be more useful.

But if you really have to choose between strengh and wisdom, i would go for wisdom because :
- +1 AC, and you're a tank.
- +1 reflex saves (a low save on most tanks)
- Mind spells immunity, a common immunity on most worlds, counters very efficiently a low will save.
