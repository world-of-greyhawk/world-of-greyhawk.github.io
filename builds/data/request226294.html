FP Here's the cheesey version of the first build. Drop CoT, go FTR 11 (to get EWS in epic) and take a late Bard lvl to max out spellcraft (+4 saves vs spells), tumble (+4 AC) and UMD. Adjust starting stats, since you'll be using one Great STR slot to grab EWS and therefore would end with -1 STR. DEX up to 14 (+1 ref save), WIS to 10 (+1 will save), STR reduced to 16 to end on an even number. Put 5 points into perform to use 1 song a day to make up for lost AB and damage for 10 rounds. Saves do suffer, but you compensate by getting a further +4 vs spells. Taunt is a bard class skill but you haven't got the points to make it effective, unless you drop discipline, which you might consider since you told KD is not worth it...I guess you have easy access to items with KD immunuty. If it is so, then drop discipline and max taunt on your bard lvl.

WM, as all prestige classes, caps at 30th lvl. Lvl 28 is the last lvl where you get +1 AB with WoC and a bonus feat so no use going past that (apart two more ki damages a day but you've got plenty already). Besides CoT (or FTR) give you bonus feats you need to get everything you want to get in the build.


If saves are not important than use the cheesey build with 1 bard lvl. Or even 1 rogue since I think you won't be maxing spellcraft either given the easy access to immunities. By going rogue you lose taunt, but you get set traps, which is a strategically useful skill.
