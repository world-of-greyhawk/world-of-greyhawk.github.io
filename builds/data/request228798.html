"Y'a pire que ne pas pouvoir, y'a savoir qu'on peut et ne jamais tenter" exalted sorceress? In low magic settings, the most powerful characters are spellcasters. Exalted sorceress or similar will be very powerful, as would a near pure wizard build (maybe a level or 2 of Rogue). In melee, a high level Cleric will be extremely tough with buffs, as would a dragon shape build (unless that is banned along with shifter - you can get Dragon Shape with druid levels without shifter, though). The spellcasters will be vulnerable to SR monk builds, and the monk will be vulnerable to melee builds and the buffing clerics. AA builds will also be quite tough thanks to the high enchant arrow power.
