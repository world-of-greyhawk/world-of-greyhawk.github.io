This build is a great tank with a ton of health, resistances and damage reduction to boot. Also has devastating criticals for the cheesy one hit dragon kills. Bard song is used to both boost your abilities and curse your enemies to give you and your companions a great advantage. You won't be able to cast 5th or 6th level spells but you will not need them anyway with the full plate and tower shield you'll be wielding.
