CATS! Interesting build.

I'd start with 18 DEX though and put the 6 points saved into CON (12) and INT(14). The way it is now, HPs are really low, even for an epic dodger with SC5. If you take my suggestion, you'll have a final -1 AB/AC but I think it's worth it. Besides skills are very important to such a kind of character. You have no trap setting skill and that's a big disadvantage. Discipline 30 is pointless, it's just wasted points that won't help you resisting anything.

Epic fiendish servant might be swapped for AS or EP, I hear it's no big deal (but ask Grimnir77, he's a BG guru). Otherwise dropping two great dexes for EP and AS would net you +1 AC in exchange for -1 Ref save. I'd consider going for SCIV and put in Crippling Strike.

Going Rogue 6/SD 4/BG 10 preepic would net you +1 Fort save. Otherwise Rogue 8/SD 4/BG 8, earlier Weapon Finesse at the cost of -1 Will save, but improved playability. Weapon Finesse as of now comes late.

No room for dual wield and KD/IKD is a real pity.

Just a few suggestion
