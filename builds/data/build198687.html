This build is an attempt at creating a flexible Dwarven Defender that not only can shrug damage, but that can also not receive some of it thanks to his clerical buffs. In addtion to being able to tank for a group, he also has a more punch in solo play and will be able to take down ennemies faster thanks to the weapon master levels. The ability to cast himself full heals is also very interesting!

The level progression is a bit ackward, but he will be solid throughout the build progression, gaining more and more abilities up to level 40.

Comments are welcomed!
