I like this build better then the other builds I made. This one does loose 2 AB, 2 AC, and 12 HPs but in comparing, I like having more spells to choose from. You end up getting less HPs but you'll have a stronger Bardic Song. You'll then be able to benifit from using any weapon with this build. Casting Armor, Protection Alignment, Bull Strength, Darkness, Ultravision, Silence, Keen, Haste, Wounding Whispers, Improved Invisible, War Cry, and Ethreal Viage or Greater Dispelling. Pop an Endurance potion with maybe some Strength and Constition equipment and you should be in good shape. This would be my choice to play these classes, it offers a good AB and plenty of buffing spells to your arsanal.

This character is playable from level 1-40 PvM. No XP penalty.
