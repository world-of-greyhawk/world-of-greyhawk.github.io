Comments:
This character is a versatile sneaker that can hit like a fighter, damage like a rogue and hide like a shadow. He is a good scout and a deadly stalker.
He has 3 fighting styles: Dual wield, sword’n board and ranged attacks.
He can produce repeated paralysing death attacks with stealth and HIPS, with the darkness-ultravision combo or by going invisible with invisibility spells. Improved knockdown let you deal death attack damage as long as the victim is down.
It is easy to play this character through the levels. The leveling aims at getting the assassin feel ASAP with effective Death Attacks and the spell like abilities. It also gets HIPS ASAP.
I put the second SD lvl at lvl 17 for a tumble skill dump.
Some people prefer taking more ranger lvls earlier for the animal companion and earlier BAB progression and early access to ITWF. In this build I choose to build up the damage dealing early. When I played the build I found that the hit ratio and # of attacks pre epic are more than adequate for a HIPSer/stealther.
Taking ranger 10 at lvl 21 let me pick an epic ranger bonus feat instead of a 3rd FE.

Like you already have figured out, SD at lvl 17 is a tumble skilldump in a series of ranger lvls after I have spent all 9 pre epic assn lvls.

The lvl split 9/2/9 @ 20 was not too evident actually.
If you take ranger10/SD2/assn8 you get BAB17 @ 20. You get a 3rd FE but lose an epic feat, so you will not be able to reach Gdex X if you want epic prowess.
You end up with a build with dex 36 and two epic feats you can spend on epic prowess and armor skin.
The end result is the same AB but +1 to AC.
The price is improved invisibility 7 lvls later (lvl 22). The death attack DC will be 1 lower and the damage will suffer 1d6 between lvl 14 and 21. The will and refl saves and the dex skills will have a -1 penalty.
I might have chosen the latter in a build "born at" lvl 40.
