I was thinking maybe monk/druid/barb... druid (nature-lover) / monk (has training and yet doesnt abuse his abilities) / and barb (when someone hurts his home..the forest...he enters a rage) .

Any suggestions are welcome and appreciated It cant be monk/druid/barb because monk needs to be lawful and barb needs to be unlawful.

Druid has a nice spell that can simulate the barbarian rage:
Blood Frenzy (From the grimoire)
Caster Level: Druid 2
Innate Level: 2 School: Transmutation
Component(s): V, S Range: Personal
Save: None Spell Resistance: No
Area of Effect/Target: Caster
Duration: 1 round / level
Description: The caster enters a rage similar to that of a barbarian. The caster gains a +2 bonus to Constitution and Strength and a +1 bonus to Will saves, while suffering a -1 penalty to AC.

You dont really need anything else than druid and monk, but if you wish you can always take Fighter for specializing in kama and get dual wielding feats OR CoT if you want to avoid the xp penalty that would come with fighter.

I would, better than using kamas give him zen archery, point black shot, rapid shot and called shot (you need, a minimum of 13 dex for this) and heavily focus on wisdom for spellcasting, AC and AB (And being able to fall out to his fists if he needs to melee).
