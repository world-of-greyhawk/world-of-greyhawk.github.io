Here's a "less conventional" character build.

She doesn't immediately focus on melee damage, and is geared more towards spellcasting. The idea was to make a Cleric/Arcane Archer character, but as it came out, of course she's not using her bow that much. Still, it's nice...

Notice that she has high Hide & Move Silently skills, so she can sneak around in stealth mode and full plate. At least it works in singleplayer

The DC for her spell saves are high, a Destruction cast by Frail can kill an Ancient Black Dragon. A dracolich can't resist her Heal.

So, in summary: fairly stealthy, capable of casting Stoneskin & Improved Invis, capable of disabling most traps and opening most locks on her own, capable of casting Wizard spells from scrolls and using wizardly gear, a fairly powerful Cleric, and an Arcane Archer.
