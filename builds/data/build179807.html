This was an attempt to make the highest AC character I could think of without using the monk class.

Race: Halfling
Alignment: CN
Classes: Bard 17 / Palemaster 13 / Red Dragon Disciple 10
Starting Stats:
STR: 6
DEX: 20
CON: 10
INT: 12
WIS: 8
CHA: 15

Lvl 1 Feat: Dodge
Lvl 1 Skills: Max Tumble, UMD, Spellcraft, Perform, Lore

All Stat increases go into DEX except 1 point into CHA.

Level Progression:
First 10 levels: Bard, keeping Tumble, UMD, Spellcraft, Perform and Lore maxed.
Lvl 11 - 20: Palemaster, putting points in Spellcraft only
Lvl 21 - 30: Red Dragon Disciple, putting points into Spellcraft only
Lvl 31 - 37: Bard. Max out Tumble, Perform. The rest can be used to max out either UMD or Spellcraft, with the remainder going into the other.
Lvl 38 - 40: Palemaster.

Feats, in order:
Mobility
Blind Fighting *
Curse Song
Weapon Focus: Light Crossbow *
Improved Critical: Light Crossbow *
Point Blank Shot *
Armor Skin
Epic Weapon Focus: Light Crossbow *
Rapid Reload *
Expertise (shows up at level 30)
Imrpoved Expertise
Great Dexterity I
Great Dexterity II

* Feats marked with an asterisk can be substituted for other feats without compromising the integrity of the build. The post-epic-level feats can be substituted for Great Dexterity which would add 1 more point of AC to the final build.

Final Stats:
STR: 14
DEX: 31
CON: 12
INT: 14
WIS: 8
CHA: 18

Naked, unbuffed armor class: 42
Fort: 22
Reflex: 31
Will: 24

I ended up with 267 HP, YMMV

AB with regular light crossbow: +35/+30/+25

Major Strengths: Using HOTU gear and a few buffs (noteably Bard Song, Mage Armor, and Cat's Grace), this character had an AC of 110 in my test build. It could only be hit by natural 20's, and was immune to criticals. Lots of handy immunities (critical hits, sleep, fire, hold, paralyze, stun).

Major Weaknesses: Weak damage. The Bard Song helps with this a bit. Also has weak Fort and Will saves. This is not a huge issue, however, because the character is immune to a lot of things that require Will saves, and to Devastating Criticals.

This is my first attempt at an epic munchkin. Any feedback would be welcome! I have seen some similar builds listed here, but the levels were different. I made this build with a particular goal in mind

If you make him 14 Bard 16 Palemaster 10 Red Dragon you gain another 2 AC from palemaster, and you can take 2 epic spells...like Epic Mage Armor. Depending on your gear this will add another 5 - 20 to your AC. I took the extra bard levels to get 40 ranks in Tumble. I will try the extra PM levels. I went with 12 bard / 18 PM / 10 RDD and took more Great Dexterity feats and Epic Mage Armor and Epic Warding. End result: AC 113, only a 3 point increase. However, the DR 50/+20 was a major asset. The mod I use to test builds has 3 ridiculously powerful characters with uber-weapons. Durok is FTR 40/WM 20 (CR 19864), Sir Gunky is a WIZ 40/ CLR 20 (CR 12306), and Morfirion is CLR 30 / ROG 30 (CR 21091). The ultimate test of a character build is to either a) survive longer than 1 or 2 rounds against the trio, or b) actually kill them, using only items that are available in HotU (no custom items). This character survived 5 or 6 rounds. Build successful! When looking at this build I notice that you put 20 as your starting dex. If you instead lowered that to 18 you would get 6 points to add to other abilities. If you added points to intelligence so it was at least 13, then you could take expertice and imp. expertice pre epic and have two free post epic feats that can be used for great dex giving you the same dexterity as you have now. though of course this would influence your feat choices pre epic so you might not want that, but I think I would go that way.
