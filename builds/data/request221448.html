.."..".. It will be easier to make this build as human.
You get one more skill point per level, and have no xp penalty.
You get more freedom when choosing levelling order.
With Int 14 you get enough skillpoints to get fair ranks in fighter skills (discipline tumble), stealth (hide move silently), caster skills(spellcraft concentration) and a couple of points for some RP skills (spot lore persuade).With this class combo, they can all be taken as class skills.
Your dex will be lower, but you'll have no problem with Epic Dodge at lvl 36 or 39.
If you go heavy on cleric, like 23 or 26 levels, you can take Great wisdom as one of your bonus feats (maybe Armor skin as the other), leaving room for Epic weapon focus, Epic dodge and Great dexterity V on your regular feats. If you start with Dex 16 and Wis 16 you can have Dex 18 and Wis 19 at level 20, ending with Dex 28 and Wis 20 at level 40.
Trickery is a good domain for this character with improved invisibility and divine trickery.
War, Strength and Travel are all worth considering as the second domain.
