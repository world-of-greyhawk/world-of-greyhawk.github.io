As for clerics and melee presence, don't worry. Buffed, you don't even need to spend much gold on items. By lvl 15 you make your own complete set with +5 items. Weapon, Armour, shield, natural(with plant domain) and deflection. On top of this, flame damage on weapon, imp invis (with trickery), healing, a lot of melee buffs and ability buffs, penalties to your enemies rolls and even attack spells. You can easily afford to wait for the RDD path if you want to. Clerics can easily hold their own, even WIS based. Undeads? Yeah, those would be fun to fight.
