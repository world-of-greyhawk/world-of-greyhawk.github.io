Crippling Strike doesn't work with ranged weapons, so that's why i dumped it. You don't really need more than 10 INT, since you get a ton of skill points anyway and you're not looking to take any feats requiring 13+ INT.

This build gets you 4 attacks per round, and if you're Hasted, you get another. Plus, PBS and Rapid Shot will net you yet another attack at only -1 AB, but +1 damage (in melee range).

EWS gives you +6 damage on all your successful attacks, which I think is worth the investment in FTR levels.

I'd recommend considering taking your 8th FTR level at level 30, so you can top up Discipline if you want the skill.
