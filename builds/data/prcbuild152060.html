[Note: This build is based off of a little thing someone mentioned on the PRC forum, but I can't remember their name]

The Shard is a pure archer, using the bard levels to buff itself. Having a massive dex gives it a decent AC and AB, although lower than a pure AA. What is its biggest advantage though is against lower AC enemies it can activate one of the Power Shot feats to add 5, 10, or 15 to damage at the cost of the same removed from AB. It can also max out UMD and Tumble, another nice benefit.
