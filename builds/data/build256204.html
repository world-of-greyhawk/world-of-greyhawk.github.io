A couple thoughts...

You might want to consider going with more cleric (say 9, or better yet 11 cleric levels) to get divine power and enough buff magic to bring your AB up a bit. Take those extra levels away from COT epic levels and you should see a decent increase in power.

Thundering Rage and Overwhelming Crit don't stack... basically it's probably not worth it to take Thundering Rage in a build with O Crit. I don't remember how it works, just that it doesn't work the way that you'd expect and is pretty disappointing.

If I were going to build this, I'd honestly ditch the charisma angle, but even if I was married to it I'd drop divine might. You need to save your turning attempts for divine shield with the weak AC in this build.
