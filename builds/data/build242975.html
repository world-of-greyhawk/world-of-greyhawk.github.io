I'm posting this because it was requested (sorta). The original build for this was a Halfling, and had 9 Monk with only 10 Rogue. I found out the hard way (yeah, I'm an idiot) that Halfling was a horrible idea for dual-wielding Kamas, they get a huge AB penalty for it. I lowered the monk in order to pick up Epic Dodge. The build gets it late, but at least it gets it at all. The 1 Monk level pre-epic, unfortunately, has to be taken in order to take the weapon focus feats in Pre-Epic. This means that you lose out on 1 BAB. It's not an amazing build, with only 54 AB vs. Favoured Enemies, but no reason not to post it, especially when I was asked. =)

I didn't list your AB with Self-Buffs, either...it's actually quite annoying to do so. You get +2 AB/AC from Cat's Grace, +1 AB from aid, and +3 AB to a weapon with Blade Thirst, bringing your naked stats up by +6 AB and +2 AC.

Enjoy! Comments and Critiques welcome, as always.
