The Hunter Assassin winds up with inherent Attack of +56 (+26 BAB, +13 AA benefit, +15 from Dex, +2 from Epic Focus) before any buffs or weapon modifications and also has a full 4 attacks.

Defensive bonus to AC is +15 before any buffs or armor mods, although restricted to Robes to get full effect.

Darkness and Improved Invisibility are available as class ability spells.

Death Attack contributes +5d6 on first few attacks at range and also a good chance of Paralyzation (which should be a kill given the potency of the Longbow and also the significant possibility of chain-paralyzing the target.)

Poison can create further havoc for the opponent to deal with.

Finally, Use Magic Device and Tumble can both be maxed at 40.

Sounds like a very interesting build for powergamers or roleplayers.
