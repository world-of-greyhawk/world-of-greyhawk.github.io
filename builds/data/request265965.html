Hi guys im looking for a good lvl up of a dex rogue fighter wm Im looking for a minimum of 21rogue for high sneaks i also want dual weild in daggers and highest possible ab and ac without nerfing one or the other please. Rogue/FTR/WM dexers with high AB are tricky: you'll want high WM lvls (16+) and high Rogue lvls (19+) and you'll end up feat starved.

I can advice for a split like Rogue 19/FTR 2/WM 19, Halfling getting dual wielding feats, ED, SCV, AB 48, AC 32 naked but missing out on KD/IKD, Toughness, WS/EWS (and IC, but that's not important).

Going FTR 4/WM 16 will give you one more feat at the cost of 1 AB.

Alternatively Rogue 16/FTR 8/WM 16, ED (but no SC), Crippling Strike, WS/EWS, dual wielding, AB 48, naked AC 33 and Toughness or KD in addition to a much earlier Weapon Finesse for playability.

Going for just WM 7 is IMO not worth: a high SAs build doesn't need the crits.
