Main reasons are due to the chance of preserving your BAB pre-epic, by not taking Bard until 21st at the earliest. You gain more Cleric spells overall, as well as 2 points of Spell Penetration and longer duration of spells.

What Domains are you using? Your buffed stats don't appear to use the Divine Power Spell, which would give you 4 attacks per round for a start.

You can manage to squeeze in both Epic Mage Armor and Epic Warding with only 16 PM levels. So with 18 it should be even simpler.

Still thinking about it... DEX based is not viable in this type of build. Test out STR based on the same build type and see how it fares.
