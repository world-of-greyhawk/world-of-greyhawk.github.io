This combo has up to 882 HP. He has good saves (Fort 38, Ref 28, Will 30) - even better against spells (+6) and traps (+1), Deflect Arrows against archers, Improved evasion for those nasty AoE spells, can't be flanked and invisible/hidden do not get bonus to hit him, Defensive stance for even more HP and better saves and better AC, he's immune to diease/poison, has physical DR of 30/-, and has 10 points of Resistance to all energy forms (except fire where he's got 15).

He may not kill you with his weapon, but you might die from boredom before -you- kill -him-
