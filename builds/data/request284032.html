The thing is regularly it's useless to take more than a single Shadowdancer level if you already take Rogue, you want Hide in Plain Sight, and that's all you need.

My advice is taking more Cleric, because most combat buffs cap at Cleric level 15, Darkfire caps until level 20, which I think it's the last one to cap, plus, you'll need the levels for spell duration.

13 Rogue levels (in order to take the bonus feats: Improved Evasion and Defensive Roll) and 25 DEX allow you to take Epic Dodge, which might be the most important feat to take. After that, you also gain bonus feats at Rogue levels 16 and 19, which makes it more worthwhile than taking Assassin.

I'm a powerbuilder, and I take you want help in making your build stronger, so I'd take a Cleric 20 / Rogue 19 / Shadowdancer 1, but I'm not very well suited to give non-powerbuilding advice.
