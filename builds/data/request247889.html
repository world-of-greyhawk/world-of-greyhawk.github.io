As a caveat to Finn's suggestion, I'd decide between either focussing on melee or ranged weapons. I don't see the use of taking PBS if you're not going to go the extra step for Rapid Shot and don't also have Focus in your ranged attack. If you go the ranged route, then drop the KD feats. If you go the melee route, then drop PBS and pick up something else (Blind Fight would be my suggestion).

*edit* Don't bother with ESF:Hide either, since you've got Invisibility at your disposal.
