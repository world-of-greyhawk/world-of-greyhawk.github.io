Here he comes: The Black Dragon - an evil Spellsword


This Character does really well in OC +AddOns and playable 1-40 PvM. Its very versatile since you can fight and cast, and a whole lot of fun to play. Im not so sure about PvP, but i think he should do really good, especially in low to mid magic worlds.


Advantages
- lvl 9 Spells with auto still I-III
- Devastating Critical with really good DC (40-46)
and good Range: 15-20 (spell: keen)-> 30%=once per round
- Devine Might and Shield (6-12)
- good Damage with buffs and strengts bonuses
- Decent AC (Thx to fullplate, buffs, and Divine Might)
- Epic Warding

Disadvantages
- low Hitpoints (348)
- very few skillpoints, lacks in disciplin
