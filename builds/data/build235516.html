Well, first I would change your levelling order - you are losing 2 BAB by going Druid 10/Cleric 9/SD 1 pre-epic. Go Drui 12/Cleric 8 or Druid 8/Cleric 12 pre-epic, take SD 1 at level 21, and you gain 2 AB (brings it to 15, so you still get the extra attack at full AB from DP).

Second, I would probably skip the Spell Penetration feats. With only a max of 24 caster levels, you can only get to 30 penetration anyway, so I would just skip those. When you face something with high SR, buff, change into DS, and melee. I would take Empower Spell (nice for buffs), and KD/IKD or Disarm/Imp Disarm (both excellent feats for a dragon). I would also bump Int to 14 to qualify for them (will help with skills as well, but drop Dex to 12). If you drop Combat Casting, you can take both (but not Empower). In epic, replace Epic Spell Pen with Epic Prowess, Armor Skin, or Improved Crit: Unarmed.

Use the extra skill points to max Hide and MS (without those, HiPS is worthless, and you gave up all that cheesy monk AC to get HiPS, so make it count). With no Discipline, you will want to make KD-immunity items a priority, especially when in human form.
