Congratulations, Gammata, on your first build in the Guild. It certainly is different. A few things you may want to add are things like base saves, Playable 1-40, and intended environment.

As far as the build goes, I would suggest maybe spell foci in a particular school of magic. That, or maybe finding a way to get Epic spells. You could actually focus the Sorcerer on the no-save/direct damage spells like Ikes Yikes and what-not, and thus leave Charisma at 20, and make a more concerted effort on the Intelligence front to pump up your DCs in other spells. You could then go Sorc18/Wiz22, or Sorc19/Wiz21 so that you could squeeze a few Epic Spells out of the whole process.

Lowering the Charisma by 6 could consequently raise your Int to at least 30 with room for 1-2 Epic spells, and maybe an Epic Spell Focus.
