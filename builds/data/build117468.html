What I really see as the strengths of this character, as unenhanced by any equipment are:
- good AC 38 (naked, no stats enhanced, mind you)
- death touch x 3/day DC 27
- all arcane spells, with a decent spell slot allowance (enough to arm against any high level baddies in any situation)
- unarmed Monk combat skills (offensive and defensice)that can easily take on the vast majority of monsters
- Immunities: Critical Hits (PM), Disease (Monk), Stun (PM), hold (PM), paralyzation (PM)
- Summon Greater Undead, which at level 30 is Demi Lich, Wiz 21, that makes for a badass pet
- Undead Graft x 9/day (paralyze opponent)

With some fairly decent AC bonus robes and wis gear, this character's AC is 74, which is all but untouchable to any standard monster, and with an immunity to critical hits, few monsters are able to put up much of a fight. And take into consideration the good hide and move silently, this character is able to strike from the shadows and slink back into them with relative ease.
