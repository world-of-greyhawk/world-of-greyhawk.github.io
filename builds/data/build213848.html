Hi, if I were you I would take the skill points out of listen (as there is no point of having this skill unless you take spot as well) and put it into spellcraft, and for this reason you can drop the skill focuses for better feats. Instead of using epic toughness use great constitution so that the -40hp is removed, giving you the equivalent results for the hp (if not higher). This will also increase your fortitude saves. Dump 1 druid level at the end of this build, say 39/40 so that you can max out spellcraft and animal empathy. This build has great potential with some nice roleplaying possiblities. Try moving the levels around so that you have a higher class level of druid so that your buffing spells cant get dispelled easily.
