Even in a party, 6 str and 8 con is gonna hurt you alot as you level up.

There's no point in starting with 16 cha - you can free up 4 base stat points to start with 12 con right there.

I find it's kind of counterproductive to be a buff-centric build that's not over the dispel cap. I'd suggest you siphon 3 of those SD levels off and put them into bard levels so that your buffs can't be dispelled except by disjunction.

And since you're over the dispel cap now and because bards get both displacement and improved invisibility as class spells... you can drop SC 5 - that'll free up a lot of feats. You can still be disjunctioned but... freeing five epic feats is a pretty reasonable tradeoff I think.

Those extra feats can go to EWF, epic prowess, armor skin and two great dexes.

Drop lingering song and take lasting inspiration earlier, consider dropping extra music. Get a weapon focus in there instead.

Now you still won't do much damage but at least you can hit things if you have to.
