Thanks all Here's what I've come up with. You probably could do a Barb/WM build, but you'd probably want Fighter as the 3rd class rather than Rogue, as you really need the feats. Thus, I decided to go with Rogue/Barb/Fighter, and I think it works out pretty well.

Rogue 13/Barbarian 21/Fighter 6
(Rogue 4/Barb 16 pre-epic)

Stats

Starting

Str 17
Dex 13
Con 14
Int 14
Wis 8
Cha 8

Ending

Str 30
Dex 13
Con 14
Int 14
Wis 8
Cha 8

Feats

1 Power Attack, Cleave
3 Weapon Focus: Greatsword
6 Great Cleave
9 Disarm
12 Knockdown
15 Improved Critical
18 Toughness
21 Great Str I, Epic Weapon Focus: Greatsword *
22 Improved Disarm *
24 Weapon Spec, Epic Weapon Spec *
27 Great Str II
30 Great Str III, Improved Evasion **
33 Epic Skill Focus: Intimidate, Overwhelming Critical *
36 Devastating Critical, Crippling Strike **
39 Terrifying Rage

* Fighter bonus feat
** Rogue bonus feat

Note: With only 21 Barbarian levels, Terrifying Rage might not prove useful against higher level opponents. If you wanted to, you could subsitute Thundering Rage for Terrifying and substitute Epic Prowess for the Epic Skill Focus feat.
