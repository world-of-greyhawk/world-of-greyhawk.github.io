This is a tribute to one of the Founders of DnD Gary Gygax, who sadly passed on recently.

His vision and imagination has given us, the player, many worlds to explore, many characters to play and ultimately enabled a niche market to become a more consolidated force amonst games and gamers.

If it was not for him, then games like NwN, the Elder Scrolls and countless others, both on- and offline would never have been possible.

May your soul find peace and may your D20 roll forever, we salute you. In memoriam
