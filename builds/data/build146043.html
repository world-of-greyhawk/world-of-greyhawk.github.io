Draconic Avenger has good saving throws due to his Divine Grace. With his decent level in sorc and a charisma level he can effectively cast alot of spells. He uses the power of the might Scimitar to get off a high critical when he uses his Keen edge spell and will ravange his enemies with Devastating critical. With his high defense of 55 natural and concelament bonus from improved invisiblity he will effectively dogde many attacks. Defense comes from the fact that he can where a Full Plate and a Tower Sheild. He can also cast the might Epic Mage armor to boost him up. If enemies happen to get past the Defense they have to contend with the damage reduction from Epic Warding.

This Character is effectively immune to:
Fire(RDD)
Sleep(RDD)
Paralysis(RDD)
Mind Effect Spells(Sorc Spell)

he is also hard to knock down do to his high Discipline.

Things to look for to help this character are things that give Charisma bonuses, Strengh bonuses, Immune to Critical Hit, and Evasion/Improved Evasion.

I don't think this character is designed to tank and cast spells until you've achieved the Auto Still Spell feats.

Early on, you could play as a buffing Paladin (strip and buff before combat, armor up and wade in to melee)up to level 13 when you really start taking Sorc levels. At that point you'll probably want to sell off your armor, run around in robes and play as a burly sorceror. Once you achieve the Auto Still Spell feats you can start casting spells without arcane failure.

As Martin suggested, you could take Still Spell feat early and then cast a few Stilled spells while in armor. Perhaps you could take Still Spell at level 3? I'm not sure if it's available on a Paladin level.

Regardless, if you're going to take this build onto a PW I strongly encourage you to level it up offline in a trainer mod to make sure it develops as you'd like it to. If you want to do this with your PW character, you can Save your online character to your local vault and then level up that version offline. Take notes of the development and keep them handy as the online version of your characted advances.

If any of this isn't clear (or is incorrect) point it out. Good luck.
