=======

Focused on Stone Golem Form.

There is one other build with this level layout Defender Z (Druid 5 / Shifter 10 / Monk 25)
by kulto, but it is incorrectly built in the assumption that WF Unarmed carried over to the Construct form. I also chose different feats.


This build has an EXCELLENT spot.
AC will be pretty darn good too. base 49 in Stone Golem form, not including any spell buffs or items.
Disc will be 64 in Golem Form.
SR is 37
Stun Fist DC is 39 (and lots of them)
Quivering Palm DC 31
Speed +80%
AB will be 37
DR 15/+3
DI 25% slashing
DI 50% piercing
DV 50% acid
Deflect Arrows
IKD
Concealment
+8 initiative
lots of immunities because of Construct Form.


Note: Weapon Focus Unarmed will not work when shifted. That is why I did not take those feats. Prowess works. Improved Crit Unarmed also would merge, but I did not take it.


Only Armor Stats merge for Stone. Use Iron Golem if you want Items to merge.


Suggested equipment:

Acid protection armor, shield or Helm.
Regeneration a/s/h
big +5(or better) robe/light/medium armor

Since you have a selection of 3 Constructs to choose from, you can tailor your equipment to best suit your combat foe.

I chose to take 2 Epic Fire Resistances because you tend to see lots of people with flames on their weapons.

I also chose to bump my SR by two instead of improving my stunning fist DC.

It's true that I could shave off some Monk levels and increase Druid, but I wanted as much SR and Speed as I could get since Constructs are slow walkers.
