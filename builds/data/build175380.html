I call her the short death monk because her hands can kill instantly. (Even if it is only on a 19 or 20)

Level distribution is simple- 4 levels of Fighter, then take the rest as a Monk, aside from one more Fighter level at level 39, for the Epic Weapon Specialization in Unarmed Strike. (which is awesome for adding +4 to damage)

Starting Stats:
Str: 16 (26)
Dex: 14
Con: 12
Wis: 12 (14)
Int: 14
Cha: 8

Essential Feats:
WF (Unarmed Strike)
Dodge, Mobility
Blind Fight
Weapon Specialization (Unarmed Strike)
Improved Crit (Unarmed Strike)
Circle Kick
Expertise
Spring Attack
Whirlwind Attack
Epic WF (Unarmed Strike)
Great Strength III
Overwhelming Crit (level 27)
Devestating Crit (level 33)
Epic Weapon Specialization (Unarmed Strike)

Skills:
Max the usual Hide, Move Silently, and Tumble, and put 21 into Taunt. 20 is still a high enough Taunt to affect some people.

Saves:
Fort- 23
Reflex- 24
Will- 21
AC: 27 (low for a Monk, I know)
BA: 26

Attack: 1-20 +14
Spell Resistance: 51 (with Improved Spell Resistance III)
---
This build suffers from a low AC and fairly low saves, but with 6 attacks per round unhasted and a minimum of 15 damage per hit, I think it is a fun mage-killer. Focus on obtaining AC increasing cloaks and Universal Saving Throw-increasing rings or boots. As this is not a Long Death Monk, bracers of armor might be preferred to Long Death gloves.

The Improved Spell Resistance can be swapped for Epic Toughness feats, as the build is also fairly low on HP.

Sure wish there was a way to Keen one's hands.
