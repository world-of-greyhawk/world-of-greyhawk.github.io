I know you can get some nice boots or amulets that boost Con, but for a monk those slots are perhaps better used for monk boots (which give AC and a +3 Dex i think) and amulets of wisdom (again for better AC). While Imp. Expertise is a nice-to-have feat for a dual-wielder, it is not a requirement for Weapon Master.
