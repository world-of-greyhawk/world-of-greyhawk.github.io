Introduction:
Nature's Punisher

Goodies:
  • Insane damage
  • High AB
  • All player races (or seven "random" monsters for PvM) as FEs
  • Early BoE
  • Crippling Strike
  • High Critical threat
  • KD to generate Sneak attacks
  • Decent Stealth skills for a non-DEXer

Baddies:
  • Low AC
  • Low saves
  • Looking at the low AC, not enough HP
  • Not too many skillpoints
  • 40% XP penalty level 25 forth and 20% 24-25


This is basically my version of Grimnir's superb Nature's Executioner build.
