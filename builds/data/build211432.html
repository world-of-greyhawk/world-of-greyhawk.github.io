He's immune to fear, has very good buffs (Pally lvl 4 and Sorc lvl 4), dispells on hit 50%, and has Dev Crit with a good DC. I suppose it would make more sense to go with scimitar/shield but I'm a sucker for two-handers. The build doesnt change anyway. He's also got a good AB + taunt + devine strike (+ true strike if ur really desperate), so he won't miss often. Unfortunately he also won't be missed often either, and with 330 health that doesn't bode well. Still, he has a good LoH and many defensive buffs to see him through, provided he doesn't get dispelled himself.
