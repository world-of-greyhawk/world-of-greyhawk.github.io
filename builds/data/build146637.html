End result is you have a character with monk speed and many attacks a round (5 main hand, 2 off hand, 1 flurry of blows and 1 haste). The High AC and epic dodge forces a needed 20 to hit in most 1 on 1 vs. player or monster. The character also has many of the monks abilities with the exception of Ki strike 2 and 3, empty body, and perfect self. The empty body is easily replaced by the assassin improved invisibility.

Max HP is 384, min is 160 (12d8 + 5d6 + 23d6 + 80 from CON + 40 toguhness.

AB with normal kamas is +33/+30/+27/+24/+21 and +33/+30 offhand.

AC is a base of 30 with normal clothes

Saves are
Fort: 20
Reflex: 33
Will 21

Finally has a SR of 22 which helps, but isn't great against any caster with quite a few caster levels. Fortunately HIPS and Death attacks help with them, and they get shredded up by the kamas very fast as well.
