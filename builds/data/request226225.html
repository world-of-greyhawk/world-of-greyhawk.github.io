I know it's not elf, but you can make the same build with elf with same starting stats. Keep present you lose -1 AB, -1 AC, -1 saves and that you've got an xp penalty for most of your career if you go elf (lvl 4, 10, 14, 15, 18-40).
Drop SC II or IV for Crippling Strike if you wanna be nastier.
If you wanna go dual wielding put in TWF, ambidexterity and ITWF in place of three feats of your choice (KD/IKD/called shot, f.e. ... a difficult call to make)
See if you like it otherwise try pulse cap's search engine to find the build that fits you.
