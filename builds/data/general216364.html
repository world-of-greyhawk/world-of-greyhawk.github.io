Experience is the mother of good judgement; bad judgement is the father of experience. A level 10 sorcerer can cast 6/6/6/6/5/3 spells per day. A level 15 sorcerer can cast 6/6/6/6/6/6/6/4 spells per day. So as a sorcerer(10)/pale master(10) you can't cast any level 6/7 spells but you should be able to cast 6/6/6/6/6/6 spells per day and you should be able to fill the level 6 spell slot with metamagic (CHA 16 is not enough to use the level 7 slot as well).
