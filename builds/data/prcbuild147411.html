Alternatively, you can be a MUCH faster progression and do this lvling scheme instead; Wiz 6 / Eldritch Knight 16 / Arcane Archer 18; but you lose 1 ab/dmg. But you get epic spells at lvl 26 instead of lvl 40. So... the lvling would look like:
Leveling: 1-5 Wizard, 6-9 Eldritch Knight, 10-12 Arcane Archer, 13-18 Eldritch Knight, 19-20 Arcane Archer, 21-26 Eldritch Knight, 27-39 Arcane Archer, 40 Wizard.

Anyways; pros and cons of the build.

Pros
----
Cast as a lvl 24 wiz or as a lvl 25 wiz (depending if did fast progression or slow progression) and this makes your archery ferocious especially when combined with epic spells (which, with epic skill focus, you can cast most of them; +10 int mod with int buffs from spells; 43 base, and +10 again from esf. That's 63. Pretty good, enough to make most of the checks)
Pretty high AB
Pretty high AC

Cons
----
low hp
slow progression
low DC on offensive spells
