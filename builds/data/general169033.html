Peace Your max dex bonus is dictated directly by the type of armor you are wearing. One easy way to find out is examine the armor. Full Plate for Example normally has a max dex bonus of 1, meaning if you wear fullplate, any dex bonus after +1 will be negated. (that is where you see a little red one) If the max dex bonus is +3 and your dex bonus is normally +5, you will see a little red 3. The lighter the armor the more dex bonus allowed generally.
