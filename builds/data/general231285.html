Well, it won't hurt, but her reflex save is already 28 unbuffed, 34 with buffed Cha (which he said she has), she should be wearing Boots of the Sun Soul (+3 to Dex), and an empowered Cat's Grace will boost that by 3-7 more (so +6-+10 to Dex already, or +5-+9 even using a potion). With a 3 from the Cat's Grace, he already has +6 to Dex, and a reflex save of 37. I wouldn't make further dex-boosting items a high priority. Fine if he has them, but not critical to the build by a long shot. If he's wearing heavy armor and a tower shield, he is going to be tight on carrying capacity so I would probably put Str-boosting as a higher priority.
