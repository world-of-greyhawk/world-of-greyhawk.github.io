Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

Here a couple of characters for those times when you can use an immunity to critical hits item and buff your saving throws. Under this situation, you can assume everyone else is immune to crits and has high saves, so these characters can demolish enemies pretty quickly without crits or spells.

It slices, it dices, it make Julliene french fries (out of Julliene) 6 different ways. The first character is the "Vegomatic". With 10 attacks, enough oomph to punch through most damage reduction (thanks to favoured enemy, bane of enemies and epic weapon specialization) and evasion, he's one mean character.
