Start with these stats:

STR 17
DEX 14
CON 12
INT 14
WIS 8
CHA 8

You have one point left. Put it in CON, WIS, or CHA, whichever you fancy. Instead of raising INT at level 20, just raise STR at every stat boost. Same net effect for STR, but you end up with more skill points from the get-go.

I'd also suggest dropping that 21st level of FTR and picking up another Rogue. This will allow you to spread your 4 Rogue levels around a bit to do some regular skill dumping.

I know this would lead to a loss of 1 BAB, but I'd really consider taking at least one Rogue level pre-epic (and possibly two). This will give you earlier skill dumps and will make the character far more playable from the ground up. Having ranks in UMD before level 22 will give your character far more versatility and survivability and be worth way more than that extra 1 BAB, IMO.

I'm also in the camp that feels there's no need for both Spot and Listen. I'd max one only and put the saved skill points into something else.

Improved Disarm so late in the build? I'd try to fit it in far earlier. Disarm is pretty untenable alone, especially when not using a large weapon. I'd also seriously consider Avado's advice and drop the Disarm feats altogether and go with more WM. And look at trying tog et Dev Crit ealrier.
