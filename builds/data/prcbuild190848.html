Comments and suggestions are welcome as ever nice build. I'd personally use wizard instead of sorc, hierophant makes a good dc booster for generalist wizards, since it applies across all schools. Those extra 4 bonus feats will make a big difference. (max focus in a second school of magic, plus better metamagic, perhaps) I'd probably also go 13 cleric/5 hierophant/22 wizard. the cleric spells will be used for buffs and the wizard for offense, so I want as many wizard levels for damage (horrid wilting needs 25 levels to max) and spell penetration. Also, having less cleric saves on the wisdom requirement for spells, so more towards the arcane casting stat.

I'd probably use yuan-ti as you've suggested, since otherwise the stats are spread pretty thin.
