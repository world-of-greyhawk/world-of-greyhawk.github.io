This character being kind of a rogue-knight really only makes sense as a church assassin. If you think about it, not that far-fetched a concept. The Roman Catholic Curch, at the height of its power, certainly made use of spies and assassins. And any government or powerful organisation would recognize the need for assassination as a political tool.


Advantages

-Devastating critical with potential 12-20 threat range.
-Improved evasion and fairly good saves. He's pretty spell resistant
-Hide in Plain Sight
-Has Use Magic Devices. Can cast spells from any scroll.

Disadvantages

-39 AB while dual wielding. Lowsy, but quite good for a rogue
-Low hit points.
-Low damage. He could have some trouble damaging opponents with damage reduction.

Variant Comparison

-Variant 1 has an additional +1 to AB and damage with each weapon.
-Variant 2 has an additional +2 to AC, and improved knockdown.
