What would be your build of choice to play in a high magic world?

High magic in this case mean:
Weapon: +20
Armor parts: +15 (a few have more) with damage immunities
you'll be able to max-buff your important stats from items
Important bosses are immune to KD, crits, mind effecting spells, ...

Casters and archers usually don't survive enemy contact long, since the equipment for the doesn't have the needed immunities ...
Which pretty much leaves some kind of melee build.

In case someone want to check for more infos, I'm talking about the Halls of the Worlds PW (http://www.halloftheworlds.com/) ... Fighter 10/Bard 2/WM 28
