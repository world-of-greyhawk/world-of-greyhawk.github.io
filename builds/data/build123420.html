Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

I gave an earlier idea of how I would do a ranger/assassin but here it is in full detail. The first variant is the ranger/assassin, the second is a ranger/rogue. Feel free to critique as you would like.

I'd go with a panther companion on both of these builds. The hit points are kind of low but okay (what do you expect from an elf?). Both builds have low saves, but this is unavoidable. This character has serious feat deficiency but should be okay. I went for maces because I didn't have the feats to spend on improved criticals and very few enemies have bludgeoning resistance. The second build also has an XP penalty.
