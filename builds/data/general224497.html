I thought that when you win the initiate you get them caught flatfooted and got an AoO. If you win initiative, your first flurry will catch them flat-footed, IIRC, but you don't get an AoO.

AoOs are generated by:

- opponent making a ranged attack
- opponent making unarmed attack (without Imp Unarmed Strike feat)
- opponent casting a spell
- opponent using a scroll, wand, rod
- opponent using a spell-like ability
- opponent running
- opponent using certain feats
- opponent drinking a potion (depending on your difficulty settings)
- opponent moving in the combat radius

there may be one or two more I haven't remembered.

Just because he's flat-footed, it doesn't mean you'll get an AoO.
