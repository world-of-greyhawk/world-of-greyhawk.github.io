Time to move some of my builds into here, I suppose. . .

I made this one to be a versatile fighter, melee or ranged. It can deliver its damage equally well with Mace or LongBow; if the enemy is sneak immune or has high DR, there is Epic WSpec on both weapons, and there is also Power Attack for melee (+2 damage from STR too). Called Shot works for both weapons too. I picked those weapons because I wanted two damage types; bludgeoning particularly to handle undead. The Exotic feat is taken early to allow any other finessed weapon in a pinch as well. And Epic Dodge for defense, of course.

The 10 COT levels were chosen for the feats, and to increase saving throws by 5 over a typical Fighter/Rogue build. Those COT levels went in early to get that boost. These Fighter/Rogue types normally have BAD Will saves. . .to also help that, I took Slippery Mind (Iron Will or Spellcraft points might be another idea). The Charisma sucks, but if a some ability-boosting items were worn, there might be some small use made out of some of the other COT feats.

I made it a single-wield build but frankly, if there is one thing this build ISNT missing, its feats. A version with that could be made very easily, if someone decides what to drop. . .
