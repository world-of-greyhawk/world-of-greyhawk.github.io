Two wrongs don't make a right but three lefts do. To elaborate, say Elf (you could go half-elf to avoid the XP penalty) With base attributes
STR 10
DEX 16
CON 12
INT 14
WIS 8
CHA 16

Go Bard 4/Rogue 6/AA 10 pre-epic, for pre-epic feats: point blank shot, rapid shot, called shot, weapon focus (longbow), curse song, lingering song, toughness. For epic feats: great dexterity I-IV, epic weapon focus (longbow), epic prowess, improved evasion, defensive roll and epic dodge. Spend all ten attribute bonuses on DEX.

You should finish with a 47 AB, unbuffed with a normal longbow unbuffed. With bard song and war cry you can raise that to 49 and with curse song you can lower your opponents AC by 5 (effectively raising your AB to 54, 60 on a successful taunt). Your damage should be 6+1D8 peircing +7D6 sneak attack (+3 bludgeoning and +2 slashing with bard song and war cry) with a normal longbow.
