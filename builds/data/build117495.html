It might be better to go with rogue instead of assasin if you are just looking for the sneak attack. Death Attack is 10 + lvl + int bonus. So your DA is only 23 without intelligence buffs. I would recommend cutting the ranger lvls down to ten which would give you a DA of 33. You could add items/spells to give you 39. That is a little on the weak side but close to giving you more death attacks.

But if you want just the sneak attack it would be better to start as a rogue with less int. You will end up with more skills and not have to increase int. (Or even strt with lower int.)
