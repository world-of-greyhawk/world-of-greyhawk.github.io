cool build. I'm always fond of divine might/shield builds.

bard 26/rdd 10/bg 4 gets one more bonus feat and a better bard song. rdd 15 gives +1 ac, but so does 25 bard if you've got the perform skill high enough. fewer hitpoints though.

The extra feat could go towards great cha, so you can lower your starting cha to 15 and you'll get two more extra starting stat points to spend (+2 con would work nicely to make up for the lost hps).

Alternatively you could grab blind-fight and keep the existing stat spread. I hate trying to beat down invis or concealed guys without blind-fight.

going 26 bard instead of rdd also allows you to get lasting impression at lvl 36 instead of 39, which could make leveling easier. The problem I had was to find a way to obtain 80 in perform skill if i chose to go bard26, and i must admit, i didnt find it ( unless perhaps sacrificing all the free feats to get epic perform skill ) Good point. It really depends on equipment, since that is the only way to reach epic bardsong perform levels.

If you can max your cha and if you take 43 points in perform you can get to 57, which is a lvl 21 bard song. You don't get that +1 ac, but you get +30 temporary hps and +9 skills. You also get 6 more curse songs to use before resting.

With a +30 perform item (epic minstrel gloves) you'd exceed the 80 perform needed for a lvl 26 bardsong. If the right equipment is available you won't need any skill focus (perform) feats.
