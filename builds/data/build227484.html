I like this guy. Due to his low damage potential, he's probably not the most effective build around, but he's pretty reliable at what he does: Sneaking, hitting, and not being hit. i would suggest to drop epic prowress and get blind fight instead Can't. It's a Weapon Master epic bonus feat, so you have to take an epic feat with it (from the Weapon Master's bonus feat list). The other viable option there is Improved Whirlwind, but I don't like Whirlwind Attacks, so I went with Epic Prowess. If you can live without your sole level of Shadowdancer, I'd replace it with a single fighter level pre-epic at level2, enabling you to pick weapon finesse 16 levels earlier, and then at level 18 you could pick blind-fight. And since 20 levels of rogue don't give you anything more than 19 levels, you could grab a second level of fighter somewhere in epic or pre-epic to nab an extra feat, something like toughness maybe, for an extra 40HP, or KD.
