Well, assuming that the restrictions to feats and epic feats only apply to items and not characters, I would say this looks like a good server for either mages, shifters, fighter/rogues, or fighter/clerics.

No spell immunities means mages will be the toughest - no way to avoid their damage.

Druid/shifters will do well (assuming they are not banned) since the abilities granted by the forms will often be better than the +2/+3 limits.

A dex-based fighter/rogue will have high AC in a low-magic environment, and a hig AB. Enough rogue levels will let them do a lot of sneak damage, and no KD-immunity means they will be able to knock you down effectively for lots of sneak attacks.

Finally, a Cleric/fighter or Cleric/fighter/monk will be able to buff themselves up considerably beyond the low-magic limits, making them quite dangerous.

Pure melee types will fare poorly in a low-magic setting, since they won't be able to either protect themselves from magic nor get the equipment to penetrate stoneskin/greater stoneskin or hit high AC from magic buffers.

TM If restrictions are item restrictions only (as it would make sense) then that's the reign of spellcasters and/or shapeshifters. Go with a nuking cleric, or a dragon druid (yes you can get DS by 30th lvl, or even earlier) they are lots of fun. Smart multiclassing is mandatory
