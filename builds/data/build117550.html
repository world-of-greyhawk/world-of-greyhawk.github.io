I tossed together a Barbarian 26 / Dwarven Defender 14 with a total damage redux of 24. Everything seemed to stack, but with the stats at the character creation, I was limited in what I wanted to do. Since I _needed_ 13 Dex for Dodge and Dwarven Defender, I had to blow feats to raise Str and Con. Also, I couldn't take ___ Rage as a Dwarven Defender, so I had to switch back to Barb for those feats. Of course, with flip flopping alignment in the mid 30s, this isn't really that feasable from 1 to 40 campaign-wise I guess. Finally, there was probably a way to ditch at least one of the +stat feats, but it didn't occur to me.
