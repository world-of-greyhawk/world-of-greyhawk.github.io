Since it's RDD 10 builds time on the forum I decided to add my own contribution. This build was developed indipendently but it is similar to the Grizzled Dwarflord's Dwarven Warmonger. Also in grizz's OP there was a talk about going with my same lvl split but nothing specific was posted.
If alignment changes are an issue take all the 6 preepic bard lvls in a row then take the last 3 bard lvls as your last character lvls. This way you get just 2 alignment changes in your career (from non lawful to lawful and back again...pick neutral something for a start).
RDD lvls are taken early on to make use of the stats boost asap, if that is not a concern one might want to change the preepic leveling to bard 6/RDD 4/DwD 10 for +1 AB (saves stay the same) but you need to take 6 bard lvls straight away.
The Draconic Wall is a stout defender able to stand massive damage (DR 24/- and around 700 HP) with ease and can shake off many spells (good ST). His hardened skin and acrobatic skill make him harder to hit and he can move around in full plate and tower shield without provoking AoO. His sight inspires his allies and demoralizes his enemies (lvl 8 bard song+curse song) and his sharp tongue has often been deadlier than his axe (maxed taunt). He has the skill to use almost all the magical devices/scrolls he might find (35 UMD) and has some spells of his own to get ready for battle.
His training as a dwarven defender has granted him the power to stand his ground against hordes of enemies (defensive awareness, no flanking!), calling to his inner strenght to become a fiercer and harder to kill warrior (+4 dodge AC, +2 STR, +4 CON, +2 saves, 11 times/day).
And besides all the rest this dwarf his damn goodlooking ... oh well, wings apart
Enjoy it!
