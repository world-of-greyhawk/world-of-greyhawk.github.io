The Divine Wyrm is a righteous creature who strives to protect all things natural from both evil creatures and advancing industrialization. It will defeat any foe with fearsome power, a magnificent dragon who has studied both the magic of nature and the more mystical fighting arts from around the world.

This character build will not be good for high magic worlds, but rather for low magic worlds where its AC and AB arent easily topped with use of magic items.
