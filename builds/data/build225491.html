Looks fine enough

Though I have to ask: why are you boosting Constitution in pre-epic and start picking DR feats so late as 32nd level? I mean yeah, you get more HP early on but seriously. I suggest you either boost Strength in the beginning and plan your Constitution to hit 21 at level 32 or start going for EDR first thing in epic. It's a minor thing.

Then, EWF > EP (+2 > +1, aye? ), pick EWF first.

And lastly, I'd take Armor Skin in place of Energy Resistance. Though 10/- fire resistance helps if there's PvP - people tend to use Darkfire/Flame Weapon. I guess mobs could use them, too. If that's what you're going after, take two of them. If not, I recommend Armor Skin (now I'm repeating myself ).

Surely, without war there would be no loss - hence, no mourning, no grief, no pain, no misery... WM 8 gives you one more ki damage per day.
I'd go FTR 6/WM 8/DwD 26, and change preepic lvling to FTR 6/WM 6/DwD 8 or FTR 6/WM 8/DwD 6 to improve saves. Also, why WIS 9? Put WIS down to 8 and increase DEX to 14, for a +1 ref save. I'd suggest you to take armor skin instead of epic energy resistance, AS it's on the DwD bonus feats list.
If you keep 7 FTR then you could drop toughness to make room for WS at 4th lvl and take EWS at 21st lvl.
You could actually consider going FTR 10/WM 8/DwD 22. You get 1 more feat at the cost of 3 DR and 8 HPs, WS/EWS would be easier to get.

Just a few tweaks, the build is a solid tank.

And thanks for the help. You & Fin made it an even greater one. Listen is something of a pointless skill in my view - anything that can be detected with Listen can be detected with Spot, and Spot can detect some things that Listen can't.

i'd drop Listen altogether and start with stats of
Str 16
Dex 13
Con 18
Int 13
Wis 8
Cha 6
which means you'll be able to drop a Great Con feat in epics.

instead of taking Toughness on level one, take Mobility, and shift up all the other WM feats. you'll be left with a spare feat on level 6, and you can take Weapon Specialization for +2 damage.

take Toughness on level 9, Improved Crit on 12, and the two Disarm (or KD) feats on 15 and 18. you miss out Blind Fight but it's worth it for Weapon Spec, tbh.

then, if you go fighter 6 / WM 7 / DwD 7 pre-epic, you can take Epic Weapon Focus on 21, take level 24 as your 7th Fighter level, and pick up Epic Weapon Specialization, for a further +4 damage.

so basically you'll be dropping Listen and Blind Fight for +6 damage on every hit.
