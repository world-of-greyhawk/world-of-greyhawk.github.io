The Robust Reanimator

This char is a support character. His job is to buff and heal the rest of the party and to raise them when they die.

It uses the same leveling scheme as the Combat Medic from the epic chars page. I like that build, but the focus on spellcasting takes away from the main focus of the build. In my mind, the focus is being as indestructible as possible in order to survive when your party gets wiped out by the nasties.

So my build has the epic dr feats, much more con, epic skill focus concentration and improved expertise.
