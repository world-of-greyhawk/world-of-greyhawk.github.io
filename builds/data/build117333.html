Raise perform, tumble and UMD every bard level. Stop raising perform at 25 ranks.

Final levels are Bard 16/ Fighter 14/ RDD 10

This build also matures nice and early. Everything after devastating crit is just gravy.

This character has the 4 base attacks per round, and a massive damage output without relying on crits. Bard levels give a very tasty bard song, as well as flexibility from spells including the ever lovely bulls strength and haste. Solo or party player - this character can do it all.

I like this build even in a tough low magic server because you get the essential ghostly visage for low lvls, and eventually improved invis. You may want to swap to a shield/sword combo for a low magic server, but otherwise, due to bard spells/buffs, I don't see why it wouldn't fare just as well short of criticals being so nasty on the server every meleer needs PM lvls.

The revised builds I've been using have ftr 5/rdd 8/bard 7 as its pre-epic lvling. This is mainly to use the ftr bonus feats for damage reduction if I need to tank, as well as the standard focus/specialization. And since the server I play on has dev crit disabled, the pre-epic ftr feats are largely a waste.

Mostly I love this build for the fact you have bard buffs/spells to complement your melee abilties. Improved invis as mentioned before, along with greater magic weapon, keen edge, warcry, and the stat buffs, even in a high magic world, it is nice to be able to use other items instead of always needing to find a keen, +5 greatsword or whatever.
