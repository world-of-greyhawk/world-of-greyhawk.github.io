If you are saying to yourself hey he sure has a lot of hide\move silent points why didn't he work toward a level of SD. Well on the server I play SD's aren't allowed. You get 9th level druid spells, permenant +5 fists, dragon shape, a spell resistance of 28, and 70 something AC. Once bioware fixes the Dragon hide to where it has a +5 DR hide it may be more viable to take a lot less monk levels. If you wanted to make it a shifter do the level progression the same up until level 22 where you would take level 22 and 23 shifter then at level 24 monk for ki strike +5 then shifter the rest of the way. You would get all the shapes and still have +5 fists for your unarmed attacks... oh... and don't forget kobold commando and his nice HIPS ability.

To my knowledge the weapon focus/improved crit for unarmed doesn't work with shifter forms(at the very least about half the forms are armed), but finess does. You may with to consider taking epic prowerss instead , and using the other feats for whatever you desire (toughness and blind fight would be good picks). You could drop down your monk levels to just 1. You still will get flrry of blows and the AC modifier, as well as evasion. Monk doesn't give you much from level 2-5. You could also then take monk as your last level and not usffer the xp penalty. You can stop putting points into tumble at 40, and you probably don't need that much lore (unless they are using the pnp epic spell system on your PW).
