I had some help with this build, thanks Kaliban99, Grimnir77, Xylophone, and Cinnabar Din. I'll break it down for everyone here.

I managed to get a decent AB and high HPs with this build. If you wanted you could switch the extra CON bonuses and trade them for STR, but for and extra 20 HPs I figured this would be better. Just curious to the other builders what there preference is to gaining 20 HPs or +1AB? And would you guys go with Armor Skin instead of Epic Toughness? You'll find it quite effective using Darkness spell and Ultravision. Add in all your buffs you should be in good shape for some stalking. With Bardic Song, Cursed Song, Taunt, and Divine Shield used with Eagles Splendor you'll grab +11 AB and +10 AC. When I first made this build I included Improve Crit. Using Epic Builds with high AC you can just throw that out the window. So to accomidate, I added higher HPs. Granted you get +5d6 for Sneak Attack, you'll do decent amount of damage to someone caught in the dark.
