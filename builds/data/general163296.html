Just wondered if anyone had done one of these previously?

Obvious split is Ranger 21, Monk 6, Druid 13.

Ideas:
Take Ranger levels first for max AB.
Get Bane of Enemies at level 21.
Boost Wisdom as high as possible.
Take Zen Archery.
Monk gets AC bonus and IKD.
Druid gets Dragon shape.
All classes make use of Wisdom.
Ranger and Druid make a natural fit (e.g Animal Companion)
Plenty of skill points so no need for high int (or cha) so the Ranger can be OK ahead of the Druid levels. You need 18 levels of Druid to get Dragon Shape.

Besides, Bane of Enemies will only work on 5 different types of enemies (if I remember correctly). Best way to see if anyone has posted a build like this is to go to the index on the builds forum. Then hit control-f and type one of the classes. Just keep hitting find next until you find something similar, or until you get through the whole page.
