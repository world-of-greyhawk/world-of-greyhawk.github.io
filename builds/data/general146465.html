New Ultimate Archer

- I got a kick out of this build and it hardly ever missed. I pissed a lot characters off because I never missed. (Well almost.)

- I got hit by a few character who had blind fighting, but most didn't take the feat so they SC 40% and high AC, keep them from hitting me all the time. In fact, getting hit was almost rare.


Disciplined Knight

- She did 62 damage without devastating critical nor enhanced weapons and land quite a few criticals.

- Did little to no damage from REF attacks in PvP and she did evade mind affecting attacks pretty good too.

- Lasted for a long and had a lot mage's running for their money.


Puck the Adventurer

- Weeded out traps and I end up with 18 Strong traps in my inventory.

- With UMD, he could wield almost any item and was own mage & cleric.

- His nigh 100 damage sneak and 16 weapon damage made fights real short.

- I found throwing darts very useful than a using a bow.

- Other Players had a hard time hitting me while using SC 50%


Avatar of Torm

- As I suspected, it did a 400 something smite on a Lich (Ouch!) in the monster arena.

- Had a friend do Harm and a moment later I used Lay on Hands while using Aura of Glory and Eagle's Splendor and recover my life in full.

- Though it was made for PvP, it did some good damage against opponents in straight up melees when buffed.


As for the Battle Archer, though I built it in a arena server, I couldn't test it out since it was made more for PnP than PvP.
