This evil knight hunts intelligent prey--mankind.

I am not sure if I like the build or not. He's currently only designed on paper (slow day at work again). I was trying to go for a dark knight, melee type that knocks down his enemies and tears them to pieces.

Fighter might be a better build than ranger, but ranger has more skill points and ranger FE's are a very nice thing. I'd pick playable races as his FE's (human, elf, dwarf, gnome, halfling)

I went with 13 wisdom because 4th level ranger spells don't seem worth taking. You can easily put 10 CHA and 14 int/wis. You'd get 43 more skill points and 4th level spells at the cost of only -1 saves. I'm not sure whether epic prowess or epic fiendish servant would be better. (I don't know stats of the epic servant)
