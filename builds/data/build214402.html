Let's all admit it - Red Mages are awesome in the Final Fantasy series. That having been said, I figured it would be fun to try creating a Red Mage build for NWN. This isn't much of a munchkin build for obvious reasons, but I tried to make it as powerful as possible while still keeping as many elements of the traditional Red Mage as possible (quick casting, rapier usage, above-average competency in both elemental damage and healing magic, etc.)

Playable from level 1 - 40 PvM

Naked-sans-buffs with a rapier, we have 20 AC, just shy of 200 HP, and an AB of 31; saves of 19(f), 22(r), and 26(w). Buffs and equipment boost everything nicely. Our spells aren't at all bad, as we have access to all levels and can cast everything Quickened and Stilled automatically (I myself mostly use maximized mid-level spells, as the fire/cold/lightning evocation spells seem to be most prolific there, and that's more Final Fantasy-ish, non?). Our spell focus and penetration feats should make up for a lack of Wizard - and rarely Cleric - levels, and since we took Healing domain we can cast Heal 5 times, and any other generic healing spell is automatically Empowered.

Thoughts?
