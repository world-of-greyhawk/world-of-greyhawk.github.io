It's a fair point. You get Greater Wisdom III and IV for free and you need one of them to cast Level 9 spells and the other for the AC bonus from your monk class. Obviously, you get the benefit of the six caster levels and two AB points from the War Domain (mind the cap, however). That compromise certainly inspired the V.2, which takes Monk to 6 to pick up IKD. Taking it to 9 would pick up Improved Evasion and would increase your damage. Two things I think I'd do differently, upon reflection.

One, take Blind Fight instead of Great Fortitude.

Two, take Epic Prowess and ESF: Concentration instead of Great Strength III and IV.
