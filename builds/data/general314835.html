A dev-critter spellsword? nice.

By the way, yeah I forgot to mention what I like to see in a spellsword. And well, basically, I want to see buffs, offensive spells that can really be used (why the heck can't a Druid be considered a spellbow? I can Stonehold your mom while I hit you with my cool arrows; works for me) and melee stats above of what a Pure Fighter can get (acceptable damage, AB and AC to survive just on that). That way you can at least say "hey, I've got spellpower, and I still hit harder than a playable meleer, so taking this classes was worth something".

I like builds that excel at what they do, and since a spellsword won't excel at fighting or casting as much as a specialized build, playability and versatility are musts.
