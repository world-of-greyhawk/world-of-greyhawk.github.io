Playable From Level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

Standing on the rooftop you chuckle to yourself. Not the hearty laugh of someone who has just heard a joke told, but the cynical laugh of one who has seen too much.

Killing, that is your skill and your trade. You remember back to better times, times where most chuckles were not cynical. You had friends then. A ranked scout in the army, you did quite well for yourself. That all changed when you were approached by the Shadow Infantry. An elite commando unit famed for its almost magical stealth abilities. It was here that you met Red (everyone just became known by their nickname, real names were kept secret). She was your mentor, your superior officer, and your lover.

Your skills proved to be exceptional within the unit. It was on a standard mission that the biggest change to your life would come. Tracking down brigands upcountry it wasnt unusual for the squads to split up and move on solo communicating only by making animal sounds.

You eventually came across the enemies camp, just in time to see a helmeted black knight execute Red. Outnumbered you had no choice but to just sit there. You stayed and watched the camp all night. They broke up shortly after dawn. You took the time to bury Red's tortured and limp body before you set to tracking them.

You eventually caught one of them and managed to get a name out of him , not for your Mentor's killer but for the leader of this particular camp. Resigning your commission in the army , you set about training for revenge. Almost exactly 10 months later and an equal
number of assassinations you finally tracked down Red's killer, a rich noble who was in fact secretly running the local brigands. With no evidence there was no way you could take him to justice. You bid your time until the time was right. His death was slow, you made sure of that, cutting his inside thigh you sat and watched as he bled to death. When asked why , you just said "For Red".

You have lost count how many souls you have sent to void since then. It is what you do , it is what you are good at. Why be anything else.

Still you cant help but wish the path you travelled had not been so bathed in blood.
