A quick build I put together that was inspired from the movie "The Last Samurai".

Samurai Archer

A Samurai who is equally skilled with his Bow as he is with his Katana, even his hands and feet are useful. With 3 methods of attacks it rounds out a fun/versatile character.

Human Lawful Neutral
