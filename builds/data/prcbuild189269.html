For those who would like to have their builds hosted by the PRC:

The usual rules for the Epic Builds guild apply. Email all submissions to PRCDev@gmail.com with "General Build Submission" in the topic. Please include a level 40 bic of the build, name of the author and name of the build.

ANY build is eligible as long as it uses PRC content.
