Damage types bug? Didn't know one existed (not suprised seeing how buggy NWN is)

If your wepon has a base damage of two different types (halberd is piercing and slashing) and you come up against DR against one of those types, the way it's supposed to work is all damage from the weapon would be considered as the other type. The game just treats all damage as the type that your opponent has as DR.

Example: You're up against an opponent with 20/- DR slashing. You hit with your halberd for 25 points of damage. What's supposed to happen is all the damage is supposed to be considered as piercing (since you deal both types of damage). What actually happens is the game considers all the damage as slashing and you only deal 5 points.
