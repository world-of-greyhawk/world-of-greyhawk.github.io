What do you mean by "iffy"? And which "either Longbow feats" do you mean?

The character was built to dual-wield a rapier and a kukri. Why should it be changed to an archer build? How do you propose fitting in SC5?

Why don't you post your version as a stand-alone build and everyone can evaluate it? Couple of thoughts:
First, Epic Dodge does more to protect you than SCV. I would get that first - take Rogue 13 on a general feat level, and get both Defensive Roll and Epic Dodge.

Second, you are wasting feats to take both Kukri and Rapier. I would either just dual-wield shortswords or handaxes (saving 3 feats) or dual-wield the Kukris (saving 2 feats). With low str and base damage, you aren't getting that much from crits anyway. Since you have some sneak damage from Rogue, I would add KD to make better use of it - that will help you more than extended crit range.

Personally, I would completely drop the SCV - you are better off with ED and 2 more Dex instead. You have UMD, you can always cast Imp Invis from scrolls, or get a cloak of Displacement, etc. to get the 50% concealment.
