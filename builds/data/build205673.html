Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM or PvP

My goal with the Stonedeath build was to have a character that could optimize his sneak attack/knockdown damage. I chose the assassin because I've always loved the concept of the death attack. I'm playing with him now on a PvP server and he's doing very well. I usually stay invisible until the mage's shields wear off then I use improved knockdown coupled with death attack to send them to their graves.

With Stonedeath, you're going to want heavy armor, a good tower shield, as much AC gear as you can find (remember, he can wear Monk items such as Sun Soul Boots), rings to boost Intelligence (this raises his Death Attack DC), and various other items to raise his Strength.
