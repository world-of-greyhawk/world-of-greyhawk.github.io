You lack some info, quite a few actually, post them please. Read the Epic build rules sticky and free template sticky. Initiative feats in a dex build are a waste IMO, you'll be hitting first anyhow. Blinding speed is a waste too if permahaste items are available or even just potions of haste. Crippling strike is a nice rogue bonus feat I would even delay SC feats and get it in first. Dodge can be changed for something better, since it is NOT a ED prerequisite. By dropping imp.initiative, dodge and toughness you can get the three dual wield feats. Always nice for a sneaker to get as many attacks as possible.
No EWF? Come on, that's +2 AB! Also you are getting 3 Gr.DEX for a final Dex of 33...a waste of a feat.
I would also move 2 RDD lvls in epic for +1 AB.
INT 10 is kinda low IMO...rogues get lotsa useful skills.
