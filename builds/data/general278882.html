Hey everyone. It's been a long time since I've played NwN, and an even longer time since I've posted here, but I'm getting the game again in a couple of weeks and wanted to plan out my new build. I've always been a fan of dodging, but what I had in mind was recreating a prestige called Dread Fang. Dread Fangs are elite drow warriors that combine fighter and rogue classes.

So what I had in mind was combining barbarian with shadowdancer. I only want to invest in Shadowdancer long enough to get epic dodge, so this will end up being more of a duelist type build. What I wanted to know, is how I can best utilize the barbarian part of the build. Any suggestions? Alright, let's go with your demands, you need 10 levels of Shadowdancer and 25 DEX for Epic Dodge, though I'd take Rogue instead of Shadowdancer myself.

You should take a third class to get more stuff in your build... if you want a lot of Barbarian levels you can get good boost with only a few levels of Monk or Fighter.

What environment you're planning on playing your build? if you're on low magic then you should take caster levels like Wizard or Cleric for buffs.

Barbarian itself gives you rages, so you know you can buff STR and CON to good amounts even being DEX based, but as DEX based I think you're only getting up to Terrifying Rage, which you can get with 15 Barbarian levels, since you probably won't qualify for Thundering Rage nor Mighty Rage. Barbarian levels give low amount of DR and a bit of HP but going too much as of Barbarian 30 / Shadowdancer 10 is a waste, you better get a third class...
