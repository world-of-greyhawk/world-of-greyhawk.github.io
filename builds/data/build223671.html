Why did I stop UMD at 26?
The reason I stopped at 26 instead of 25 is that 26 will give you an actual 25 in UMD which opens up the use of most magical items.

Why did I stop Concentration at 20?
I would have had to cross class skill points to raise it any higher and 20 is usually enough to save against most creatures Taunts.

Why did I choose a Battleaxe instead of a Greataxe or Greatsword?
I wanted the extra AC you get from a shield which comes in handy in the early levels, plus a Battleaxe has a nice critical range. If you can get a Keen Battleaxe your critical range would be (16 - 20 / x4) which will give you lots of criticals and high damage.

Why did I take 8 on Charisma instead of 6 and use the stat points somewhere else?
The reason I put Charisma at 8 instead of 6 was so I could use magical items earlier in the build. Use a Eagle Splendor and grab a Nymph Cloak early in the build and you will be able to use scrolls with an Innate Level of 4. Scrolls like Greater Magic Weapon, Keen Edge, Flame Weapon, Death Armor, Elemental Shield, Mestil's Acid Sheath, Improved Invisibility, etc.

If you see anyway to improve upon this build, please let me know.
