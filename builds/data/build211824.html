There was a knock on the Commanders door. His servant came in. "pardon me sir, but the Dwarven commander is here."
"Send him in." He replied. A bit early, but that's fine. The sooner i get the grumpy one out of here the better.
"Greetings and well met, Commander!" a surprisingly pleasant and charming Dwarf spoke to him. "I am Carwin, the commander of the Dwarven army."
The commander was dumbstruck a few moments, taken by the Dwarfs winning person.
"Well met. You are early, that is good. We have much to discuss."
They started talking about their plans of conquest, and the cooperation of their army's.
The Human commander was again surprised at how easy the Dwarf was to speak to, but he was still a tough negotiator, as he had suspected.
Suddenly the Dwarf stated sharply. "Send your guards away, I have one secret proposition to give, and noone can hear it!"
The commander was reluctant to comply, but something made him trust this Dwarf. He went to the door and told his guards to take leave the post. He followed them with his eyes until they were gone.
"There, my guards are out of earshot, what was that you......" He suddenly felt a crippling pain in his spine, and with his last breath he knew he had just made a grave mistake. His last....

"So, now we'll just have to await the Dwarf, and your two armies will have lost their leaders. Then we'll see who conquers..." He wiped the blood off of his battleaxe and sat down in a dark corner to await the Dwarf.

but it is very different.

I have noticed that a lot of good guys out there really enjoys their smite's, so imagine when Goody-Two-Shoes arrive to stop you from bullying others and attacks your undead friend. When he feels the SMITE from your battleaxe, powered by a decent sneak attack, he's in for a treat. After that, get him down, and SMITE+SNEAK, SMITE+SNEAK until he buys a farmhouse. A small one with a earthen roof.

At the end of the build there are a few tweaks possible, drop Gr.STR 1 and take great Smite. Take CHA at lvl 40. This will reduce AB and dmg by 1, but yield better Divine, saves and Smite dmg +28.
I did not do that because I feel AB is low as it is.
Another version is to drop 1 Great Smite for 1 Gr.CHA, to get the even CHA score that way.

A very good tactic is to have a friend summoned, keep him between yourself and your opponent and keep that tactic until your opponent engages your summon, then close in and SMITE, sneak or knock him down. Or two of the above.

This is a PvP build only vs good-aligned opponents, though you got UMD galore to get buffs, invisibilty and such to generate sneaks. IKD is a must to. 4 Rogue levs seems like 1 to much, but you get three early which makes him more playable, and 1 in the end for skilldump. And the alternative is 1 fighter level, which brings nothing.

I would like a better AB, but sorry. I would like an even CHA score, see above for how. I would like Epic Fiendish Servant, but I chose different. The build also has few hp's very few considering that it is a Dwarven melee build. The original build had Toughness instead of IKD, but it is better as it is.
