Honestly, with 25 Wisdom and 25 Cha requirements... the price is way to steep for it's worth.

If you are looking to turn a lvl 40 Shifter Rak, you'll have to be a lvl 36 (minimum) cleric. and at that level you'll be able to bypass the Rak's SR since it does not have 36 SR.

When turning outsiders, you have to bypass 2 things. First is the HD check. That is just like the check you would do when turning undead. Second check is to overcome the "turn resistance" which is their SR. Your normal HD check value is used for that too. With Planar Turning you 1/2 their SR value for determining if you can overcome their resistance.

Sun Domain helps buff up your turning power when it comes to the HD check. Good/Evil domains allow you to turn Outsides like undead (simular to Planar Turning except you don't 1/2 the SR check).
