Summoning - Mirdautas Vras AA has full AB progression and adds +1 to ranged (bow) AB and damage at AA level 1 and every 2nd level after that.

If your character primarily is a sorc/wizard, you can get + 9 to ranged AB if you take as many AA levels as you can (8) pre epic, and end up with 9 or 10 AA levels in the build. A wizard 30/ AA 10 built that way will have a ranged AB somewhere near 30/25/20 and damage 1d8 + 5 unbuffed, making him a mediocre mob killer at most.
If your character primarily is an archer, the extra AB and damage can come in handy.
AA can also be of some use to give strength (or charisma)based meleers a better ranged AB.
