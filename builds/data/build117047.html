For those who do not want to have Devastating Critical in their build, you can change it something else. It will not alter the build in any form other than the critical. With just overwhelming critical, you should be able to get at least 120 to approx. 200 or so. That is only if you equiped the character with weapon with keen, enchantment +12, and STR +12.

This character build can score critical every 7 out of 10 hits. You can add more damage using Weapon Master's Ki strike, along with Improved Power Attack. If you equip anything that gives character CON +12, the health will change from approx. 550 to approx. 800 HP depending on how you do the build(trick is to check before you "ok" the level build, check how much HP will be added, it must have more than 11+ and you will be able to get more HP out of this build by then).

More information: This build is built mainly to deal with dragons, it can easily topple ten dragon in less than 40 seconds(real time), even with just Overwhelming Critical if you chose not to have Devastating Critical. Also, if you equip the character with any items that gives him immunity to fear, you are pretty much set there.
