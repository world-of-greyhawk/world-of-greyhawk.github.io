The goal of this build was to have a Great Smiter that had Listen and Spot as class skills and could survive on the front lines. The down side is it might be hard to play before level 9, as it has low DEX and only light armor at that point making it hard to get a decent AC. It also does not have as good of AB as most fighter types due to its low STR. On the plus side it eventually has Great Smite 6, heavy armor, Evasion, martial weapons, a decent Sneak Attack, an Epic Vrock, good saves, high Divine Shield and Divine Might stats, and a good undead turning check. I suggest using a full plate and large shield with this as a tower shield would have too great of an impact on Tumble.
