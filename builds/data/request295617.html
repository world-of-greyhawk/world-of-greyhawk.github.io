You have lots of options, depending on what you want and what you're willing to give up, but you cannot have it all. There will always be a trade-off.

Cleric 26 / Fighter 4 gives you Weapon Spec / Epic Weapon Spec, free Martial Weapons Proficiency, and makes you undispellable.
Cleric 26 / Monk 2 / Shadowdancer 2 gives you Hide in Plain Sight, Uncanny Dodge, Monk's attack progression with kamas, and Monk's WIS armor bonus, and it makes you undispellable.
Cleric 20 / Shadowdancer 10 gives you level 9 spells, Uncanny Dodge, Epic Dodge, and Hide in Plain Sight, but you are dispellable.
Cleric 16 / Rogue 13 / Shadowdancer 1 gives you Uncanny Dodge, Epic Dodge, Hide in Plain Sight, and lots of Rogue skill dumps, but only level 8 spells, and you are even more easily dispelled.
