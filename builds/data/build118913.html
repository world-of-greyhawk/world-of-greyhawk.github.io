This character was never posted, It was slightly better than Divine Blender, but pales in comparison to the Vegomatic. I know I could add in the extra attacks from divine power and the damage from darkfire. However, the former is a bug that may be fixed, and the latter is also currently bugged.

Advantages

-This character has a potential 10 attacks per turn. Coupled with bonuses from epic weapon specialization, and clerical spells he can do alot of damage.
-Evasion. This character has some spell resistance.
-Deflect arrows.
-Access to level 7 cleric spells.

Disadvantages

-One Mordenkainen's Disjunction and he loses a lot of power.
-If he can't rest, he's no good.
-Bad Saves.
-Only 408 hitpoints.
-39/40 AB. Throw in the cleric buffs, and he can hit fairly well.

Variant Comparison

-Variant 1 has devastating critical. It only has a threat range of 18-20, but with 10 attacks that's pretty nasty
-Variant 2 has a 1 point higher AB and 2 additional points of armor.
