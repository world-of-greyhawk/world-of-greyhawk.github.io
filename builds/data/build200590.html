PvM build, playable lvl 1-40

That's right, yet another dragon build. I designed it with the goal of having a good shapeshifter druid without using the shifter class, so that he can still get maximum attacks/round. His high wisdom and reasonable caster level make him a good spellcaster druid, but I use that mainly for buffing and occasionally against groups of monsters that just aren't worth the time it takes to shapeshift. I wanted to reach druid lvl 12 as soon as possible for dire bear shape, but I've also taken monk levels early to keep a high AC and to get stunning fist which has a very good DC with his high wisdom. At lvl 40 he has infinate wildshape, elemental shape 3x/day, dragon shape 3x/day.
I looked around a bit before posting to make sure this build would not be a copy of an older one and found these two which are rather similar:

Christophs Dragon of Torm (Druid 25/CoT 6/Monk 9)
The Dragon Druid by AntonTD (Druid 20/Monk 10/CoT 10)

I decided to post this anyway as I feel it has some advantages.
