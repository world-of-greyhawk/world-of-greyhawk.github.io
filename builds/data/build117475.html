Advantages

-Dragon form. He has high saves in this form and high hit points. Throw in his paladin and druid buffs and he is pretty strong.
-Divine might and Divine shield. Even if he is dispelled in dragon form he can still use these abilities.
-Evasion. Coupled with high saves he is pretty spell resistant.

Disadvantages

-Very weak in human form.
-Low AB.
-Can't cast spells in Dragon form.
