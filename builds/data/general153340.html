I'd say go for a character that does not require a lot in lines of gear. Clerics do well at level 20. One can get the DC on their Implosion very high. And since it is just a level 20 server, with very limited gear (13000 GP per item), people tend not to have the best of saves. And if they do have good saves, they are usually weaker to melee, which Clerics are also very good at (better than fighters in my opinion).

It would also depend on the server's rules. If the server has 'fixed' True Sight so that it does not see people hiding in Shadow, Ranger/ShadowDancer does very well (if you focus everythingo n hiding). That build would be weaker in melee, and is easy pickings if a Mage catches them, but they would be able to hide from most everything. If you were go to that route, using a Bow and Arrows is the way to go.

I also feel that Monks do well at level 20. Though getting good gear for them tends to be hard (for both Server Vault and Local Vault), but they are very defensive, which is always a plus (outliving everyone is just as good as being able to kill everyone).
