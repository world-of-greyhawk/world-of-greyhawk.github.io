Not quite. Rogues get a bonus feat every 4th Rogue level after Rogue level 20:

Every character gets a feat at level 1, another at level 3, and every 3 levels thereafter, regardless of class taken.

Humans get a bonus feat at character level 1.

Certain classes get bonus feats at specific pre-epic class levels (FTRs, Wizards, Rangers, Rogues, for example).

Once a class reaches epic levels, they get bonus feats at specific class intervals. These can be every 2, 3, 4, or 5 levels, depending on the class (and may be different than the pre-epic bonus feat interval).

As noted epic Rogues get bonus feats every 4th level. (Pre-epic, they get bonus feats at Rogue levels 10, 13, 16, and 19)
