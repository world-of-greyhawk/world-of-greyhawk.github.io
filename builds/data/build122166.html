Inspired by Mith's Lone Ranger and Torias' Rogue Archer, I set out to create a stealthy build that used a crossbow in conjunction with Devastating Critical. This character combines all of the support abilities of the bard with the ever-useful Hide in Plain Sight ability of the Shadowdancer. This build only gets three attacks a round before Haste, but it makes those shots count, using the many buffs available to the Bard class to boost its accuracy and damage.

In addition to the heavy crossbow, I have the character carry a morningstar with them for meleeing undead and construct opponents, who will be a bit of a problem in the absence of Sunburst or Crumble scrolls. The last four feats you get are pretty flexible, and I have only included one possible setup. You may want to pick up Devastating Critical: Morningstar, or you may want to improve the defensive abilities of the build by including Lasting Impression, Armor Skin, and Epic Toughness. Your call.
