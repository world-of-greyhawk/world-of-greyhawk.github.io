Monk/Fighter/Weapon master

This is my favorite melee build. She has incredible speed, six attacks per round unarmed, eight with dual kamas, natural immunities, damage reduction, great spell resistance, and high AC.
With Empty body active it's possible to sneak up on nearly any enemy then strike with Quivering palm. Sometimes this will end a fight before it even starts. A periapt of wisdom makes this even more likely.
Dealing with packs isn't a problem with flurry of blows, great cleave and dual kamas equipped. Even without a keen weapon critical hits strike often enough to end battles quickly.
Creatures that are immune to critical hits are often overwhelmed quickly just by the sheer number of attacks she's able to dish out.
