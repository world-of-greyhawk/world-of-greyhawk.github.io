I designed this build for soloing. In fact, I'm soloing this though the OC now. You'll want to carry around a 2-hander, like a heavy flail, to power through an occasional monster with high damage reduction.

The search engine shows 35 Rogue, Fighter, Weapon Master builds, but only one with this same distribution, Master of the Deathaxe. This build is significantly different from that one in feats, abilities, and skills.
