I was surprised how poor the Will Save was, despite taking both Iron and Epic Will. I had considered opting for Luck of Heros/ Strong Soul at the expense of Imp.Initiative, but after consideration this really wasn't worth it.

Tymora's Smile and the base Spellcraft ranks go some way to helping with this, but you will always suffer in a build of this sort with saves.

A downside is the reliance on a single weapon type.
