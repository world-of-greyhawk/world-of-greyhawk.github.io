The Good
AB on par with your average tank. Can be buffed (GMW)
Decent AC, even better when buffed (Blinding speed, Cat's Grace) = up to +6
Good hitpoints for a sneaky midget
13d6 Sneak attack
Hard to sneak up on
High UMD
Holy Sword and Bless Weapon will help with tougher undead.
On sale with one bonus free dodge per round.

The Bad
Low saves
Extremely susceptible to knockdowns
Would be encumbered by a feather
Small size reduces Knockdown effectiveness somewhat

The Ugly
20% XP Penalty means toughing it out a bit from level 10 onwards.

Might consider sacrificing some spot and/or listen for a half a tank of discipline, or even a full tank (you'd have to shift a class).
