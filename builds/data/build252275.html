Defender of the Monastry (Fighter 6/Monk 9/Champion of Torm 25)
PvM,PvP level 1-40

I wanted to make a fighter with better defenses. More like a an all round fighter. I like this mix of classes. I have found a few of them here in this forum, but most are fighter-class heavy. I wanted better defenses so I made it CoT/monk-heavy. I ended up with this character, and I think it is very strong indeed.

Advantages:
- Excellent saves. Hard to land a devasting critical at him and not easy to roast either (38 refl and Imp. evasion)
- Good AB but not like a Weapon Master off course. Have powerful divine wrath once a day, and 3 smites for really hard to hit and strong opponents.
- 30% chanse to land a critical hit with scimitar, combined with DC 40 dev. crit. and 4 attacks pr round, most opponents without dev. crit. immunities is in trouble. (45% with a keen scimitar like Ula's Heart)
- Also proficient with warhammer for multiple damage types.
- Lots of combat feats. Imp. Knockdown, Imp. Power Attack, Great Cleave, Blind Fight and Superior Initiative.
- Stealthy having 40 ranks in bote Hide and Move
- 63 ranks in discipline making him very hard to knock down
- Monk abilities

Disadvantages:
- Mediocre AC (naked 20)
- Mediocre basic damage for a fighter
- Not to may HP for a fighter (462)
- Can't self buff except potions
