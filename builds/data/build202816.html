PvM build, playable lvl 1-40

This is a revised version my caster dragon build, but because it now gets all the shapeshifting feats a druid can get, without shifter levels that is, I felt it deserved the new name . There are plenty of minor changes, but the major difference is that I've taken more druid levels, getting infinite elemental shape and two epic spells. It also making his buffs very difficult to dispel (roll of 17+ with Mord's Dis). The other important change is that I got higher STR by lowering CON, which allows power attack, improved power attack and great cleave, but makes him quite vulnerable in human form. He's a pretty solid tank when shapeshifted, his offensive spells have a good DC with owl's insight, and he can buff himself and party members reasonably well.
