This is a build I posted in the NWN general forum a month ago or so, in answer to a build request. The idea is that of dual kama wielder with a decent AB, with all the rogues tricks up his sleeve: crippling strike, sneak attacks, UMD, epic dodge, trap setting skills (coupled with skill mastery). Add called shot and expertise to help in melee. Fighter brings necessary bonus feats, WS/EWS and the fourth attack per round. And it forces a somehow odd levelling to avoid XP penalty
Skill mastery can be swapped around with slippery mind, according to when you prefer getting one or the other.
I was sorry not to have enough skill points to max out concentration too. I too, like Grimm, often find that skillpoints are not enough
Hit points are decent for an epic dodger and saves are not bad either. Skill focus: spellcraft and maxed out spellcraft would add another +1 to saves vs spells, ESF: spellcraft a further +2. If just there was room for it.

Those that don't care about XP penalty can lvl up in a more straightforward way.

Enough with my ranting, let the build speak for itself.
