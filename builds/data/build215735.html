One problem with this build. WM gets feats and Chosen wep focus on lvl's 13, 16 and every 3rd lvl, not every 5th like the manual says. So you have to end it with 13 or 16 WM levels, which will give you some problems. One suggestion, go 7 bard and then 5 PM until you qualify for WM, ending your build bard8/PM16/WM16. You will lose an ab early but end up the same, and get an WM epic feat. You will lose one attack tho, but i don't think you'll miss an attack at 24 ab.
And wher do your damage come from? You will have quite nice AC, nothing uber perhaps, but medium AB and little or no damage output and few attacks.
