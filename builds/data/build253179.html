It's a pretty simple build, after all is said and done. Kill things with spear.

In the initial incarnation of this build, 34 Strength was taken, but doing so meant losing 1 point in Dex, Int and Chr, a loss of 43 skill points, and the loss of ESF: Taunt. While a 47AB looked really cool on a 3 APR build, I didn't find it as effective or as nasty as the 46AB version. And while 3 attacks per round doesn't look like much, he does tremendous damage on each hit, and can crank out more attacks when he flicks the spear into Whirlwind Mode--very useful against the mobs. Basically, it's a smooth blend of both offense and defense.

Only a few other builds like this exist, and they go for PM16 for the +4AC and the girly-man Epic Warding spell. This cat, I thought, could hold his own in combat without Epic Warding, and it doesn't protect one against spells anyway, so I choose Offense over defense and went for the highest amount of WM possible and still get PM10.
