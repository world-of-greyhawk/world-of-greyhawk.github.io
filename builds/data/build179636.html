Yes, everyone and their mother has made a variation of these classes. This one has a twist though, which has helped make it fun for me; and so I've always played every new module with this first. Such a simple build, but I've gotten more PMs on it than any other, from people who liked playing it.

Designed for use in HotU (I went back and used it in SOU too; just as effective). The overall idea being to stop STR at maximum effectiveness for bows (18+12 STR gives +10 mighty damage), and in general be an effective melee Rogue as well as a good AA. Original intent was to use henchmen but build was powerful enough that I soloed it instead from Chapter 2 on. (Just powered over the final bosses piercing DR; didnt even have to use his weaknesses).

Strategies
Uses simple spells,UMD,Traps to change battlefield to his advantage.
Primary attack via bow, behind summons/henchmen.
Finessed Shortsword & shield as backup, sneaks using IKD and Ultravision/Darkness.
Two-handed Flail when needed (Undead, perhaps).
