Appearance2da Fighter/mage won't be a tank (lower hitpoints, can't cast spells in heavy armor, stat focus splits between three abilities), and as noted, spellswords need a lot of levels.

Dualwielding scimitars is a bad idea, since they are non-finessable long blades - meaning that you need a 15 dex for ambidexterity but can't be a dex build (wasting points there) and suffer a -4 dualwielding penalty to your AB for both weapons.

Experience is the mother of good judgement; bad judgement is the father of experience. You can build a decent spellsword with 30 levels, IMO. The key is not to be obsessed with getting 4 attacks. If you're willing ot accept only 3 attacks, you can grab decent spellcasting levels and Autostill III.
