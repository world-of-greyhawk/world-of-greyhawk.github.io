Well, since noone else has posted a build here besides me in a LOOOOOOOOOOOOONG time, it is time that I finally post a build, the LAST PRC build, im afraid. It is the last cuz after I post it, everyone will move to PRC and never ever play NWN again! I can only hope and I know this wont be so.

Is this the ULTIMATE char? well, let me explain my reasoning for this.

I) We end up with a pure caster level! THat's right, we get 40 levels of divine caster! This is undispellable! This is 80 rounds of Blade barriers! This is casting extended buffs for soooo long that you could leave and go to work and come back and still be fully buffed!

II) We have 5 (max) levels of Heirophant, which is a divine clerics DREAM!! We get Spell Power +10! For those that dont know, it is +10 to spell DC! This means that with this build and +12 to wisdom, we get a 46 dc on Implosion! Higher than any nwn build (esp with ONLY 19 base wisdom!), and only 9 off the max DC possible of 57 achieved with a wis 38 heirophant cleric.

III) We get EPIC spells! Yes, this guy gets Epic Mage armor, warding and ALL the others (with spellcraft gear).

IV) We get PRC spells. FOr a cleric, we get a simple little level 2 spell, Living Undeath. THis is what I call, "Pocket Palemaster" It grants you immunity to crits!

V) WE get Dev crit at lv 21! Yes a pure divine caster with dev crit at 21! WOW! And the best part is, it works!

VI) We have SoV and Firestorm to take out those nasty crit immune things like Golems and such. I have taken on a map full of gols and been done with them in a mere 4 mins!

VII) Oh, and since hes a cleric, we get Divine power and favor, battletide, prayer, bless, and a few other prc spells to boost both damage and ab to the 20 cap limit!

VIII) Darkfire!

IX) He gets to use a rapier/scrimter! With a nice bard/wiz companion (or a keenable spell from an itm) we get 12-20 crit range!

X) HALF ORC!! For the ultimate coolness factor. And for the newbies to look at you in awe as you have built the ultimate character possible!

Basically, this character, in the hands of a knowledgable player is able to solo almost anything possible! WIth the right gear, he becomes a living GOD!

Are there flaws? Yes, Kill wiz/sorcs before they can breech you! Loosin the buffs can make you weak. But once they are down.
