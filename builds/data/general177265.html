Syrath You only need a dexterity of 28 to tie the AC provided by a Full Plate armor thus an 18 dex with +12 from items can easily beat it.

Cleric spells are helpful but not that helpful (ie helpful only in some situations).

Also if you went elf (assuming you don't care about the xp penalty) you could easily get your DEX much much higher and not only would you have a higher AB and AC but you could put the Zen Archery Feat toward something else.

To me an extra few spells won't make as much of a difference as a higher AB and AC and extra feat. Also this build doesn't look like it's aimed at getting a super high DC for spells so much as it is for buffing to supplement the AA strength so losing a bit of Wisdom won't hurt too much in that respect.

As far as the build itself, I've used similiar ones and they're extremely fun to play, so it's a good suggestion for fun.
