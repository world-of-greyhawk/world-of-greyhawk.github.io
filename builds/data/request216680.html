First off 30 lvls of WM is totally and utterly useless. The ab on a WM increases ever 3 lvls after lvl 10 so: 13, 16, 19, 22, 25, 28, 31 since you can only take 30 lvls of Wm your reletive usful maximmum is 28.

Also if you are playing PVP there are very few builds that are any good with TWF, That is Monks weilding Kamas, anyone with monk wis ac since sheild negates it, and CSers (Corner sneakers) Otherwise your classic tank is best off with a shield for the extra AC or a big weapon for the extra Damage.

As for a Str Build go:

10 Fighter/28 Wm/2 rogue

Depending on the Weapons on the server you play with you want to take either Rapier or Scimitar depending on which one is better for the 10-20 crit range on a keen one. Or alternitively go Dwarven Waraxe for More damage if there are a lot of crit-immune people.

With this your looking at around 62 or so ab with a +5 wep and +12 str

As for a Dex build with so many WM lvls you either Dump epic Dodge/Uncanny Dodge and go for killer ab, or loose ab get uncanny and Epic Dodge. I'll show both:

Fighter 10/WM 25/HS 5

Go HS for the two bonus feats and take both of them great dexterity, go Elf start with 20 dex, and with +12 dex you end with 50 dex. The +1 ab you loose from 25 WM lvls over 28 is negated by the +2 dex, also you get +1 ac, tumble, spot, disc etc and beter saves.

You're looking at about 66 ab with +12 dex and +5 weapon

For Epic Dodge/Tumble go

13 Rogue/19 Wm/8 Fighter

Your ab will be 62-64 ab with +12 dex and +5 weapon, but on the other hand you go gain 7d6 sneak attacks, epic dodge, and uncanny dodge. Also you are 1 ac less then the aforementioned HS build.

Hope that helps.
