Corvette is an ongoing project to create a formidable pc for any combat circumstance. With her massive SR and AC she is capable of shunning almost every attack away weather it be a sharpened steel blade or a barrage of arcane flames. She is however one of my favorite builds due to the ability to leave her standing there in the middle of battle whilst you run for that much needed toilet break you've been putting off for the last hour, to come back and find her surrounded by either corpses or alot of attackers all getting nowhere against you!

Skill Point Distribution:
Lvls 1-21 (monk levels) max out Discipline, Listen, Tumble and dont forget 8 points in Hide and Move Silently to meet the assasin reqs (not necessary for the rogue alternative.)
Lvls 22-23 (Assasin) max UMD, tumble, spot, listen.
Lvls 24-27 (Monk) max discipline, tumble and listen.
Lvl 28 (Wizard) i like to put 15 points in spell craft here just in case.
Lvls 29-36 (Monk) max discipline, tumble and listen.
Lvl 37 (Assasin) max spot and get UMD to 41 (taking account of the negative CHA bonus). You wont have enough points yet to max all the other focused skills Corrvette uses but you can get them later.
Lvl 37+ (Monk) max discipline, listen and get tumble to 40 (mmm...lovely ac bonus.)
You will have points spare so at anytime in the build feel free to put a point in pick lock and disable trap.
