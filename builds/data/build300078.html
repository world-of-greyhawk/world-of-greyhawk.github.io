A strong dev crit'er. High dev crit DC, good fort saves and a sky high discipline that few can challenge gives him a fair chance of winning most dev crit duels.
Whether a discipline of 67 is overkill or not depends on how the CR and magical equipment is balanced in the mod you play.
The AB is high enough for effective use of combat feats.

The AC would benefit from armor skin and expertise. You can free up an epic feat slot for armor skin if you sacrifice toughness, improved knockdown or improved disarm and take great cleave pre epic. Another option is if you can drop esf discipline.
If you can sacrifice another of the pre epic feats you will also have room for expertise.

IMO you should list AC in full plate without shield in this greatsword build. AC = 31

Why 13 DEX? When I started reading the build I was sure there will be a feat that needs that much dex (like Dodge or something), but there was none. Why dont you reduce Dex to 8, and make Int 14 / Con 16? You have the level 2 bard spell Cat's Grace, that way you can increase your Dex to 12 (+1 modifier, which is max if you intend wearing Full plate) This way you have lost nothing, but gained +40 skill points and hps. Or go for 8 Dex / 14 Int / 14 Cha, leaving you with 2 unused points. Put it to Wisdom for +1 Will save, or to Con / Cha (that way it gets to 15), and change an epic skill for Great Constitution / Charisma, depending on your choice.
