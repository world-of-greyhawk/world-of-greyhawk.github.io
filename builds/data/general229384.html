Your roll 1d20+AB vs AC if it hits and it's within the crit range the you "threathen a crit hit". This means that you roll again 1d20+AB vs AC and if you hit (doesn't have to be within threat range this time) then you "confirm" the crit and deal crit damage.

Keep present that to confirm the crit you need to actually hit the AC on the second roll (1d20+AB>=AC) without the 20=automatic hit, so very high AC foes might be practically immune to crit (since you can never confirm the crit)
