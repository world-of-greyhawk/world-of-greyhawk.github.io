What is your favorite rogue? Cleric 20 / Monk 1 / Rogue 19, take Epic Dodge and dual wield kamas, it rocks. I like the "Rogue Archer" in the main bioware epic build section. My two favorites are my "Sneaky Hobbit" build (Rogue 25/Fighter 10/SD 5, dex-based) or Grizz's "Zhentarim Field Agent" (Rogue 25/Fighter 14/SD 1, Str-based halfling wielding a scim as a 2-handed weapon). The dex-based one is a bit better in terms of skills and much better in terms of AC, but can have a tough time against sneak-immune or high-DR enemies. The str-based one can get into trouble against ED/SCV builds, but can dish out some serious damage. He's as good as a str-based fighter against sneak-immunes, and can dish out far more than most fighter builds to anything sneak-susceptible (13d6 sneaks on top of 15 points of str-bonus, and 6 from EWS, and a 12-20 crit range, means he can finish off most sneak-susceptible enemies in 1 round following a successful IKD from stealth).

TM Oh, if you like Monk/Assassin, you should also try Monk 9/SD 5/Assassin 26. You get HiPS and Epic Dodge that way, and still have an excellent Death Attack DC.

TM Fighter/CoT/Rogue is most excellent and playable even for beginners. Both Dex and STR versions. Fighter/SD/Rogue as mentioned. I like the Dex based Halfling of Doom, my revised Rogue13/SD1/Assassin26. The Tiny Reaper.
