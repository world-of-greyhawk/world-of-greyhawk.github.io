Using a mace would reduce criticals from x4 to x3, damage from 1d8 to 1d6, however would free up a feat to take as you see fit, blindfight initially springs to mind!

HOWEVER, whether you want to use Mace, Warhammer, Spear or otherwise, you may wish to consider dropping a further 3 Weapon Master levels in favour of more bard levels, this will give you access to 4th level spells such as Improved Invisibilty & Warcry, with +2 AC from song!
