Well, there's a problem you're going to run into. You need 30 DEX for self-concealment, but a dire mace isn't a finesseable weapon. Granted, 30 DEX will still be nice in terms of AC, but it will prevent you from pumping STR and thus prevent your AB from being as high. Perhaps either making it with a finesseable weapon (like dual-wielding maces) or using 10 levels of RDD to boost your STR to help your AB and damage.

If you want a build that starts with Str 18 and you don't need Int/Cha above 12, Half orc is a good option.
Str 18 only costs 10 buy points with half orc, while it costs 16 buy points with the other medium sized races. When you've spent 4 of the buy points you earned to overcome the Int/Cha penalty, you still have 2 more points to spend

Eg. Human: Str 18, dex10, con 14, int 10, wis 10, cha 10
Half orc: Str 18, dex 12, con 14, int 10, wis 10, cha 10

If you want a build that starts with Str 19 or 20, half orc is the only option
