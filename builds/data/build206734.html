Quote: Posted 08/02/05 01:51:59 (GMT) -- Kaliban99

Quick question:

Why no Weapon Spec and Epic Wpn Spec? Those would be useful to a dex-based dual-wielder.

Exactly. In a low-to-mid magic setting, he's going to have a hard time against all the sneak/crit immune critters.


Also, I would advise taking Knockdown and IKD over Expertise and Improved Expertise. Drop Toughness, and take WS instead. An epic-dodging SC halfling has no need of hitpoints.

You also manage only a 16BAB in spite of 10 Fighter levels pre-epic. I would consider taking 9 fighter and 11 Rogue and postponing that SD level until Epic. I would also take that 10th fighter level in Epic to nab your EWS and to top out discipline.

In regard to SD, I would actually take 10 levels of it in this build and take only 19 Rogue. If 19 Rogue can't deliver on what you need, chances are 29 Rogue won't either. But the 10 SD will give you better defenses, and pick up all you SC and Epic Dodge feats faster than what the Rogue side could deliver.

Finally, make sure you take Rogue on Lvls 12 and 17 so you can top up your Tumble skill for instant +1 to AC. You could also do this at level 3, and postpone that 2nd Ftr level to 4. Remember, at lower levels it's not his AB that's the wek point but his AC.

And one last thing: this definitely does not look right--
Quote: 27 Rogue (great dexterity II, epic weapon focus kukri)

You get a General Feat, which is Great Dex, and a Rogue Bonus Feat. But EWF is not a Rogue bonus feat. It looks as if you may have to adjust those 30th level feats you have slotted.
