heretics will have thier hearts givin to lolth on my sacrifical plate!!! Caster level remains unchanged by PM levels. In NWN, that's the way it is supposed to be. In PnP, I believe things differ.

DC has nothing to do with caster or character level, so DC is unaffected by PM levels. A DC is based off of the spell level, and your casting stat modifier. Caster level has nothing to do with it.

Spell penetration is also based off of caster level, as is dispellability, for what it's worth.

Incidentally, there are 'fixes' that add PM to your caster level for duration, and such. Some servers use them. You could also add them to a mod yourself. Personally, it seems to me that this is an awfully big power increase, and that from a class which does just fine, but to each his own.
