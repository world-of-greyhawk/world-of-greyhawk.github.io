I believe you get +1 AC/AB and saves with halfling but this is not reflected in the stats. If so, then add 1 to everything. He has low HPs but with all that stealthy protection, he does not need a lot. I prefer Expertise for an AC boost but Imp Knockdown is also viable and kinda necessary since he's a Halfling.

Most people do not like lots of levels of SD because the Shadow Evade only lasts 5 rounds. It's been my experience that on many persistent worlds, shadowdancers are boosted a bit to compensate for this. For example, where I play your shadow evade lasts 2 rounds per level of SD which on this build really pays off. Also, the epic Shadowlord has concealment further sweetening the deal.

Even without the enhancements, the extra feats in epic levels are the biggest bonus.
