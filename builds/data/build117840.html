Master of Defense (or 'Epic Dodge' and '50% Concealment' as soon as possible)

Rogue(20)/ShadowDancer(8)/Harper Scout(5):

Start with an elf (or halfling) with DEX 20 (Str 10, Dex 20, Con 10, Wis 10, Int 12, Cha 10):

Level 01: Rogue(1), Dodge
Level 02: Rogue(2), Evasion (RF)
Level 03: Rogue(3), Weapon Finesse, Uncanny Dodge I (RF)
Level 04: Rogue(4), DEX+1 -> DEX=21
Level 05: Rogue(5)
Level 06: Rogue(6), Mobility
Level 07: Rogue(7)
Level 08: Rogue(8), DEX+1 -> DEX=22
Level 09: Rogue(9), Iron Will
Level 10: Rogue(10), Move Silently=8, Hide=10, Tumble=5, Improved Evasion (RBF)
Level 11: ShadowDancer(1), HIPS (SDF)
Level 12: ShadowDancer(2), Alertness, DEX+1 -> DEX=23
Level 13: ShadowDancer(3)
Level 14: ShadowDancer(4)
Level 15: ShadowDancer(5), FEAT1, Defense Roll (SDF)
Level 16: ShadowDancer(6), DEX+1 -> DEX=24
Level 17: ShadowDancer(7), Slippery Mind (SDF)
Level 18: ShadowDancer(8), FEAT2
Level 19: Rogue(11)
Level 20: Rogue(12), Search=4, Persuade=8, Lore=6, Discipline=4, DEX+1 -> DEX=25
Level 21: Harper Scout(1), Great Dextery I, Great Dextery II (HSBF) -> DEX=27
Level 22: Harper Scout(2)
Level 23: Harper Scout(3)
Level 24: Harper Scout(4), Great Dextery III, DEX+1 -> DEX=29
Level 25: Harper Scout(5), Great Dextery IV (HSBF) -> DEX=30
Level 26: Rogue(13), Epic Dodge (RBF)
Level 27: Rogue(14), Tumble=30, Hide=30, Self Concealment I
Level 28: Rogue(15)
Level 29: Rogue(16), Self Concealment II (RBF)
Level 30: Rogue(17), Self Concealment III
Level 31: Rogue(18)
Level 32: Rogue(19), Self Concealment IV (RBF)
Level 33: Rogue(20), Self Concealment V

RF=Rogue Feat, RBF=Rogue Bonus Feat, SDF=Shadow Dancer Feat, HSBF=Harper Scout Bonus Feat

At level 28/32 you can put points in DEX too but it압 not necessary. At level 15/18 you can chose a free feat, take 'Shield Proficiency' if you want a better AC or 'Ambidextery'/'Two Weapon Fighting' (unfourtunately there압 no room for 'Improved Two Weapon Fighting' until level 36 ).

Never played this char so I don앖 know if he압 any good but he압 really a Master of Defense.

If you want to start with DEX 18 (because your char is human for example), you could take Self Concealment V at level 36 (and because of the extra feat at level 1 you can take all the three dual-wielding feats). There are a lot of other builds around rogue/HS/epic dodge/self concealment. You can drop the SD for example, take more rogue levels and add fighter (or champion of torm).

Also don앖 underestimate the epic Harper Scout. If you want to maximize dextery (or charisma) that압 the way to go.
