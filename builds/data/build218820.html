Although some may say dual wielding Dwarven Waraxes is overkill, I really like the wickedness of this guy's appearance, and felt it was truly needed after the Human Dwarven Waraxe wielder experience . Of course if dual wielding is not your mug o' ale, feel free to drop Dex to 8 or 10 and leave Ambi, TWF, and ITWF at the door. This would open up some room for nice-to-have feats.

An interesting anomaly came up during the building of this character. I've never done a Dwarven Waraxe wielder and was quite shocked to find out that it doesn't trigger the nice 'feature' of CoT to take Epic Wpn Spec. Thus the need for 6 Fighter levels, instead of my planned 4 (all pre-epic, mostly to obtain Weapon Specialization). I reported this oddity to Encylopedia Brown (Cinnabar Din) and was even more shocked to find that he didn't know about this anomaly! But to restore his reputation, he whipped up a test build and confirmed that CoT can not get Epic Weapon Spec in Dwarven Waraxe. Odd, yes? But true. So a nod of thanks to Cinnabar for his laboratory research
