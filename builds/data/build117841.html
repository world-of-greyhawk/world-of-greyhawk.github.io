Monk of the Defense Order

Monk(16)/Rogue(19)/Harper Scout(5):

Start with an elf (or halfling), lawful good/neutral with DEX 20 (Str 10, Dex 20, Con 10, Wis 12, Int 10, Cha 10):

Level 01: Rogue(1), Dodge
Level 02: Monk(1), Evasion (MF)
Level 03: Rogue(2), Weapon Finesse
Level 04: Rogue(3), Uncanny Dodge I (RF), DEX+1 -> DEX=21
Level 05: Monk(2)
Level 06: Monk(3), Weapon Focus Unarmed (or Kama)
Level 07: Rogue(4)
Level 08: Monk(4), DEX+1 -> DEX=22
Level 09: Rogue(5), Iron Will
Level 10: Monk(5)
Level 11: Rogue(6)
Level 12: Monk(6), Alertness, Knockdown (MF), Improved Knockdown (MF), DEX+1 -> DEX=23
Level 13: Rogue(7)
Level 14: Monk(7)
Level 15: Rogue(8), Improved Critical Unarmed (or Kama)
Level 16: Monk(8), DEX+1 -> DEX=24
Level 17: Rogue(9)
Level 18: Rogue(10), FEAT1, Defense Roll (RBF)
Level 19: Rogue(11)
Level 20: Rogue(12), Search=4, Persuade=8, Lore=6, Discipline=4, DEX+1 -> DEX=25
Level 21: Harper Scout(1), Great Dextery I, Great Dextery II (HSBF) -> DEX=27
Level 22: Harper Scout(2)
Level 23: Harper Scout(3)
Level 24: Harper Scout(4), Great Dextery III, DEX+1 -> DEX=29
Level 25: Harper Scout(5), Great Dextery IV (HSBF) -> DEX=30
Level 26: Monk(9), Improved Evasion (MF)
Level 27: Rogue(13), Tumble=30, Hide=30, Self Concealment I, Epic Dodge (RBF)
Level 28: Rogue(14)
Level 29: Rogue(15)
Level 30: Rogue(16), Self Concealment II, Self Concealment III (RBF)
Level 31: Rogue(17)
Level 32: Rogue(18)
Level 33: Rogue(19), Sneak Attack damage = 10d6, Self Concealment IV, Self Concealment V (RBF)
Level 34: Monk(10)
Level 35: Monk(11)
Level 36: Monk(12), FEAT2
Level 37: Monk(13)
Level 38: Monk(14)
Level 39: Monk(15), FEAT3
Level 40: Monk(16), Normal Attack Damage=1d20

MF=Monk Feat, RF=Rogue Feat, RBF=Rogue Bonus Feat, HSBF=Harper Scout Bonus Feat

At level 28/32/36/40 you can put points in DEX too but it´s not necessary. At level 18/36/39 you can chose a free feat.
