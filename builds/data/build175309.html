Now...to work the bug what you need to do is memorize 1 of each spell you like. Next in unshifted form hit the hotkey to cast the spell you want to attack with...once the icon comes up to target the enemy press the hotkey to shift into Drow form. Now finish casting the spell. What you may not have known is that the spell never gets marked as used in your spell book....so you now have unlimited IGMS and whatever else you want. Since you went to the pains of trying to match your characters looks to the shifted character it would be hard to tell for people when you are or are not shifted. If you could get a sword to hold unshifted with a cold aura and craft some armor to match the drows...they will never know.

Again...this is a gimicky...cheap sort of build, but with as long as NWN has been out now...sometimes you just want to find ways to amuse yourself in a different manner. Please...if you absoulty hate this whole build...just ignore it.

Skill wise I maxed hide..concentration..spell craft..tumble..discipline..craft armor...move silent.

I also choose a generalist, but in retrospect...and illusionist may have been better for the extra spells. Since I only have a 19 Int...spellslots are thin for the upper levels.

This build would actually be better as a kobold commando....since you wouldn't loose any strength, plus you get some great skill bonuses from the kobold. The problem is you wouldn't be able to hide what you were doing.

I choose evocation since I just wanted to be able to rip out so direct damage type spells before engageing in melee to dev crit.
