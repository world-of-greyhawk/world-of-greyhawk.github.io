Tis a shame you have opted for WM in this build. Solely due to the pre-reqs for it (Dodge, Mobility, Spring Attack, Expertise, Whirlwind Attack and Weapon Focus) thats 6 feats used just to qualify for the class.

At first glance I would suggest dropping Great Cleave as you are not going for Over or Dev Crit - Cleave should be sufficient.

Epic Toughness I? 20 Hit points Epic Prowess would do better here as your AB is a pretty low 36. The trouble is the three classes all rely on different requirements. WM & Barb is based on STR in this case and Druid for the WIS.

The Focus in CON seems a bit of a waste in epic levels, you would be better to take Dwarf to get the extra 2 points to CON at startup and the -2 to CHA penalty - that way one feat in epic levels can be dropped

Other Feats really worth considering:

Extend Spell
Still Spell
Combat Casting - if you want to cast in combat
Epic Prowess
Terrifying Rage (can be taken at 15th Barb level)
Epic Skill Focus: Intimidate
Epic Skill Focus: Taunt (if you put skill ranks in it)

Skills:

Consider the use of Taunt - Barb class skill
Spread your Druid levels across the life of the build to take advantage of the Spellcraft bonus.
Parry? - worthless it does not do what it says on the tin
Lore? - quite what this is in aid of on this class mix is unclear.
Persuade? - again not sure of the aim of this - I would opt to maximise Intimidate instead of Persuade/ Bluff given the lack of skill points.
Spot - perhaps take Listen over Spot - particularly as it is a class skill for Barbs and can be developed until 40th easier than Spot with WM.

Level Progression

Take 4 Druid Levels pre-epic only - to preserve your BAB at 19, or better yet wait until 21st to preserve it at 20. Doing that will allow you take all 7 WM levels pre-epic - which can only improve your melee effectiveness.

Revisit your starting stats - consider dropping STR to 14 and bumping DEX, CON, WIS and INT to 14 - the reason being that all these four will then benefit from a +2 modifier.

DEX - the reason being that this is the highest value you can get in Medium Armor wearing Breast Plate (you do not have access to Heavy Armor Proficiency as a Barbarian or WM).

CON - this frees up a bonus stat during progression - swap for STR

WIS - fress up a bonus stat during progression - swap for STR.

INT - gives you another skill you can max out

From a personal view point I would drop Weapon Master, probably for either Bard or CoT - Bard for the Song, Skillset (including Tumble); CoT for the increase in Feats and Saves.

Either Way hope this comments help you.
