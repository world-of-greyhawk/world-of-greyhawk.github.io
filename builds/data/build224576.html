Two straight and very similar builds to slice through your enemies like there's no tomorrow. High AB, KD/IKD, called shot, blind fight, high damage output, decent saves (will apart), DR 9/- this guy is a melee master. My only regret is dwarves incredibly do not get weap prof: dwarven waraxe for free and therefore I had no room for it at all in the first build. In the second one could move FTR 7-8 preepic to make room for WP: exotic, foregoing 1 epic feat, which ends up to be epic thoughness after you rearrange feat selection (get EWS with CoT), so it's dwarven waraxe at the cost of 20 HP.
First build gets +1 AB, second build +1 ST and +20 HP or +1 ST and WP:exotic (for dwarven waraxe). Just pick yours.
