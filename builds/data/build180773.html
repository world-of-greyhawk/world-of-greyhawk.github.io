This is a variation on the typical Rogue archer build. It's an AA build that actually has a decent # of Wizard levels. It still gets decent damage and AB, it gets Epic Dodge, and it's quite effective with traps. It can use spells to boost both it's AC as well as it's AB, and with both Epic Dodge and %50 concealment from Improved Invis, it can be difficult to hit.
