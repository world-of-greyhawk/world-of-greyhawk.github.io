Based somewhat on this build by stravinsky, and I looked for other similar builds in the search engine but found none. At anyrate, I was working on this two years ago, too.

What is has:
Devastating Critical
Terrifying Rage
Level 8 Cleric spells, maxed cleric buffs
Concealment from cast Improved Invisibility
DR II + Epic DR III (DR 11/-)
Massive hitpoints (600 max)

Walks a tightrope on the feats and ability score increases. Make one mistake and it's over. Do it right and get everything a melee character wants and more. Suffers a 20% XP penalty from level 19 to level 21, but none before or after that.

Very nice. I like this build a lot. Barbarbian/Cleric/Cot has other options, too. The DR 11/- is nice, but not all that great. By dropping CON to 16 to start and raising INT to 12, you get 43 more skill points, +3 STR, and four additional epic feats. Or you could start with 14 WIS as well and gain +6 stats in other areas to start, and use the COT levels to raise your WIS to 18. Then you could take epic skill focus Intimidate and epic reflexes. Combined with 40 ranks in spellcraft, you'd never fail a save. By Dropping CoT levels to 8 you could raise barb level to 16 and get an additional rage. Or stay at level 15 Barb and raise Cleric levels to 17 for a few Implosions. Can run for a long time on +12 max CON and STR cap by casting bull's/endurance, then extended aura of vitality, then greater rage (+2+4+6=12). This would raise his Dev Crit DC to 45. Could also raise DEX or CHA a bit instead (dex for reflex saves, CHA for Terrifying and making the War Domain Power last longer). Or you could forego Dev crit if it is disabled and raise CON to obscene levels.

I think it's a pretty sweet class combination and I'm surpised there haven't been more builds based on it.

Concealment is still valuable. 50% less damage from attacks missing, coupled with the DR, means he'll going to take 60 to 100% less damage per attack against him. Concealment also applied to touch and ranged touch attacks (such as Harm), so it can prevent even more.
