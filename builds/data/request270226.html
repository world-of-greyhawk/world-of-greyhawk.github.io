Without the benefit of Weapon buffs, you are going to have less AB and Damage than a regular weapon user.

That leaves a few alternatives for damage sources.

1) sneak damage
2) stunning/IKD opponents
3) curse song, Wounding Wispers
4) Mage Acid Sheath/Elemental Shield/Death Armor
5) Divine Favor
6) Smite
7) Palemaster fancy touch attacks


You can't dev crit with an animals creature weapon.
You can't Weapon Focus/specialize an animals creature weapon.
You can't add poison to a creature weapon (see assasin/blackguard affinity to do that).

===

So, as a big bad wolf, you'll have to think of HOW you are actually going to do damage. Expecting to rely only upon biting is not going to work.
