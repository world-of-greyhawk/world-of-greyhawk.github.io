Mord's has two effects:
1) An improved "Breach" effect that can affect both single targets and groups. This works against a specified list of defensive spells.
2) A "Dispel Magic" effect with a 1d20 roll + 1/lvl up to +20 modifier, that can target a singel target or a group.
The DC to beat with the "Dispell" part is 11 + Level of the caster that made the spells you want to dispell.

You will be undispellable by Mord's if you have 30 caster levels.
You can still be "breached" by Mord's and the other Breach spells.
