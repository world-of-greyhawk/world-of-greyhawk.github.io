Singing Death (Sorcerer 24 / Bard 16)
playable 1-40 PvM

I've been looking at trying different arcane casters, to see what can bring the most to a party. This is what i've come up with, as you get your full Sorc spells (plus a coulple of Epic) and a Ok Bard song, then you can use the Bard spells as most of your buff spells. As when I play a Sorc I find it a hard choice between what buffs to take and what offensive spells. The bard song is not to bad, and you have curse song to lower the saves so your Necro spells have more chance of working.
