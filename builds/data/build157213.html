This char is made for PvP, but can be used at Persistant Worlds. If you are going to play at a PW you can do this:
- Take Strength instead of Dexterity
- Take Great Strength instead of Great Dexterity (duh)
- Take Devastating Critical instead of Epic Skill Focus: Concentration (you can take it as a Weapon Master Bonus Feat also)
- Switch Toughness and Weapon Finesse
- Remove Weapon Finesse
- Add Overwelming Critical where you had Weapon Finesse
- Remove 2 Wizard Lvls (which you remove is up to you, i recommend the 2 last 1s, because its good to get True Strike early)
