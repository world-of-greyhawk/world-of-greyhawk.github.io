Purple Dragon Knight

Prerequisites: BAB +4, Mounted Combat, intimidate 1, listen 2, persuade 1, ride 2, spot 2.

Casting stat: Strength
Primary Saving Throw: Fortitude
BAB: +1/level
Skill Points: 2 + Int modifier
Class Skills: Craft Weapon, Craft Armor, Craft Trap, Discipline, Intimidate, Lore, Persuade, Ride

Level 1
Rallying Cry: +1 AB, +20% Movement Speed to all non-hostile creatures within 10m for 1 round (Usable 3x per day).

Heroic Shield: +4 dodge AC to one friendly creature (but not the caster) for one round (Infinite times per day)

Level 2
Inspire courage: +1 AB, +2 vs. mind spells to all non-hostile creatures within 10m for 10 rounds (PDK level/2 uses per day).

Level 3
Fear: Causes all targets in a medium area to become afraid for a number of rounds equal to the caster's character level if they fail a will save vs. fear with a DC of 10 + PDK levels + strength modifier of caster. (Usable once per day)

Level 4
Oath of wrath: +2 to attack, damage, saves, and skills of caster vs. a targeted non-friendly creature's race for a number of rounds equal to twice the PDK level. (Usable once per day).

Level 5
Final stand: 2d10 temporary hit points to a number of non-hostile creatures up to the charisma modifier + PDK level for a number of rounds equal to charisma modifier + PDK level. (Usable once per day).

---No further levels to the class---

Multiclassing tips:

High strength builds may find the fear spell handy when tackling many enemies.

The Purple dragon benefits provide a quick short term boosts to AB which may be quite useful to defensive builds allowing them to go on a short aggressive spree.

Classes with high APR can often make the most of PDK benefits. Though the class is designed much more around aiding a party than going solo.

Disadvantages:

Although this is a party build it does little to help a solo caster's summon at level 40. Besides the +1 AB from inspire courage, it is generally better if the caster concentrates on other things than aiding the summon for a round.

There are very few uses for PDK, especially in higher levels as high level abilities tend to outdo the short less effective buffs.
