Playable from 1-40... PvM build

This build was built at the same time as the shadow warrior, the idea was to try and build and effective build that had both devastating critical and epic dodge. Unlike most rogues, it can fight well against enemies with damage reduction but it still has a good hide and move silently skills. The paladin levels were chosen to allow the character to max out its taunt skill to help it with its AB trouble and for the immunity to fear, which is quite useful. This is really the shadow warrior for those times where there is no benefit to HiPS. There is an XP penalty associated with this build, but if you can tolerate it, it is playable from 1 to 40.

Advantages

-Devastating Critical. Unfortunately this build doesn't have a fortitude save high enough to resist said feat, luckily he is hard to hit.
-Epic Dodge. If he uses a shield he has a pretty good AC, so this feat does help.
-8D6 Sneak Attack. With improved knockdown he can get alot of sneaks in. This does help against characters that defend themselves from devastating critical with high fortitude saving throws.
-High use magic devices. Good to provide some spell casting ability, this also lets the character use monk equipment.
-Improved Evasion and a good reflex saving throw. This character has some defence from evocations.
-Immune to Fear.
-Immune to Disease.

Disadvantages

-Lowish AB. With a sword and shield he has a 40 AB unbuffed, this isn't bad for a fighter rogue but it could be better. He has a 42 (41) taunt, this has a chance against an opponent with a maxed out concentration (depending on its constitution). The 38 AB while dual wielding should be good against mobs, against a serious threat he should go shield and sword instead.
-Low hitpoints. Hopefully with epic dodge and Improved Evasion he can last a while.
-Abysmal fortitude and will saves. Enchantments and Death Spells will be havoc for him.
-XP penalty
