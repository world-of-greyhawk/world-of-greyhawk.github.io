Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

This character was originally posted as an alternative to the high STR rogue. Any enemy that is vulnerable to sneak attack is also vulnerable to devastating critical. So this character was designed to either be able to use devastating critical against his enemy or get a +7+2D6 damage bonus. He was posted on February 10th,
Click Here

Advantages

-Devastating Critical.
-Evasion. The character has low saves, but this still affords some spell protection.
-Hide in Plain Sight. More of a tactical advantage than anything else.
-Quite a bit of damage against his favoured enemies.

Disadvantages

-Limited number of favoured enemies.
-Low saves.
-Low hit points.
-No spell casting ability. Well he can cast Ranger spells but, except for invisibility purge and greater magic fang, those don't count.
