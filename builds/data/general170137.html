3. How does damage reduction stack? If i have a 6/30 armor and epic damage reduction 3 9/- what exactly does that mean?
DR doesn't stack, except the DR from barbarian, DwD and epic DR feats. The best is always taken when two are considered.
DR "+X/Y" or "Y/+X" or "+X soak Y" means that if you are hit by a weapon with an enchantement lower than X, you deduce up to Y physical damage. For example, "30/+5" stops 30 damage from any weapon +4 or lower. "9/-" Means that you block 9 damage from any physical damage source, whatever the enchantement may be.
