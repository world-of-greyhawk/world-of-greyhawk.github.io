The main difference is that this build has Devastating Critical and uses a Scythe (and is therefore not a halfling), which has a x4 critical multiplier. Most battles usually take maximum 2 hits, occasionally a few more when up against "impossible" opponents. I can't remember what the max damage is it can do (usually enemies don't survive a dev crit hit anyway), but 200 damage per hit is common, maximum should be around 280 with a standard scythe. With a Frost Scythe (+5 enchantment, keen, massive criticals: +2d12 damage) you can easily go over 300.
