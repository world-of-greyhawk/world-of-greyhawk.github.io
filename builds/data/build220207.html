If you want a bit more AC you could add a third class to the mix:

A single monk level would be great, but it has alignment issues.

A single rogue level can give you access to tumble, if taking it to 40 you get 8 extra AC (7 extra in your build). And 1d6 sneak attack (not a great deal). Taking 2 rogue gives you evasion, what starts being a good deal . Also access to UMD for wands/scrolls/rods and gear.

A single bard level can give you access to tumble, UMD and spellcraft (40 spellcraft == +8 to saves vs spells) plus a weak bardsong (+1dmg +1AB during 10 rounds once per rest).
