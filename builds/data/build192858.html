The enhancement bonus to arrows improves on odd numbered arcane acher levels. What about Bard 9 / Fighter 4 / AA 27?
Looses 1 AB, but gets more skill points and spell power ... Diggin' this one up as it seems to be a popular class combo for AA munchkinheads. Surprised it's the only build posted!

As I understand it, you need to have two bard levels for you can take your first AA. Bards only have cantrips at first level, and 1st-level arcane spell-casting is the AA requirement. I would suggest taking the bard levels at 3, 7, 12, and 40. Taking them at levels 3 and 12 gives you the opportunity to take Extra Music, Lingering Song, or Curse Song and leverage the bard song a little bit at lower levels. You may have to sacrifice some currrent skills/feats to do this, but it's something to consider.

I'm guessing (but don't know) that Imbue Arrow gets maximum damamge well before level 27/28, so I'd go with Fighter 9/Bard 4/AA 27. Also, I *think* you need to take the 8th fighter level at 22 to get the epic weapon spec.
