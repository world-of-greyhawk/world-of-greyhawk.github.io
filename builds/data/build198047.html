Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

This is my first build here, dont be too hard with me .

I know it is not the uberest archer build, I planned this build to make him easy to play through any level and have things I think all archers(and all other dex builds) should (better speed than monsters and Uncanny Dodge -Isn't it Indispensable for dex builds?-, tumble). I also wanted him to get the best possible enchant arrow and being able to slighly buff myself.

Anyway, i fear he is a bit item-dependant, make sure you find a good composite bow and, if necessary, a str boosting item to fill the mighty slots your str cant (Remember you can cast bulls str and cats grace ). Later in the game you will be able to use monk/mage robes that wont give any casting penalty (Anyway, I only use casting for buffs, undress your armor, cast them and then dress it back).

Half-Elf //To avoid xp penalty, if you dont care go Elf

Str: 14 // To be able to carry some loot and make mighty bows a bit useful
Dex: 16 (Ending at 34) // This is our main stat here, AB and AC
Con: 10 // We wont be meleeing, we dont really need lots of HP, Toughness will help here
Wis: 8 // What can I say? We arent Yoda
Int: 14 // Skills, skills and more skills, you can leave it at 10 and put the points somewhere else
cha: 14 // A bit of casting, 13 is enough for bard level 3 spells (but I dont like odd stats) If you dont need level 3 spells you can leave it at 12 and use those extra points somewhere

An alternative could be:

str: 16 // A +3 Modifier with extra 4str from Bull's str (if lucky) can allow you to efficiently use a Composite longbow +5
// without needing any magic str boosting item.
dex: 16 (34)
con: 10
wis: 8
int: 10 // Forget about spot and maxing discipline (UMD is important here, we want monk clothes).
cha: 12

You can play with those stats as much as you wish, remember leaving cha at least at 12 to not to make bard levels useless.

If you are playing in a low magic world without haste items you can invest more levels on bard and less on AA in order to be able to cast extended haste, just take extend spell instead of dodge.
