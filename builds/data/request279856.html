You can get EMA as early as level 27; it is just up to you how much of your AB you want to sacrifice and how late you want dev crit

Just remember to take your PM level 16 on a natural feat level so that you can double up on the Epic spells. Also; if you push your PM levels lower in the build I would suggest taking a 6th wizard level just after PM 16 (or closer to 40 if you prefer; to max out Spellcraft) and finish up at fighter 18 instead of 19. The last wizard level will let you select two more spells on level-up and utilize those higher level spellslots for any critical fills.
