Do not meddle in the affairs of Dragons, for you are crunchy and taste good with ketchup. You need at least 16 monk levels total to get 1d20 damage, 5 SD, and then the rest can be fighter if you like. I'd take monk to 18 at least to get empty body. So, you could so monk(18)/SD(5)/Fighter(17), or something like that. You'd have to be dex based, so pump that dex and get weapon finesse. If you want to completely maximize your AB, you could go monk(4)/fighter(16) pre-epic, but you might be better off saving more of those fighter levels for epic to get more epic feats. So, maybe something like monk(8)/fighter(12) pre-epic, for 18 BAB, and then you'll have a couple more fighter bonus feats in epic. Something like that at least.
