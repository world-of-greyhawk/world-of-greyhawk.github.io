Hello everyone,

I created and tested this build as a level 40 build with no magic items in the character training module with a +20 monster powerup, and it did OK. My guess is that it's not totally unplayable PvM, but I haven't tested that yet. If you do decide to play it, watch out for the alignment shift at level 20. Other than that it's a great build.
