A redesign of a previous character I posted... The Dwarven Wall. This character is designed to have damage reduction 35 (the most possible without an alignment change) and devastating critical. He's got a lot of hit points, and is designed around the theory that it doesn't matter if you get hit if you can't be hurt. He's good at moving around in combat, and when overwhelmed he can mix whirlwind attack and defensive stance to devastating results.

I redesigned this so that you could get the crit feats sooner, and do more damage earlier. There's only so many hit points you need at low levels, so I took the stat points in strength and saved the epic feats for getting the pre-requisites for damage reduction. The end result is he's a lot more useful at early levels, and is viable from level 1 on as a good melee build. Before anyone asks, I took iron will because otherwise you'd get dominated by a first level kobold with a charm person scroll.
