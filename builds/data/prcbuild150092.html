This build was inevitable, so I went ahead and did it in my spare time, even if its not my usual style. . .

You always see posts about people who want their Dragon builds to have DevCrit, but since Bios classes dont support DevCrit(creatture), it cant happen without the benefit of the very nice PnPShifter class. Besides the pre-reqs for both DevCrit and Dragon Shape are very high, and only a very few builds could do it. Well, here is one of those builds, for the Crit-inclined. . . .

The minimum pre-reqs for Bios DevCrit are not quite as documented, and PnPShifters DevCrit did it too. This build takes advantage of that, although it doesnt really NEED to, to work. Weapon Focus is not really required. This is more than just a 2da mistake, I think; more of a limitation of the current 2da format. As I see it, they dont have enough "feat required" slots to do all pre-reqs for Overwhelming Crit. Weapon Focus is a natural thing to want though, but in a feat-restricted build like this, I chose not to get it.

These shapes (and some lesser shapes as well) will all have access to DevCrit(creature). All but Construct is available in this build, although if you want Construct, you can take one of the other shapes out.
