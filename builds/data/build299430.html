This quick and cunning charmer is a lethal mix of knives and evil. Able to launch 6 attacks per round, he can not only bring 13d6 sneak per blade, but Epic Weapon Specialization and Divine Damage into each thrust as well. Supported by Epic Dodge, the ability to set, disarm and recover Deadly Traps, UMD scrolls, wands and Monk gear, and to unhinge his opponents with a 47 Taunt score, the Halfling Blade Runner is as versatile as he is sly, and has a weapon for every situation.

Notes: There was only one Halfling Fighter/Rogue/Blackguard in the guild (a crying shame that is), and he was a dart thrower: Master Thrower (FTR 14 / Rogue 13 / BG 13) by Kreestopearq, and his sole purpose was to do nifty damage with Darts. The two builds also differ in that The Blade Runner gets a slightly higher Dex and Chr score, and also goes much deeper into the Rogue class.

There were a few other Ftr/Rogue/Bg combos, but not many, and they were all strength-based critters. I actually was pleased at how this one turned out. It is able to do that one thing that many Dexers have difficulty with, and that’s finding a way to damage sneak-immune opponents. He can stand and deliver divine projectiles afar with a sling, mine the battlefield, deliver decent sneak from the shadows, and, if needed go toe-to-toe in combat with Epic Dodge to give him the edge. I chose Crippling Strike near the end of the build because it looks sexy, but it’s not vital to the build, and can easily be substituted for Slippery Mind or an Epic Skill Focus. I didn’t bother with Discipline in this build, and quite frankly, he couldn’t afford it without dropping starting Dex, but I don’t think it’s worth it.
