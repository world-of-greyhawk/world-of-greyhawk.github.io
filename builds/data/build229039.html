A couple of notes:
This is a tough build. unless you like putting icepicks through your ears you may want to pass.
XP PENALTY and ALIGNMENT SHIFTS required for this build.(I did try to push off the xp penalty and shifting as much as I could though)

Why the crap stats for str/dex?
Dragonshape overwrites those, so unless you have armor with str/dex buffs, its gone anyway.

Why Maximize instead of Empower Spell?
I am conservative. I prefer knowing I am getting the max of anything as opposed to maybe getting a little more. For example, I know I will alway get a str buff of 5, which is enough because that 5 does carry over to DS + greater rage = 11. Now I only need armor with a +1 str to max Dragon strength for max AB while shifted.

I also left off 53 skill points for you to put where ever you want

IIRC you cannot take metamagic feats on non caster lvls.

About STR buffs, don't forget aura of vitality, you won't need any equipment to reach the cap.

XP penalties and alignment shifts are always a pain,but some builds are worth it. If alignment shifts are easy enough, I'd even go so far as to take monk lvls preepic (12 Barb/Druid 4/Monk4) which would improve your saves, then carefuling lvling up to minimize xp penalties.
It is true that DS overides STR, DEX and CON but you get it only at 36th lvl, and you are putting points into CHA...I'd seriously consider starting with at least 12 STR and 10 CHA instead since you have to stay alive (and carry things around) until that time.

I'd take extend spell before maximise and as a minor note spellcraft counts modified ranks, not base ranks (unlike tumble)
