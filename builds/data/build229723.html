Why are you taking all the SP feats with 33 caster lvls? You don't need any additional help to beat maximum toolset SR. I would advise spell foci in evocation instead, starting with 17-18 WIS and end with higher WIS, drop maximise spell (you are already getting empower) and try to get AS too (depends on starting stas/Great WIS slots).
Actually I question the usefulness of DwD in the first place. You'd be better off with FTR 6, granting you WS/EWS, free martial WP and 2 additional feats, 3 if we consider you don't need to take dodge.
