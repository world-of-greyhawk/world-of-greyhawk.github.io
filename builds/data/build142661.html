One thing that I think some people miss with ranger builds is that you get the most benefit by taking 21 levels of Ranger and grabbing Bane of Enemies (I believe +2 ab and +2d6 dmg vs. favored enemies). To me, that feat completes rangers. Especially builds so close to lvl 21. Otherwise, I love CoT and most builds that use them.

-EC Ya definately try swapping an early CoT level with another ranger level. Then at level 39 you can take your 21st ranger level and get bane of enemies instead of epic toughness 1.
