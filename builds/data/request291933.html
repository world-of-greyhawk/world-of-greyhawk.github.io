You seem to be slightly confused about the use of finessable weapons. The feat weapon finesse; http://nwn.wikia.com/wiki/Weapon_finesse can only be used in conjunction with a small weapon i.e. one that can be wielded one-handed for all races. The only exception to this is the rapier, which AFAIK cannot be finessed when used two-handed (with a gnome/halfling).

Normally the only stat which determines your AB bonus is strength. Finessing a weapon enables you to change your AB according to dexterity, not strength. Which means that the only way to use any weapon other than something small or a rapier and achieve any kind of AB is to focus on strength.

Hope this helps,
Kurth
