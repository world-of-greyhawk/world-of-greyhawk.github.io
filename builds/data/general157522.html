Joe C. To calculate your BAB, look at the table "Base Saves and Attacks for all classes" in your hotu manual.

Add up the first AB rating for each class. Eg: If you have a 6Ftr/8Rogue/6SD, your BAB would be

6(from Ftr) + 6 (from Rogue) + 4 (from SD) = 16.

To get 4 attacks per sound, your pre-epic BAB has to be 16.
