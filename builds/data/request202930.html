This wouldn't actually work. A cleric(7)/bard(1) only has a BAB of 5, and you need a BAB of 6 to take Arcane Archer. So, you'd need to move another level of bard or cleric before your first AA level.

I'd honestly probably use less bard...maybe something like cleric(15)/bard(2)/AA(23). Or maybe if you wanted another AA bonus feat, cleric(12)/bard(2)/AA(26). Take the last bard level very late to max tumble and UMD. 15 levels of cleric maxes out a number of your cleric buffs, although 12 takes you most of the way to maxing them out if you could use the extra feat.

Rapid shot is definitely one you want to pick up, but the power attack/improved power attack feats are for melee weapons only. Power Attack is still nice if you want to use Divine Might and Shield though.
