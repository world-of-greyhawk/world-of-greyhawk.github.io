Advantages:

  • High buffed and unbuffed AB
  • High Saves (36(44) /42(50) /36(44) buffed)
  • High AC (buffed mundane robe = 58) & Improved Invis for SC 50%
  • Level 9 Cleric Spells and Extend spell, can easily get ICC if desired.
  • Monk speed, IKD, Blind Fight etc
  • With Haste, 5 attacks made at 60+ AB a round
  • Average HP, 616 buffed
  • Resists Combat Feats: 54 Discipline, 59 buffed

Disadvantages:

  • Low Cleric DCs, buffing not offensive.
  • No Uncanny Dodge, but True Sight to compensate. If TS nerfed, max Spot.
  • Damage fair, good for a Dex build. 1-6 + 25 + DF dmg (11-16) on main hand.


Comments:

26 Cleric levels ensure only Mordenkainen's Disjunction can strip buffs.
6 Monk levels for IKD
Flexibility: If you want more skill points eg for Hide, change STR to 10 and INT to 14 = another maxed skill.
If you want CC and Improved Combat casting swap out Extend spell and ESF:Disc

In short, this sort of build is why Kamas have a tendency to be banned on PWs and PvP arenas.
