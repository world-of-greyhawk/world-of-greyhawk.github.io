Thanks. If you want Dev. Crit. as soon as possible, you will probably want to take RDD levels. You will need either Bard or Sorc to qualify for RDD, so you'll be either a Bard/Cleric/RDD or a Sorc/Cleric/RDD.

A good example of a Bard/Cleric/RDD would probably be the Blade Juggernaut by cdaulepp. I believe chndragon has a Sorc/Cleric/RDD. Either build is feasible; although the Bard/Cleric/RDD would probably be more effective in melee, the Sorc/Cleric/RDD should be a stronger caster.
