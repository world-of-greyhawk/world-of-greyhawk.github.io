On the server I play, there is alot of magical properties on weapons so dmg comes primarily from that. I debated on posting the build due to the reason that the dmg is dependent on the enchantments on the weapons, but it's normally the case with kukri wielders. You either use it to get sneak dmg or have some type of decent enchantment on the weapon to crit with; so, for those servers where high magic weapons are available, this is an alternative character build. I played a varient of it on another high magic server with less magical properties and I was critting for 120s whereas on the current server I play on, I crit for 250+ to give you an idea. You can imagine what the epic mobs do to you in return so the dr, epic dodge, and hp help.

The character can solo, but when I see builds, it's hard to say how good they really are until they are tested on one's own server. Against high ab, low dmg critters, it works wonders - against AAs for example. It's also a party player and the first to run into battle to soak up the arrows, horrids, and yes, even hellballs.
