Just trying my hand at an AA build. I wanted high AB, and Uncanny Dodge in the build, so that's why I took Elf and Barbarian. Elf allows me to min/max to start with the 20 DEX, thus getting amost as much DEX as possible in the game. Barbarian provides UD and a bit of extra HPS (as well as 2 uses of Rage for STR and CON boost) while not sacrificing BAB like Rogue would. Bard gives a bit of boost through Song, along with Tumble and UMD. There are only two of these in the guild. Fernandooa's Solid Archer build is quite similar, but the Singing Sniper has higher AB and AC and distributes the feats a little more efficiently IMO. Singing Sniper also has less Bard in order to eliminate the XP penalty that would ensue and also needs less CHA. Anyway, it's different enough to warrant posting, I think.

I envision this character as strictly a bow user, which is why I opted for Dodge and Mobility. Mobility grants a +4 bonus to AC vs AoOs, which will be generated by using the bow in close quarters. Finesse and Toughness would probably be a good idea if you're not comfortable with the AoOs.

HPs are low, but he's not really intended to get into the fray that much anyway. He's a sniper and thus is intended to be at long range for his attacks. Called Shot will slow down anything trying to get to him, and if he has to cut and run, he's got the Barbarian speed boost to help out.

Saves are mediocre to poor, other than the Reflex save.

AC is good, and the AB and damage with a bow is excellent, as he has Enchant Arrow XV for +15 AB and damage.

Bard provides a level 6 Song, which gives a bit of a boost to AB and damage. Also, access to level 2 spells gives the opportunity to bost DEX with Cat's Grace as well as another spell of your choice for utility purposes (although you only get 3 castings of your level 2 spells per day).
