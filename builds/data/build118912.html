This character was never posted, and I was quite displeased with the results.

Advantages

-This character has a potential 10 attacks per turn. Coupled with divine damage bonuses that apply to both weapons, and he can do alot of damage.
-Evasion. This character has pretty good saves that can be increased fairly easily, he should be fairly spell resistant.
-Deflect arrows.
-Access to level 8 cleric spells.

Disadvantages

-One Mordenkainen's Disjunction and he goes from hero to zero.
-If he can't rest, he's no good.
-34 AB. Buffs make up for this a bit, but he couldn't hit the broadside of a barn with a H-bomb.
-Only 368 hitpoints.
