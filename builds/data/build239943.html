I run the Pre-Epic Builders guild. Join and share your experience. Unfortunately I have to agree with other posters... you've got far too many paladin levels than you can benefit from.

11 wis only allows you to cast 1st-level paladin spells, you need 12 wis to cast 2nd level ones, which would be useful and a reason to take 10 paladin levels. You'd be better off dropping the paladin levels to 2 and wisdom to 10, and putting the 1 point from wisdom into intelligence for skill points. Pal 2 gives you all the useful features of the paladin class as far as your build is concerned (you don't need turn undead since you have weak cha and aren't taking divine might/shield).

I'd split it as fighter 10/paladin 2/WM 28. Take a look at Mithdradates
Heartseeker build for some ideas on this.

I'm assuming that you know dual wielding katanas is a style-over-substance choice... but your ITWF feat comes really late for that. Blind-fighting is unacceptably late, also. I'd consider dropping KD/IKD, or delaying them, to get ITWF and blind-fighting earlier.

You can get a higher strength (and thus more damage and AB), I think, if you make better use of your WM epic bonus feats and tinker with your level progression a little.
