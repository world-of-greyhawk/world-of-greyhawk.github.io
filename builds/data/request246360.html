Notes: This build gets the full attack scheme with 4 attacks per round, and it gets Epic Weapon Specialization from the Fighter levels; it makes full use of the Fighter Class and squeezes out all it can.

I took the 10 RDD levels for the higher Hit Points through Constitution, as well as the immunities to things like Flame Weapon, Darkfire, and all Fire Oriented Spells which are commonly used on my server.

There are a few feats that are subject to change such as Armour Skin and Blindfight. Empowered Spell may take the place of Blindfight of that won't bother you, and Armour Skin might be changed for Epic Skill Focus: Discipline. IKD is an issue on my server, and a +10 Bonus will go nicely with my exceedingly high Strength.

--------------------

Strengths and Strategies:

-Darkness/Ultravision is one of my favourites. It gives an incredible advantage if you have Ultravision and your opponent doesn't, while under the presence of Darkness. Luckily, this can be done even while wearing your Full Plate and Tower Shield.

-Taunt will give you the edge over spell-focussed casters by forcing them into attack, and lowering their chance of success in their castings.

-Curse Song will work well when brought together with Darkness and Taunt. It succesfully furthers the damage done to your opponents condition, making them all the easier to actually hit and kill.

-Dirge, though a Save is applied and your DC's are low, is still effective. Since it has a duration, the enemy will have to make multiple Saves and may eventually fail one, which will even further their growing weaknesses. If you have the first initiative, this may be a good spell to cast in the area the fight will take place.

-With a huge Critical Threat range, and enormous Strength, a Critical Hit will land around 60-80 Damage, with 30-40 on a normal hit which is quite nice with 5 attacks per round, hasted.

--------------------

Weaknesses:

-Grossly low Saves. All are below 25, which is somewhat disheartening.

-Not really a weakness, but I wish I had more Skill Points, but I can't sacrifice any Attribute Points to Intelligence.

-Cannot cast while in combat due to Arcane Spell Failure, so if buffs get Dispelled, they will not be coming back until the fight is over.

-If dispelled, the AB is greatly diminished along with AC. It is a major concern, though if there is time to cast Expedius Retreat, not all is lost.

How many levels of Bard did you have? Bards learn new spells at level X, but can't cast them until level X+1 unless their CHA modifier grants a bonus spell of that level. For example, a level 16 Bard knows 2 level 6 spells, but cannot cast any of them until Bard level 17, unless he has a CHA of 22 (which grants a bonus level 6 spell per day). If you look at the table of Bard spellcasting in one of the manuals, you'll see an entry that shows 0 casts per day. This means you can only cast spells of that level with a Bard of a particular level if you get bonus spells from high CHA.
