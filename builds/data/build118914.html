Playable from level 1 - 40 ..PvM Build

This character was the third competitor for the Generalist. At the time I didn't realize that to hit bonuses didn't stack over 20. With that considered, now, I would have probably posted this guy as the generalist.

Advantages

-AB of 53 with normal weapons and no buffs. This is the second best you can get as a Human.
-Evasion. Okay, the character has low saves, but that's what scarabs of protection are for.
-Use Magic Devices. Why take cleric and mage levels, when you can cast any cleric or mage spell from a scroll.
-Devastating critical.
-Does quite a bit of damage per hit. He can punch through the damage reduction of most enemies.

Disadvantages

-Low Saves.
