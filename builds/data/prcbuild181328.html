Since I had yet to see a build which made use of the wonderful Oozemaster class, I decided to create one. What you get is a completely crit immune and sneak immune dragon with 40/- DR, that can cast 9th lvl Cleric and Druid spells in human form.

Due to the Intuitive Attack feat he gets a decent AB with his Scimitar, especially with Cleric buffs. With a combination of Cleric and Druid buffs (mostly Cleric), he can boost his AB by a whopping 23-24 points, which is enough to allow him to break 60 AB in Human form.

In dragon form he loses 8 AB due to the fact that he doesn't get the Weapon Focus feats and his weapon enhancement doesn't merge, but you gain +9 due to having 48 base Str, so you actually come out a point ahead, meaning that you can get up to 63 AB with this build.

Although you don't get the Improved Knockdown feat, you're an ancient Dragon with a +63 AB, so you should still be able to knock some people down, and with 17 Druid levels your breath weapon should be able to do some damage too.
