A ranger designed with flexibility and stealth in mind (and quite a lot of FEs). Probably has been done a lot of times before but anyhow... My version uses the bow preferentially but can also melee if needed. Gets ED. If you're going to play this, choose what you want to focus on more and tailor the feats accordingly (maybe sacrifice an FE or two, take crit/spec in shortswords, get IKD).
