.."..".. Alternatively you could drop CHA to 16 and pump your WIS up instead, taking about 11 levels of Cleric, 10 of COT and 19 of Barb.

This lets you drop Armor Prof Heavy, allows you to cast in armor, increases your will saves and provides a whole host of buffs that can help you. Take Extend Spell instead to allow your buffs to last longer too.

Not forgetting that some domains have CHA dependent benefits, such as War, Strength, Trickery, etc. and yours only drops by 2 points by the end of the build doing this. Hmm, a smiting Barbarian eh? Different. A real quick and easy way to improve things a bit would be to slot in some Paladin levels for COT levels to give you a nice saves boost at not much cost and pick up divine might/shield to make use of your CHA. This will obviously need a bunch of alignment changes though which could be annoying/impossible. The paladin levels count towards smites so you don't lose out in the smite department. Also consider starting with a lower CHA and higher STR since boosting to 18 costs a lot of ability points, and put a few towards STR during level up - wouldn't go over 26 CHA myself.
