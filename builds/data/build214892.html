Some questions and suggestions for you....

Why take so many Fighter levels if you want to be a Wizard/Arcane Archer? Perhaps you should drop Fighter to 6 or so and bump AA to 17?

Why have a Constitution that gives a hit point penalty (and then take Toughness and Epic Toughness I and II)? And why put points into Charisma?

Assuming you're going to start Con at 10 (and not 8 like you have it now) I'd push Toughness back a few levels, since at level 1, it only gives you 1 extra hit point. I'd move it to 6 or 9 where it will start making more of a difference.

Take Weapon Spec before Improved Crit. The extra damage will affect every hit.

As an archer, I would probably take Rapid Shot earlier in my career. Surely before Imp. Crit.

Instead of Epic Toughness I'd opt for Epic Prowess and Epic Weapon Focus.

You should take at least one Fighter level in Epic so you can take Epic Weapon Spec: Longbow.

You may want to put a point in Open Lock and Disable Traps to permit some use with these skills.

Kaliban

Well there's a lot to be said for wiz 21, fighter 6, AA 13.

For one, you'll be a lot better able to offset your low hitpoints with epic warding and epic mage armor, you'll still have the chance to take weapon spec/epic weapon spec, and with the epic penetration feat you'll be able to land spells all the time on anything but a monk after hitting them with just greater spell breach (so you don't have to use the 9th level disjunction spell to lower SR if you don't want to).

In a nutshell that would be my recommendation for a level split given what you seem to want to accomplish with this character.
