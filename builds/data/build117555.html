I have seen similar builds for the below character like Tal Rasha posted, but they all seemed to lack epic spells (I did not look through all the threads, sorry if this was already posted).


Name: Grim Reaper
Race: Human
Alignment: Chaotic Evil

Final Build :

4 lvl bard/10 lvl RDD/26 lvl pale master.
Final Stats

The character hat natural immuninties to the following
Fire, paralyzation, sleep, sleep, stun, hold, critical hits

Major Feats:

Power attack
Cleave
Greater Cleave
Overwhelming Critical
Devasting Critical
Two Weapon Fighting
Ambidexterity
All Epic Spells (Hellball and Dragon Knight are awesome)
Weapon Focus bastard sword
Epic Weapon Focus

Note:
Epic Spells are not effected by armor casting penalties

The greater summon undead at level 26 PM will produce a level 19 Alhoon wizard.

No gear AC = 40
No gear attack bonus = 31/26/21

With good gear found in the HotU module and casting epic mage armor spell, you can get this character to an AC of high 70’s – low 80’s. Ditching the offhand bastard sword and replacing it with a good shield you can get into the high 80’s to low 90’s.

Building character

Starting Stats:
str 14
Dex 14
Con 13
int 16
wis 8
cha 11

level progression

3 levels BRD
2 levels PM
10 levels RDD
24 levels PM
1 level BRD

Increase stats

int at levels 4 and 8
dex at level 12
str at levels 16, 20, 24
con at level 28
str remainder of the levels

Keep maxing out concentration, spellcraft to 34 ranks. At the last level of bard, max out discipline and tumble. I like to have a high use magical device for this enables using class restricted items. The remainder points are up to you.

Alternate:

Instead dual bastard swords, the above character with a +10 scythe w/ 2d6 acid and the character wearing a cloud giant belt will produce huge critical hits. I was getting critical hits in the 180’s – 190’s.
