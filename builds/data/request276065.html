What type of effect are you trying to protect yourself from? I don't know if the OP is still around, but here is a build that might fit nicely into the Sith Lord type of roll. Starting good and falling into evil ... you know, the typical cliche.

I tried to build it as a spellsword (heavy on spell), using Paladin and Blackguard to boost saves, as per your request. They aren't as good as the aforementioned Pal/COT/BG, but if you are prepared, your Sorc buffs will handle quite a bit things that are thrown at you even before you require a save.

If you could do with weaker saves, I would consider dropping the paladin for 4 levels of RDD or perhaps Bard for Tumble and Bard Song.

Anyway, I played a variant (Fighter-Cleric-Sorc) through the Lords of Darkness mod, and it performed pretty well. You might consider Longsword and shield for extra AC. It should work fine for PvM, but you will end up falling back on Spells for PvP (Timestop, IGMS, etc.). Of course, I don't PvP often, so take it with a grain of salt.
